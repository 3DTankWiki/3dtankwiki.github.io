防御

There are couple of ways you could go about defending in 隧道对抗. The most 普通 way is to lay地雷on the ramp leading up to the 旗子 as well as around it, and when you're done, hiding behind the building behind the 旗子 or the pipes alongside it with a combination of冰风暴and维京. The logic behind this is when an attacker comes to steal your 旗子, he or she will blow up a couple of 地雷, reducing their health. Then, just when they Repair, the冰风暴进入并将冰风暴坦克杀死。

Another way is to make a heavy 底盘, such as a轰天炮和泰坦or a极速炮with猛犸象sit on the 旗子 with a few 地雷 and one磁力炮. The heavy 底盘 will lessen the 几率 of anyone getting the 旗子 while the磁力炮确保猛犸象doesn't die in the process.

Attack

For offensive presence,滑膛炮is a solid choice. It hits like a truck and causes a huge amount of 伤害. However,轰天炮,雷暴炮,激光炮,火焰炮,冰风暴and磁力炮aren't bad choices either. As for离子炮和火龙珠, I'd suggest people using them to stay on the cliff face right in front of the enemy 旗子 and provide cover fire for the attackers.

There are generally two ways to attack, and both of them are fairly simple:

  • Going through the top:

If you plan on going through the top, only do so if the top is in your 团队's 职务. This is because once you go up top, there is really no way to escape than to loose a lot of health and jumping back into your own base, or getting killed when trying to jump in the enemy base.

  • Going through the bottom:

If you love to be sneaky and have light-medium 底盘, this area is specifically tailored for you. Make sure the enemy 团队 is distracted by heavy 底盘射击 from the top while you go, get the 旗子 and go right back where you came from.

死亡混战/团队对战

在隧道对抗死亡混战中获胜的一个好策略是站在一个能够看到大部分地图的地方并持续开火，但这并不是说你应该上去尝试成为一个硬核玩家，绝对不行。你应该始终靠近或在围绕任一基地的墙上，或者在第一个悬崖上，这在两个基地中都有。这些地点有最多的掩护，同时流量也最多。此外，你所使用的底盘在这里会尤为重要，因为中型底盘可能会被证明在这里太“过于轻便”。我建议使用。猛犸象or泰坦with a 高伤害 dealing weapon, along the likes of火焰炮,冰风暴,离子炮,火龙珠,火箭炮,马格南,轰天炮甚至滑膛炮.

In 团队对战 however, the main strategy is just to go up top with a huge combination along with some 磁力炮, spam some 道具 and keep 射击 until the game's over.
