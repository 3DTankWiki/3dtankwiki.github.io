How to join a 军团

see also:军团和Rules for 军团


加入军团之前你应该知道的一些事情：

  • There are no 等级 limitations when it comes to joining a 军团, but make sure you have a decent 车库 and plenty of 经验值 before applying. 军团 are looking only for the best.
  • 军团被分为不同的等级，因此你可以找到并加入一个适合你等级的成员的军团。
  • You need to meet the 军团's requirements in order to be accepted in it.
  • 你还应该阅读军团 Rules, they will 帮助 you a lot.

Finding a 军团

This is not as difficult as some people think it is. All you need to do is choose whether you are suited to Tier 1 or 2 军团 and then scroll through the list of all the 军团 and find one that suits you. If you are fairly inexperienced, then Tier 2 is for you, but if you have played 3D坦克 for a long time then try joining a Tier 1 军团. Check out their members, see how they play, how they can 帮助 your game and improve your 能力, how they can affect your game. Remember, no one likes '军团霍珀' so don't join one 军团 after another just to have the best of all worlds. Choose one, and devote yourself to it! Make sure you meet the requirements, and also that the 军团 is taking applications.

For more info, check the following topic on our论坛:加入一个军团!

申请加入

  • Look for the form or the 'method' of applying to join the 军团 (it's usually posted in the 军团 topic's main post).
  • When applying, do not forget to give as much information about yourself as you can. There are several ways to apply, but the best way to find out how to apply for a 军团 is by reading their page, the information you need will probably be there. All you need to do is 关注 the steps laid out for you by the 军团. The easiest method of application is by sending a PM to the 队长/Co-队长. Make sure you write something more than just 'Can I join your 军团?'. Provide information about yourself and a little about your 车库 and your 等级, how long you've been playing and your time 动感区域.
  • 一旦你准备好所有信息，发送它并等待回复！

Getting accepted into a 军团

Once you get accepted into a 军团, the 队长 should guide you through how to communicate with the rest of the 军团. Do not be shy, do not feel left out! Just be yourself! You're a 军团 member now and you will be able to talk as much as you want to the 其他军团 members, make new 好友, meet new people, find new 'role-models' to look up to.

如果你没有那么幸运，未能收到军团队长的回复，或者你的申请被拒绝，不要失望！当然，如果你没有收到回复，可以再问一次军团队长，不过这次一定要问他为什么没有回复。如果你被拒绝了，也不要失去希望。还有很多军团可以选择，也许这样反而更好！
