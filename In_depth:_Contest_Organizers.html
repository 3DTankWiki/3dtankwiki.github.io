In depth: Contest Organizers

Note
This position no longer exists.

What is a Contest Organizer?

«

Contest Organizers are players with exceptional skill in organization and ideas to entertain players with new contests every couple of weeks.

»
— skeletorXVIII, Former Contests Administrator



«

A contest organiser is a player who is part of a team responsible for creating and running community contests.

»
— Ninja, Former Contests Organizer




«

A contest organizer is somebody that will develop his skills to create new contests, new challenges for players and always do his best to make it an enjoyable place for him.

»
— Sabry.Superman, Former Contests Organizer




«

A contest organizer is someone who wants to make a lot of fun to the players by creating contests, also organizing Chat Contests which includes questions about the game which I believe that every player would like to take part for that and win some rewards for answering one of these questions. A great place to start your helper career!

»
— Apost, Former Contests Organizer



What does a Contest Organizer do?

«

Contest Organizers take care of the contest section and anything related to official contests. They create, prepare and organize (local) Community contests and help organize international contests like master of parkour

»
— skeletorXVIII, Former Contests Administrators



«

Contest Organizers take care of official contests, from the making of them to judging, picking the winners, helping out players with answers to their questions - basically doing everything connected to contests. They usually do not have anything to do with developer made contests though, or only help to judge them.

»
— Nikk, Former Contests Organizer



«

Contest Organisers create and run contests which usually happen on a roughly monthly basis within the community contest section on the forum.

»
— Ninja, Former Contests Organizer



«

Contest Organizers are responsible for dealing with entries of his own contests, as well as helping players with any kind of questions they could have regarding contests to avoid any misunderstanding. It is also their responsibility to make sure the place dedicated for contests is a peaceful place that everybody enjoys.

»
— Sabry.Superman, Former Contests Organizer




«

Contest Organizers are the head of making contests, organizing Chat Contests to the players on the community, and of course, to help players by answering their questions to make sure everything is clear to them, the main thing that at the end of the day, players will have fun in participating on contests and enjoy doing that.

»
— Apost, Former Contests Organizer




What is the most fun part of your job?

«

Being able to see players enjoying the contests.

»
— skeletorXVIII, Former Contests Administrator



«

The most fun part is in my opinion judging the entries. You get to see all the creativity, originality and professionality of players, and then give your opinion about it.

»
— Nikk, Former Contests Organizer



«

Looking through the entries especially when the contest is based around creativity, its always cool clicking links in a spreadsheet and being impressed with what you see. Although there is a fair share of unimpressive entries too. ;p

»
— Ninja, Former Contests Organizer



«

The great thing as an organizer is to see the entries, seeing the differences, seeing the creativity. It happened that I used to expect some entries with some ideas of what could it be, and what's nice with this is that some players will really do their best, put a lot of efforts to make a shiny entry, and at the end it is something you didn't expect. This is really something I enjoy as a contest organizer. ;p

»
— Sabry.Superman, Former Contests Organizer



«

The most fun part on the job is to see the creativity of each participant on a specific contest, and to judge every entry of a contest. I also like to organize the Chat Contests with my teammates, because I see that it's very fun to the players when we have a Chat Contest.

»
— Apost, Former Contests Organizer




What would be your advice for those looking to become a Contest Organizer?

«

We look for players which are organized, have ideas, and are motivated to create new contests on their own. Experience in Parkour is a bonus we would appreciate.

»
— skeletorXVIII, Former Contests Administrator



«

Applicants should be prepared to answer players on questions regarding contests and work closely with their team on establishing new ideas for contests then bringing those ideas to life. An ability to remain unbiased and treat all contestants fairly is crucial.

»
— Ninja, Former Contests Organizer



«

As with every helper position, my advice is: Do it due to the passion and the will to help out the community, don't do it for the rewards. If you are creative and love contests, this is definitely a position you should consider.

»
— Nikk, Former Contests Organizer



«

Before applying for a position, it is important to see what's the main task,and not to apply for rewards. If you really believe you can do a great work and that you are very organized, just go ahead, that's something you won't regret, something you expected when you applied for this position, like if a contract was made and fulfilled. Besides judging entries, being fair towards every players is something crucial, it can happen you're closer to a player than another, but this factor should not have any influence on your decision when taking a decision.

»
— Sabry.Superman, Former Contests Organizer




«

My advice for those who are looking to become a Contest Organizer is to be creative, think outside the box, think about ideas for contests that you think it will be fun to the players. If you think you are able to do so, and give your very best to the players by creating contests, go ahead! This is a very good place to start from.

»
— Apost, Former Contests Organizer
