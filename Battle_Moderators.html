Battle Moderator Administrators

Battle Administrators control the activities of the group and organize their work. They handle complaints about errors in the work of the moderators on top of monitoring the order of the game.

In the list of the Forum Members their names are highlighted in pale red.

Battle Moderators

Battle Moderators are specially trained professionals who fight against battle violations — sabotage and hacks. If you have evidence of third-party software usage or team betrayal, you can create a thread in the "Game Breakers" section of the forum to make a report.

In the list of the Forum Members their names are highlighted in dark lavender.

Battle Moderator Candidates

Battle Moderator Candidates are tankers who decide to devote themselves to the tough and professional life as a Battle Moderator and must first undergo extensive training. Battle Moderator Candidates can be found in the Chat with (BC) beside their nickname.
