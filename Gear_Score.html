战斗力


战斗力 (战斗力)— is a number that indicates the overall strength of the 坦克.

Current personal 战斗力 is displayed in the车库. In 战斗, it can be checked and compared to 其他玩家 by pressing theTab钥匙.

Color 射程

To improve readability, the 战斗力 number in the 车库 and in 战斗 is displayed with specific colors. The color used depends on the 射程 the 战斗力 number is in:

积分分配

For upgradable equipment, 战斗力 is directly proportional to the 升级等级 (modification +升级).

The 最高 possible 战斗力 amount is9999. 在特殊情况下，它可能会达到10000. A 坦克 with this number must not only fulfill all of the criteria listed below, but also have危机 XTor亥伯龙神 XTwith max 升级装备, or 装备 unique 防御模块 Demon or Lord.


Displaying 最高
战斗力 in the 车库

实现最高战斗力的条件:

  • 炮塔,手榴弹, and底盘: Mk7 with all possible 升级 (MAX)
  • 防御模块: all 4 slots are taken with fully upgraded 防御模块 or one of the Spectrum 防御模块 variants.
  • 无人机: 装备 and fully upgraded (except神谕者)

According to those rules, 战斗力 changes in real time (for example: after upgrading equipment or switching in 战斗).

Interesting facts

  • 战斗力 was added to the game on August 21st, 2018.
  • In the update released on August 24th, 2018, 迷彩’ influence on 战斗力 was reduced;, also, 皮肤 were now counted.
  • 与updatereleased on October 11th, 2019, 道具 and cosmetic 升级 of the 坦克 no longer contributed to 战斗力.
  • 随着2022年9月首款XT无人机的发布，标准玩家首次获得了超过9999战斗力的能力。在那之前，只有开发者或技术账号上的组织者才能展示那种“坦克酷炫”的值。
  • 与updatereleased on January 13th, 2023, 装备改造 rarity categories started having influence on 战斗力. Up until the update, all 装备改造 gave 300 points of 战斗力.
  • 自2024年9月以来，手榴弹now also influence 战斗力. At the same time, 无人机’ influence on 战斗力 was significantly reduced.
  • If the 战斗力 value is 低, a 玩家 will have trouble not falling behind 其他玩家 in terms of equipment. In such a case, a warning message will appear when waiting for a 战斗.
