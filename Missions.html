任务


任务- 特殊任务 in which the 玩家 receives 奖励 upon completion. You can track all 任务, their statuses, and claim 奖励 in the «任务»section in the大厅. Additionally, the 战斗 end screen will show you a 任务's progress if you contributed toward its progress in 战斗.

任务 are grouped by different thematic categories (for example, tutorial, 每日, 每周, etc.). In most categories there is asupermission that gives extra 奖励 for completing all 任务.


Attention!Sometimes 任务 progress doesn't count. This can happen due to the following 原因:
  • Not all conditions are met. For example, some 任务 can only be completed in certain 战斗模式. Read the 任务 description carefully!
  • 活动任务 are only started when the 玩家 opens the "任务" screen.
  • 任务 are only counted in匹配战斗. It is impossible to complete 任务 in玩家 created 战斗.

任务 Categories

When entering the «任务»section, the 玩家 sees 任务 categories, which are needed to sort 任务 by sections and for comfortable tracking of their completion progress. Aside from two sections for beginners («Tutorial 任务» and «Entry Bonus»), which are hidden following completion, as well as 任务 that show up during 活动, there are five permanent sections:

  • Tutorial 任务:任务 that introduce main game mechanics of the game and how it works
  • Entry Bonus:bonus 任务 available to the 玩家 only once after registering in the game
  • 活动任务:任务 that are only available during in-game 活动. The name of the section may be changed according to the 活动. There is a timer at the bottom of those 任务 sections that shows for how long those 任务 will be available.
  • 每日任务:set of 任务 which is updated every day
  • 每周任务:set of 任务 which is updated once a week
  • 特殊任务:特殊任务 with specific 奖励
  • Contracts:任务 that give 奖励 for a certain number of经验值points earned. They can be purchased from the商店for specific 活动.
  • Trophies:incredibly difficult 任务 that 奖励 with a uniquetrophy装备改造 for a 炮塔 or a 底盘


Attention!The progress of the 任务 which require you to earn 水晶 and/or 经验值, is affected by the 玩家'svip 账号.


Tutorial 任务

see also:道具

Tutorials in «3D坦克» are made in a way that lets the 坦克手 learn everything by themselves, with the 帮助 of many individual tips that can be opened on game screens by clicking onand by completing the tutorial 任务. Tutorial 任务 aren’t displayed all at once; instead they are divided into 9 consecutive parts. Only after completing all 任务 of one set, the next set of 任务 will be unlocked.

For completing a tutorial 任务 (usually those are simple actions done by average 玩家每日: equipping a无人机orupgradinga 炮塔), 玩家 receive 1普通钥匙. From part 4, these 任务 also award经验值. Also, there is asupermission in each part. Completing it will give extra 奖励.

This section is only visible if its 任务 have not been completed or 奖励 have not been collected. After completion, this section disappears.

每日任务

每日任务 are available for the 玩家 every day. They are divided into two different types of 每日任务, which appear one after another:

  • Standard 任务 (8 main 任务 and 1 super-任务) - available from the 等级 of 中士
  • ENCORE-任务 (7 main 任务 and 1 super-任务) - available from the 等级 of 军士长


At first, only the Standard 任务 are available. After their completion, a supermission will be automatically completed. Most prizes that can be received 每日 will be received for this part of 任务.

Completing the standardsupermission unlocks access to extra ENCORE-任务.

ENCORE-任务 are bonus 任务 for those who are ready to play even more for a small extra 奖励. After completing all main 任务, thesupermission will be automatically completed.

奖励 for completing 每日任务 don’t depend on等级and always consist of 15 stars, 4普通钥匙and 800 经验值.


All 每日任务 are reset every day during the服务器 restartat 2 AM UTC+0.

每周任务

每周任务 are reset each Friday, so they can be completed every week. There are three types of 每周任务, which appear one after another:

  • Standard 任务 (9 main 任务 and 1 supermission) - available from the 等级 of 三级军士长
  • ENCORE 任务 (5 main 任务 and 1 supermission) - available from the 等级 of 军士长
  • SECRET 任务 (9 main 任务 and 1 supermission) - available from the 等级 of 军士长

At first, only the Standard 任务 are available. After their completion, asupermission will automatically be completed.

Completing the Standard Super-任务 unlocks access to extra ENCORE-任务.

ENCORE 任务 are bonus 任务 for those who are ready to play even more for a small extra 奖励. After completing all main 任务 thesupermission will be automatically completed.

Completed ENCORE super-任务 unlocks access to extra SECRET-任务 that are available only for 每周任务.

SECRET-任务 are another extra set of 任务 which require an activevip 账号to be completed. After completing all main 任务 thesupermission will be automatically completed.

奖励 for completing 每周任务 do not depend on等级and consist of 30 红宝石, 16普通钥匙, 5珍奇钥匙,1史诗钥匙and 900 经验值.


All 每周任务 are reset every Friday during服务器 restartat 2 AM UTC+0.

Contracts

Contracts are only available for certain 活动 or mini-games. They can be purchased for红宝石or水晶in the商店or with a discount on the展示. To complete contracts, it is required to earn a certain amount of reputation pointsin匹配战斗. Contracts usually contain the活动 currencyas a 奖励.

A 玩家 can have multiple contracts at the same time, but only one can be active at a time. “Active” means that earned reputation points are counted towards this contract's progress. A contract can be activated by pressing the “Activate” button of the corresponding contract in the “任务” section.

As soon as the contract is complete, its 奖励 should be collected immediately; otherwise, a new contract won’t be activated. After the end of the 活动, the 玩家 will receive an alternative 奖励 for a completed contract with an uncollected 奖励 depending on the contract’s price.

Instead of completing the contract by earning reputation, its completion can be purchased. This “early completion” costs a certain amount of红宝石, where the exact price depends on the progress made.

Interesting Facts

  • 每日任务 appeared in the game on July 14, 2015.
  • As of March 29, 2017, it became impossible to complete 任务 while playing in专家战场.
  • On January 27, 2018, the «Destroy 9999 opponents» and «Earn 99999 经验值 in 战斗» 任务 with XT 皮肤 as prizes have been disabled.
  • On October 30, 2018, the quest chain system was removed from the game, with 每周任务 taking its place.
  • On February 28, 2020, 任务 were renamed 任务. A 特殊任务 section has been added.
  • April 14, 2023 Trophy 任务 with especially valuable prizes were added, becoming the equivalent of very challenging achievements.
  • Until August 2023, the 任务 categories «每日任务» and «每周任务» always gave 3 random 任务. Their progress was carried over to the next day or week if you failed to complete them on time.
