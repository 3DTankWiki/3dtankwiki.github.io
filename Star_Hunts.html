Star Hunts

see also:星级挑战



Attention!Star Hunts are no longer held in the game and have been replaced by星级挑战- a similar concept but with dedicated prizes per tier.


Star Huntsare 活动 in which 玩家 can accumulate Stars in 战斗 and receive prizes based on the amount of Stars accumulated. These 活动 aren't run on a scheduled basis and the dates and prizes of the 活动 are up to the developers. The 活动 usually lasts for the 持续时间 of a week. Star Hunts are announced on the论坛and in the 每周视频周报. They often occur simultaneously with 其他活动 and celebrations such as Halloween, 坦克手们 Day, etc.

The prizes are awarded in a "tiers" system, similar to that of星级挑战. The 奖励 ascend based on the number of Stars accumulated. The prizes of each Star Hunt can vary. Most of the time they feature 幸运礼盒 for the earlier tiers and then an exclusive item at one of the final tiers, for example ananimated 迷彩or similar item. After completing the required number of Stars for the tier, the 奖励 are delivered instantly to your 账号 with an in-game notification.

Prizes in the 活动 are summed up, meaning when you reach a specific 奖励 tier you will get that many 奖励. So for example, if there is a Star Hunt 活动 with a 1 幸运礼盒奖励 for the first tier, and 2 幸运礼盒奖励 for the second tier, upon reaching the second tier you will get 2 幸运礼盒 and not just 1 more.

Note that your progress in the 活动 is not displayed in the game under the 任务 panel as with星级挑战. You'll have to compare the Stars shown in the game with the tiers displayed in the 公告 topic to determine your progress/accumulated awards.


How to earn Stars

Stars are earned by finishing匹配战斗. When the 战斗 finishes, the 玩家 who were in it will receive a number of Stars based on

  • their 职务 on the final scoreboard, and;
  • whether their 团队 won or lost (this does not apply to solo 战斗模式 such as JGR and 死亡混战).

For the exact amount of Stars given, please read below:

  • 1st and 2nd places on the winning 团队, 1st place on the losing 团队 or 1st and 2nd places in solo 模式(死亡混战 and JGR) —3 Stars;
  • 3rd and 4th places on the winning 团队, 2nd and 3rd places on the losing 团队 or 3rd and 4th places in solo 模式(死亡混战 and JGR) —2 Stars;
  • All 其他 places—1 Star, if you were active during the 战斗.

You can increase the number of Stars earned by getting avip 账号. With it, all the Stars you earn will be doubled.
