跑酷生存

see also:跑酷andContests



跑酷 Survivalis a usually annual跑酷contest in which 团队 of up to 5 玩家 have to work through a series of 跑酷星级挑战. 玩家 have to complete these 星级挑战 one after another within a given a timeframe (星级挑战 are spaced out around a week apart.) 团队's which don't or can't complete the 每周星级挑战 will be eliminated from the next 星级挑战(s). As the contest progresses towards the last 星级挑战 many 团队 will be eliminated from it. The 团队 which successfully make it to, and complete, the last 星级挑战 win the contest.

跑酷生存的奖品通常包括每完成一个星级挑战的水晶和道具，以及最终幸存者的独特迷彩。

Below is a list of the winners, prizes, and 星级挑战 for previous editions of 跑酷 Survival:
