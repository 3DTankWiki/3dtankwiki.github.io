Guide to playing in 小岛 effectively

see also:Guides for 地图


About 小岛


Overview:小岛 is the smallest 地图 in the game based on dimensions. It is very 普通 throughout the 等级, especially in 黄蜂轻甲/Fire or 经验值/BP 战斗. There are usually a lot of 战斗 on active 服务器 and 战斗 in all game 模式.

战斗模式:死亡混战, 团队对战, 互夺军旗, 控制点

大小:6 玩家, 3 vs 3

Tactics

死亡混战

AVOID THE MIDDLE!!!

Try to get to the palm trees on the sides(as shown in the image beside)and hide behind them. It may seem useless but 1 shot from an enemy that hits the trees is an extra shot you have to hit your 红队. Always target the weakest 玩家 as every kill is equal.

You will need a heavy 底盘, as first of all, you need to survive and second of all you can be prone to be pushed off.

The best 炮塔 would be轰天炮,离子炮,雷暴炮or火龙珠as they all have decent reload, 射程 and most importantly伤害.


团队对战

Winning a 团队对战 requires coordination between all the teammates. You should know exactly what everyone's plan is and how you can support them to 帮助 them destroy the opponents as well as staying alive.

Use the houses to your advantage, as they are the only form of 防护 on 小岛. Try and take cover behind them after you run out of ammo or in-between shots. Use a medium/heavy 底盘-mounted with a 高伤害炮塔 such as滑膛炮,火焰炮, and轰天炮with an磁力炮backing up the attack.

You can also use the houses to bounce火龙珠球 off and hit the opponents while staying in a 职务 where it is virtually impossible for anyone to hit you from their base.

互夺军旗

The 钥匙 to winning an 小岛互夺军旗 is teamwork and co-ordination, like every 其他地图. You should always have 1 person ready to take the 旗子 at a moment's notice.

The best line up would be:

  • 维京with磁力炮driving around 治疗 the 其他 teammates.
  • 蜂王with冰风暴,火焰炮or滑膛炮.(capturing 旗子)
  • 蜂王,猎人中甲or维京with滑膛炮,火龙珠,轰天炮,火焰炮or离子炮.(capturing 旗子)

The 磁力炮 should try and take cover behind the houses, so he has 最低伤害 done to him. The 其他 2 should try to stay on opposite sides so they can attack the 红队 from 2 different sides, making it hard to deal with the 2 of you.

控制点

装备 a heavy 底盘. The easy way to get the points ticking up is to get 底盘 with heaps of 护甲 partnered with冰风暴,火焰炮(can 伤害 multiple 红队 at once),磁力炮heals teammates),滑膛炮or离子炮. You just need to clear out the point. Once you have 控制 over the point, 禁止 off the ways to get to the point so even if you are destroyed the 红队 have to wait a few more seconds to get to the point, giving them less time to turn over.
