Mechanics

电磁脉冲 (电磁脉冲)is a 状态效果 that disables and 禁止 the use of 道具 for a few seconds, and also forces 维修工具 and 地雷 to be reloaded if their remaining recharge time is less than the 持续时间 of the applied 电磁脉冲.

When applying a 状态效果 to a 坦克:

  • All active 道具 are deactivated:
  • Additional 道具 effects from 无人机守卫者,助推器,魔术师and危机are also deactivated.
  • All 道具 go on a 冷却 that lasts exactly the same number of seconds as the 电磁脉冲状态效果 lasts:
    • The 持续时间 of the 状态效果 is different for each method of applying it.


Features of the "电磁脉冲" 状态效果:

  • If the 道具 was previously on a longer 冷却, it can be activated immediately after the 电磁脉冲 effect.
  • Placed 地雷 do not disappear with the imposition of a 状态效果.


An 电磁脉冲坦克 can be identified by a 特殊 icon. If an enemy is affected by a 状态效果, a text tooltip"道具 disabled"will appear above it.

How to apply the 状态效果

黄蜂轻甲过速装置

The 黄蜂轻甲炸弹 applies an 电磁脉冲状态效果 to every enemy within a certain 半径, as well as an instant眩晕, 干扰器 effect, andsets fire to surviving 红队.

猎人中甲过速装置

Applies the "电磁脉冲" 状态效果 to all 坦克 within a certain 半径 for a few seconds and additionally眩晕 them.

装备改造

There are several装备改造 for 炮塔in the game that can inflict the 电磁脉冲状态效果.

状态效果防护

剑圣

The 剑圣 is immune to all negative 状态效果.

猛犸象过速装置

When 猛犸象's 过速装置 is activated, all negative 状态效果 are removed, and the 坦克 becomes temporarily immune to them.

圣骑士过速装置

When 圣骑士's 过速装置 is activated, all negative 状态效果 are removed, and the 坦克 becomes temporarily immune to them.

Polarization

Polarization effect is applied when using维修工具and when activating过速装置圣骑士 or 独裁者.

抵抗

Immediately after respawning, all 坦克 with the Invulnerable 状态效果 are immune to negative 状态效果.

装备改造

There are底盘装备改造in the game that protects the 坦克 from 电磁脉冲.

If an enemy with an 抗电磁脉冲装备改造 has the 电磁脉冲状态效果 applied by an enemy, a text tooltip"Immunity"will briefly appear above them.
