Best artworks of the monthis a simple 'best artwork' contest. To participate in the contest, 玩家 have to create an artwork, any kind of art, out of 4 provided screenshots. The hashtag#Artworks_Contestand your 昵称 has to be added somewhere within the picture. The image then has to uploaded toImgur(an image hosting site) and posted as a reply to theofficial 论坛 topic. On the last day of the month, the contest will close for the time being as the entries within the 论坛 topic will be judged by members of the Creative League. The following day, the contest will be opened again, the new screenshots will be posted, and the winners for the previous month will be published in theContestssection of the 论坛.


For more information, please visit theofficial 论坛 topic.

How to participate

  1. Pick a screenshot from among the 4 provided screenshots in the 论坛 topic.
  2. Create a piece of art from it. You can use Photoshop or any 其他 equivalent picture-editing software. Don't forget the #Artworks_Contest hashtag and your in-game 昵称 that has to be written somewhere within the art.
  3. Upload your design to the image hosting siteImgur.
  4. After you upload the artwork to Imgur then you have to do these basic steps: right-click on the artwork (in Imgur) and choose "Copy Image" from the 选项 menu, then go to the论坛 topicfor the contest and press on "Reply to this topic". Now you only have to right-click inside the reply 箱子 and choose "Paste" from the 选项 menu. Then press "post" to reply to the topic and submit your entry. You don't have to type your 昵称 inside your entry 箱子. As long as everyone can see who posted the reply.

Prizes

  • 1st Place:85 000 水晶 + 5 days of VIP 账号;
  • 2nd Place:65 000 水晶 + 3 days of VIP 账号;
  • 3rd Place:50 000 水晶 + 2 days of VIP 账号;
  • 其他 worthy entries that didn't get a spot within the top 3 will be rewarded with 40 000 水晶 + 1 day of VIP 账号.
