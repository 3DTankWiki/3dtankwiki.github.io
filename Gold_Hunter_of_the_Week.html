With the introduction ofRatings and 统计数据for 玩家, the 每周公告 of Gold 猎人中甲 of the Week became unnecessary and was discontinued after 视频周报 66.

Though 每周 winners are no longer announced or recorded, you can check the current week's top 100 Gold 猎人中甲 on theRatings Homepage. The leaderboard resets every Monday at 2:00 UTC.
