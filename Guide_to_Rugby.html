Guide to 橄榄球

Introduction

橄榄球模式 is a very fun and creative 模式, the 模式 is based on the popular game but with 坦克! There are a few spawns around the 地图, the 球 drops in one of them randomly. The 球 can be thrown from one 坦克 to another by pressing the drop button (usually set to F). Each 团队 has a base, your 任务 is to catch the 球 and bring it to the enemy base and 分数 a goal. The 团队 that 分数 the most goals wins!

Scoring

Goal scoring

Since scoring goals is pretty much everything in this 模式, goal scoring is the main source of points, for each goal scored you receive 75 personal points. But you won't 分数 goals with ease, every 坦克手 on the 地图 will be focused on 1 single thing - the 球! So here are a few tips to 帮助 you out. First thing first, always be patient. Don't rush whenever you see the 球 on the ground, look around you, notice where the enemy 团队 is, and when you feel like the right moment is here - go for it! The second thing I'd like to talk about is the possibility to throw the 球. I feel like most 玩家 do not take full advantage of this option. If you have been damaged badly and you know death is coming, try throwing the 球 to a teammate nearby instead of letting the enemy 团队 taking it.

Destroying 坦克

The easiest way to get kills is to defend your base, as everyone will come for you. For each kill, 15 personal points will be given to the 坦克手 who finished the kill and 5 personal points will be given to everyone else who assisted.

治疗

Pretty much like in every 其他团队 game 模式, you can use 磁力炮 to heal your 蓝队. 磁力炮 can be very useful in both offence and defense! The amount of points you gain is proportional to the actual amount of 伤害 healed - you won't benefit by 治疗 the weakest 底盘 first, heal the 坦克 that need to health immediately.

Offence

As mentioned before, scoring goals isn't easy at all. After playing the 模式 for quite sometime, I came to a conclusion that the best 底盘 for goal scoring are 黄蜂轻甲, 蜂王, 猎人中甲 & 维京. 黄蜂轻甲 and 蜂王 are fast and you can sneak with them behind the enemy base and 分数 goals. On the 其他 hand, 猎人中甲 and 维京 have enough health to resist to the enemy defense in a face to face fight. It all comes down to preference. As for 炮塔, close ranged weapons like 冰风暴 and 火焰炮 are very useful for small 地图 while weapons like 离子炮 and 火龙珠 are useful for bigger 地图.

Defense

Defending your base wouldn't be easy either, so make sure you 装备 the right combo! For 底盘, 泰坦 or 猛犸象 would do the job, these two have enough health and their unique 过速装置 will 帮助 a lot. When defending, do everything you can in order to stop the enemy 团队 from scoring a goal. 猛犸象 and 泰坦 are the heaviest 底盘 in the game, you'll be able to push every attacker with no problem. As for 炮塔, it depends on the enemy 团队防护.

Midfield

Midfield is when the 球 is around to drop and everyone is waiting for it. What I like to do is let my teammates 黑色营地 and wait for the 球 to drop while I do my best to 伤害 the enemy 团队坦克手们. By doing so, I'll distract them from the 球 and in the same time make it easier for my 蓝队 to destroy them. And for those of you who wait for the 球, make sure you have enough health, otherwise just let another 玩家 from your 团队 have the 球. Because there's no point it grabbing the 球 and dying two seconds later, right?
