车库 description

The simplest and most economical 无人机.

Significantly cheaper than the 其他无人机. Does not consume extra 道具 when it is activated. Upgrading this 无人机 boosts the effect of «伤害提升» and «护甲提升» 道具.

This 无人机 has no advantages 其他 than the most important one — being economic!
