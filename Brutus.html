车库 description

The simplest and most economical 无人机.

Significantly cheaper than the 其他无人机. Does not consume extra 道具 when it is activated. Upgrading this 无人机 boosts the effect of «伤害提升» and «护甲提升» 道具.

这款无人机除了最重要的优势——经济性之外，毫无优点！

Basic effects

  • «护甲提升» 道具's effect strength»: (50 →100), %
  • «伤害提升» 道具's effect increase»: (50 →100), %

效果

布鲁特斯 is the simplest 无人机 in the game; it does not have any 特殊 abilities and therefore only makes «伤害提升» and «护甲提升» 道具 effect stronger.

Pros and Cons
