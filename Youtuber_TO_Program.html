Youtuber TO Program

see also:Youtuber TO (迷彩)andContent 创建者


YouTuber TOis a program that 奖励 3D坦克 YouTubers for creating videos and accumulating views. Participation in the program 允许 you to get paid for your work in the form of in-game currency, 珍奇 items for reviews, a unique 迷彩, and evenSpectator 模式. The program started on the 2nd of April, 2018, and is still running.

Application for participation in YouTuber TO program

To participate in the program, you need to:

  • Authorize on the特殊 site«YouTuber TO» (a big “Authorization” button in the top right corner) using your 账号’s login and 密码
  • Press the «Participate» button
  • 关注 the instructions on the screen to link your channel


While your application is being reviewed there will be a “Program candidate” title in your简介

After your application is accepted by site moderation and you are added to the YouTuber TO program, you will receive points depending on the number of views you get per month. Points are awarded from the third to the sixth day of every month.

What is required to be on your channel

For a fast and successful review of your application, your channel has to satisfy the conditions:

  • Have at least 3 500 views for the last reporting month (not including Shorts videos)
  • Be composed of «3D坦克» content making up at least 70% of all your videos for the last reporting month
  • Contains no unreasonable criticism of the game, information, or videos about current bugs, insults of the administration in your content, nor does it fuel any in-game conflicts



What is prohibited on your channel

The 主题 and 持续时间 of your videos are up to you. However, your videos mustn't contain any of the following:

  • Violation of the current international laws or in-gamerules
  • Direct insults to «3D坦克» developers
  • Unreasonable criticism of the game, fueling in-game conflicts
  • Hosting of giveaways for items that are not available on the date when the giveaway drawing happens
  • Videos about current bugs
  • Advertising or any 其他 mention of sites with gambling (在线 casinos, roulettes, etc.)
  • Advertising or any 其他 mention of sites or YouTube channels that violate the rights of «3D坦克» as the copyright holder of the game
  • Disclosure of in-game awards, or sums and percents of awards for the 创建者标记 program or 其他 bonus programs

Extra 奖励

Participants in the «YouTuber TO» program unlock extra privileges for reaching a 高 view count in a reporting month:

迷彩 «Youtuber TO»
  • For reaching 20 000 views: ability to join a 聊天 with Administrators and apply for a technical 账号. The technical 账号 may be removed if the 20 000 views mark is not reached for 2 months.
  • For reaching 30 000 views: ability to apply forSpectator 模式. Spectator can be removed if the 30 000 views mark is not reached for 2 months. Applications are reviewed by the administration individually.
  • For reaching 40 000 views: ability to apply for the YouTuber TO 迷彩. The 迷彩 is removed if the 40 000 views mark is not reached for 2 months. Applications are reviewed by the administration individually.
  • In addition, for reaching 20 000 views or more, there is a possibility to monetize content with a 创建者标记. This procedure is much more difficult to realize, and each case is dealt with individually.
  • The most interesting videos of program participants often make it to the 特殊 rubric in the 视频日志.

Contacting the program technical support

If you have any questions regarding the realization of the «YouTuber TO» program you can ask them on thesite.

To do that:

1) Click on the «Technical Support» button at the bottom of the page.

2) On the following page, click on the «Create Request» button.

3) Type your question in the "Request Subject" field. Choose the «Vlogger TO» option from the "Project" list to state that your question relates to the YouTuber TO program. Describe your problem in 宇宙 provided in "Descriprion Problem". Once done, complete the captcha and click on the «Create» button to submit your query.
