Who are Administrators?

他们的功能与自己特定小组中的助手相同。然而，他们拥有更大的权限，并负责管理和监督该小组的助手。如果您想对该小组助手的工作提出投诉，您应通过私人消息将其发送给管理员。

In the list of 论坛 Members, their names are highlighted in浅红色, see below for a list of the current EN Administrators with the 论坛 colours of their respective Helper 团队.


What do Administrators do?

他们在自己的小组内执行与助手相同的任务，但他们拥有更大的权力，并且负责管理和监督自己特定小组的助手。

How do I contact an Administrator?

The best way to contact an administrator is through the 论坛, however, make sure your question/comment/complaint can be answered by a helper before contacting the administrator.
