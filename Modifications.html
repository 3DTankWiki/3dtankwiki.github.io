Modifications

see also:升级

Modification等级 (Mkfor short) are a way to optimize characteristics of炮塔,底盘and手榴弹. Don’t mistake modifications with升级(micro 升级): every 炮塔, 底盘 and 手榴弹 has 7 modifications and inside of each modification, every parameter is boosted with small steps - 升级.

Every modification 等级 is numbered from Mk1 to Mk7. If the Mk7 modification is fully upgraded, “MAX” will be written in the 车库. 玩家 often name the max modification Mk7+ or Mk8.

一般信息

The progress of every new 玩家 starts with a combination of a Mk1 炮塔 and 底盘 that they chose when assembling their 坦克. 其他 Mk1 炮塔 and 底盘 are only available in幸运礼盒. Next modification 等级 (Mk2-Mk7) are available when reaching a certain等级for红宝石. To get a new modification 等级 for 红宝石, the current modification 等级 must be fullyupgraded.

Equipment that can be upgraded is marked with anarrow in the车库. Which values ​​are improved how much and which remain constant can be read in the respective articles on the Wiki.

In theEarly accesssection of the商店, the next modifications of 炮塔 and 底盘 are also available for 红宝石. In this case, to purchase a modification it isn’t required to 升级 the previous one to 最高 and it’s available usually 2 等级 earlier than in 车库, but modifications in Early access are significantly more expensive.

剑圣底盘 and 终结者炮塔 take a 特殊 place in modification 等级. This unique equipment can’t be purchased by 玩家 and is a part of the “团队剑圣” and the occasional “Solo 剑圣”战斗模式where it is automatically 装备 on one 玩家 in each 团队. Their modification 等级 are equal to the modification 等级 of the 炮塔 and 底盘 that are used by the 玩家 who received the 剑圣坦克.

Purchase of new modification 等级

To 升级 equipment to the new modification 等级 it is required to complete the following conditions:

  • 玩家 has reached 等级 needed to unlock next modification 等级
  • current modification 等级 is fullyupgraded
  • 玩家 has enough 红宝石 to purchase the next modification



Buying Next Modification 等级


After that, the 玩家 can purchase an item of the next modification 等级 by pressing “Purchase” under the equipment parameters. New modification is immediately 装备; waiting time isn’t required.
