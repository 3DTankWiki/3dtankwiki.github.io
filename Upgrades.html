购买下一个 MK 等级

Every炮塔,底盘,防护防御模块and无人机can be upgraded. Equipment that can be upgraded is marked with anarrow in the车库.

To 升级 equipment, the 玩家 needs to select the item in the车库and press the “升级” button or press theENTERkey. 升级 are also required for unlocking newmodification等级 for 炮塔 and 底盘.


Attention! 红宝石 are required to purchase new modifications.

and upgraded parameters

升级 improve all upgradable parameters simultaneously. The Current 升级 progress and upgradable parameters are displayed above the 升级 button. After a successful 升级 of equipment that is used in 战斗, new parameters will be applied with the next respawn. If the Mk7 modification is fully upgraded, then “MAX” will appear. 玩家 often call the max modification Mk7+ or Mk8.

升级只影响物品当前的修改等级。当购买新的修改时，升级等级重置为零。


Attention!要购买新的炮塔或底盘改装，当前的改装必须完全升级。
