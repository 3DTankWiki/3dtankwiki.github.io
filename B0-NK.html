B0-NK 大锤

Close 射程 pneumatic 滑膛炮. Deals monstrous 伤害 but only works at extremely 低 distances.

B0-NK «大锤» is a great tool for industrial construction work. This massive chisel was designed for breaking concrete, brick walls, foundations, as well as ripping up asphalt and dismantling hard surfaces. The accelerating reactive element was embedded to increase the penetrating 启动加速度 of the chisel.

Be careful to only use this tool as intended. Non-standardized use may result in unforeseeable consequences and voids the possibility of a warranty service.

Interesting Facts

  • B0-NK was added into the game on the 16th of July, 2021.
  • Unlike 其他炮塔, B0-NK doesn’t change its 迷彩, instead it saves its bright-orange color that warns 红队 to stay away.
  • Currently B0-NK is only available in the«军备竞赛»战斗模式 and some festive 战斗模式.
  • B0-NK got its 特殊皮肤 called «Biomorphic Claw» for Halloween in 2021.
  • The 炮塔 cannot be fired if the 坦克 is lying upside down.
