About 山岗


Overview:山岗 is a very small 地图. It is quite 普通 and usually there are a few good 战斗 on active 服务器.
It is quite famous for its金箱子, as there are only 2 spots, it can be quite easy to take them with the right 底盘 and plan.
It can also be the home of some 高奖励战斗. As it is so small, there is no time needed to drive back to where the action is because you spawn in the middle of it.
Remember that no 维修工具 drop on 山岗, so an磁力炮is golden in 团队战斗.

战斗模式:死亡混战, 团队对战, 互夺军旗

大小:8 玩家, 4v4

Tactics

死亡混战

死亡混战 is quite different on 山岗, as you can’t 黑色营地 in one spot and steal everyone’s kills. The best way to finish top in a 死亡混战 on 山岗 would be to keep moving around. Moving targets are much harder to hit, especially at close 射程, so this will probably be your best bet at staying alive. The best 炮塔 for this tactic are火焰炮,冰风暴,滑膛炮,离子炮and火龙珠mounted on top of either a蜂王,猎人中甲or维京.

离子炮is also a great weapon to use in 死亡混战 as it has a great vertical 射程. It can in the middle of the top and hit almost anything on the 地图 and the constant fire makes it super hard for your 红队 to retaliate.
磁力炮with维京, 装备 with a mountain of 道具 and running around like it went crazy, dominates almost every 死亡混战, so if you have a massive stockpile of 道具 and are feeling bored, try this combination, otherwise you may have to settle for one of the more appropriate 选项.

团队对战

There isn’t much variation in the tactics you see in 山岗 between the different 战斗模式 as it is such a small 地图. The tactics for 团队对战 are slightly different though. As there is no objective to capture a point or steal a 旗子 you can get away with playing defensively and camping. In fact, Camping is one of the best 团队对战 tactics for small or cramped 地图.
Due to the bouncing effect from火龙珠, it makes it the perfect 炮塔 for 团队对战. You can be on your side of the 地图 and using the centre billboard, you can hit almost anyone on the 隧道对抗’s side.

The optimal weapons for the泰坦would be离子炮,轰天炮,Ricochetor 雷暴炮, making sure they don’t use the same 炮塔 to prevent 迷彩 from having a major effect. A second维京or猎人中甲with火焰炮,冰风暴,滑膛炮,离子炮or轰天炮can replace one of the泰坦as an up-front attacker, but this 职务 will require using or picking up quite a few 道具.

防御

山岗 isn’t really a defending/offending type 地图. As it is so small, it becomes everyone’s responsibility to both attack and defend. You can still play in a defensive style, but defending won’t be your only responsibility.
如果你想打防守风格，离子炮,极速炮,火龙珠or冰风暴是你首选的炮塔。当安装在一个维京,独裁者,泰坦or猛犸象他们不仅提供强有力的防守，还能成为值得信赖的攻击者.
Your best 职务 would be on your side of the 山岗 around where the 速度提升 drops, so you can catch people trying to jump over onto your 旗子, or on the top of the 山岗 facing the 红队旗子. Be careful of黄蜂轻甲和蜂王跳到你身上并埋一个地雷，但中间的杆子应该可以阻止他们获得足够的速度来靠近你。
