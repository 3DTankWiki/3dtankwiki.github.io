一般信息:

  • Usually, 射击效果 can only be obtained from 幸运礼盒.
    • 射击效果 may also be part of 特价优惠, 星级挑战奖励, and prizes in contests and 提升.
  • If you get a 射击效果 but don't yet have a 炮塔 to 装备 it to, that effect will remain in the 车库. When you have the desired 炮塔, you can then 装备 the previously obtained effect on it.
  • To change the colour of a shot, select the 炮塔 and choose the desired effect in the information window.
  • The 射击效果 can be 装备 on the 炮塔 along with an 装备改造 and a 皮肤.
  • Each 炮塔 has a standard 射击效果, which is the same on all modifications.
