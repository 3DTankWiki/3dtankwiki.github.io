Disintegration

In the 93rd 视频周报 On May 27th, 2016, developers announced a new update, "Disintegration". This update separated 迷彩 and 防护 and added防御模块to the game.

«

'With this update, all 迷彩 in 3D坦克 will be separated into two components. The visual component, which only affects the 坦克's appearance, and the 特殊抗性防御模块, which grant you 抗性 against 炮塔 in the game. [...] The main advantage of this system is flexibility. 玩家 will have a lot more 选项 to combine 抗性, and more importantly, they will be able to combine any 防护防御模块 with any 迷彩, without any restrictions. [...] The 迷彩玩家 already owned will remain in their possession, but they will also be able to combine the 抗性 they need with the look that they want.'

»
—Semyon Strizhak (Game designer)
