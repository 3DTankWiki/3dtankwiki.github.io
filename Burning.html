Mechanics

着火is a状态效果which deals伤害to a 坦克 over the course of its 持续时间. The 持续时间 of the effect is between 1 and 10 seconds and depends on how much the 坦克 gets heated up.

When applied on a 坦克:

  • The 坦克 cools down by 2.5% per tick (¼ of a second) or 10% per second of its 最高 temperature.
    • 最高 temperature is 100%
    • Some applicators of 着火 are unable to reach the 最高 temperature.
  • 着火 deals 100 伤害 per tick (400 per second), regardless of the temperature. Temperature only affects the 持续时间 of this effect.
    • If the 坦克 is heated up to 100%, it will «Burn» for 10 seconds, however, the last second of 状态效果 won't deal 伤害. This means the 最高伤害 from 着火 is 4000 伤害.
    • Applying the冷冻状态效果 will 速度 up the loss of temperature.


Properties and behavior of the «着火» 状态效果:

  • 伤害 from 着火 is not increased by«伤害提升» or any 其他 伤害 increase.
  • 伤害 from 着火 is reduced by«护甲提升» 道具 or any 其他 护甲 increase.
  • Using the«维修工具» 道具 immediately removes the 着火状态效果 and provides short immunity from it.
  • 防护防御模块 for applicators of 着火, such as火焰炮,do notdecrease 伤害 from 着火.
  • 圣骑士's 过速装置 does not protectfrom 着火.


坦克 with this 状态效果 can be easily recognized by this icon:. If an enemy receives the 状态效果, the text«着火»will appear above their 坦克.

Ways to apply the 状态效果

火焰炮

Direct 伤害 from this 炮塔 applies «着火» 状态效果 on enemy 坦克. In addition, there are装备改造for 火焰炮 that alter the effect's properties.

极速炮

During continuous shooting, the 炮塔 overheats after the reload indicator empties. When overheated, 极速炮 applies the 着火状态效果 to itself.

黄蜂轻甲过速装置

黄蜂轻甲's 炸弹 applies the «着火» 状态效果 on every enemy in the explosion 半径 for 10 seconds in addition to«眩晕» and«电磁脉冲».

装备改造

There are multiple装备改造 for 炮塔, which are able to apply the «着火» 状态效果. Such 装备改造 typically have «Incendiary» in their title, and a red-orange 车库 icon background.

南瓜

南瓜is an 活动手榴弹 that can only be obtained in the Halloween minigame "Trick or Treat". It deals unavoidable chaos 伤害to surrounding 红队 and applies 着火. It also destroys all enemy地雷within a certain 半径.

防护 from the «着火» 状态效果

维修工具

Using a 维修工具 applies the«Polarizer» 状态效果 for a very short time, providing immunity from all 状态效果.

抵抗

Upon spawning, every 坦克 receives a short period of «抵抗», which nullifies all negative 状态效果.

磁力炮

治疗 a teammate using 磁力炮 cools it down by 1 temperature per second if it's currently 着火.

猛犸象's 过速装置

All negative 状态效果 are removed on activation and the 坦克 gains immunity from them for the 持续时间 of the 过速装置.

剑圣

剑圣 has immunity from all negative 状态效果.

装备改造

There are multiple装备改造 for 底盘which protect from «着火». The «耐热性» 装备改造 reduces 着火伤害 by 50% and slows the rate at which the 坦克 gets heated by 50%. The «抗热» 装备改造 completely prevents the «着火» effect being applied.

Whenever an attempt is made to apply the «着火» 状态效果 to an enemy with 抗热, the text«Immunity»appears above their 坦克.
