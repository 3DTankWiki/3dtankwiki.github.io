坦基亚达


«Tankiada» is an official tournament in which 坦克手们 from around the community compete in a series of different 活动. Much like the Olympics, this tournament lets 玩家战斗 it out for prizes with just one catch: go through a difficult but fascinating competition journey.

Tankiada 2021

Tankiada 2021 consisted of two disciplines:

  • Headspinners Race
  • 接力赛

头旋赛车


本次活动的任务是在比赛中首先完成。然而，有一些限制：禁止走出赛道，并且您必须不断朝一个方向旋转您的炮塔。攻击对手是被禁止的，但是推撞彼此是允许的。资格赛由十场比赛组成，每场比赛有十名玩家参加。每场资格赛的第一名玩家将晋级决赛，十名最佳玩家将为奖品竞争。在决赛中，最快通过赛道并到达终点线的玩家获胜！

  • Equipment:激光炮-蜂王Mk7
  • 等级: Any
  • Prizes:
    • 1st Place: Fully-upgraded 无人机 (except 危机) + 100
    • 2nd Place: 500+ 50
    • 3rd Place: 300+ 30

Relay Race

Stages of the race
Stage 1: 禁止

Here, the orange lines indicates ways to reach the starting 职务 (denoted by orange circles). The first 玩家 will start by taking the 旗子 and waiting for the organizer's command. Upon "GO", the 玩家 will drive through the route on the 禁止 marked by the red line.

阶段 2：坡道和边缘

Once the 玩家 reaches the end of the first stage, they will 通行证 the 旗子 to their teammate waiting in the second stage. This teammate will then navigate through the ramps and edges of Stage 2 and try to reach Stage 3.

Stage 3: Barriers

在第三阶段，第三位参与者将尽力穿越障碍并达到第四阶段。或者，他们也可以跳过一些障碍，走捷径。

第4阶段：平台

In the last stage, the final teammate will be responsible for getting the 旗子 safely across the alternating platforms, make their way to the final ramp, and jump off the 地图. Whoever self-destructs first wins the race!


这个活动中的星级挑战是通过地图的四个阶段，一个一个地经过旗子，首先到达结束动感区域。参与者可以选择自己的方式穿越地图，因为有很多路线可以到达终点。也允许走捷径！
A total of eight 团队, each with four participants and two reserve 玩家 could take part in the 活动. 团队 were divided between the INT and RU locales.

  • Equipment:轰天炮-黄蜂轻甲Mk7
  • 等级: 五级准尉- 传奇
  • Prizes:
    • 获胜团队:
      • 完全升级的无人机（除了危机，只针对四个主要玩家）
      • 1000
      • 50
    • 第二名团队:
      • 500
      • 30
