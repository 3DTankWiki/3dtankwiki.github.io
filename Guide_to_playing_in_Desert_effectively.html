Guide to playing in 沙漠 effectively

see also:Guides for 地图

About


Overview:沙漠 is a very open 地图. It is commonly used for 经验值/BP Death matches
战斗模式:死亡混战, 团队对战, 互夺军旗, 控制点
大小:5v5, 10 玩家

Tactics

死亡混战/团队对战

“Camping” Tactic

沙漠 is the perfect 地图 for 激光炮, which explains why it is commonly used for 经验值/BP 战斗. There will be a ton of 露营者 in 沙漠 as it is set up perfectly for it. There are a few good spots where you have a great view of the 地图, have great cover and can go unnoticed. You will want to pick one that isn’t targeted by 其他 or being used by 其他 so you stay “under the radar”

  1. 职务 A – Top right corner of the 地图. This is a great spot if you can 射击 through bushes with precision. You don’t have a massive area to aim at but the area you can see it generally filled with 坦克手们
  2. 职务 B – Bottom right corner of the 地图. This spot is quite open and does not cover you as much, but pick your shots well and you should remain hidden. You will need to watch the wall for someone trying to get a sneaky shot on you.
  3. 职务 C – Very commonly used, but if you gain 控制 of this area then you will certainly get a good 分数.

“Hit and Run” Tactic

This tactic is much harder to use effectively as it requires you to be as unpredictable and sneaky as possible, which is hard in a very open 地图. This tactic works well in any part of 沙漠, from the close combat side (Middle) to the open area (Sides). The key to pulling off this tactic effectively in the 红队’ base is to always try to attack your target from behind and keep out of your targets 其他红队 sights, which shouldn’t be too hard considering their focus will be on your base. You will need to be moving almost always as that makes you a harder target to hit, especially for镭射炮and激光炮at extremely close 射程. You will need to try and finish off your 红队 very quickly as there will be 露营者 teammates waiting to steal your kill. The best combos for this would be火焰炮,冰风暴and滑膛炮, as they are extremely 高伤害炮塔.

互夺军旗

Defence

Defending in 沙漠 is easy if done correctly. It is very easy to stop an attack is you pick the right equipment and spots. Camping is a great way to defend in 互夺军旗, but you will need to know where to go and what to do there.

  1. The “walls” – The walls either side of the 旗子 are the perfect spot to defend. They 允许 you to pop out and 射击 the 旗子 carrier while giving you 防护 when you are reloading. As there are spots you can defend from in the middle section of the 地图, you can also 允许 you to go and quickly return the 旗子. Works best with雷暴炮,轰天炮,激光炮and滑膛炮on top of蜂王,猎人中甲or维京
  2. 角落 – 这些地方也很好，因为它们在重新装填时提供了一些防护，同时仍然拥有广阔的视野。它还可以很好地看到旗子，这意味着你可以在他们占领或即将夺旗时对他们造成伤害。你仍然藏得很好。最适合与激光炮或镭射炮

攻击

As it is so open, attacking in 沙漠 is all about 速度 and agility. It is near impossible to attack with anything heavier than维京. You will be exposed to enemy snipers for short distances while trying to get to their base or 互夺军旗, so you need to get through these areas as quickly as possible. You will have to try and get 2-3 坦克手们 going at once and preferably from different directions. This will provide good backup and a stable attack. When returning the 旗子, never go straight up the middle. It is extremely exposed and you are a very easy target for a镭射炮或激光炮. Try zipping around the sides.

控制点

控制点的战术与往常一样，冲向控制点并尽量长时间控制它。由于所有5个控制点都位于地图的中心，重型底盘到达那里会有些慢。最有效的底盘是：

  • 猎人中甲- because it has insane pushing 启动加速度 and good health to 速度 ratio
  • 维京- because it is incredibly stable and has an awesome health to 速度 ratio
  • 蜂王- 因为它具有极好的速度、机动性和不错的生命值。

使用的炮塔取决于您要做什么。如果您计划去尝试从红队控制点偷取一个点，火焰炮这是最佳选择。它有着令人难以置信的高伤害，能够一次性伤害多个红队，即使你死了，遗留的焚烧也可以消灭剩余的红队，这样你的团队就可以进来抢夺据点。滑膛炮如果周围没有很多红队的话，这样也很好，特别是他们使用轻型底盘或已经受损的情况下。如果你打算保持后退，然后从射程消灭他们，再进行攻击。轰天炮,极速炮和雷暴炮are the way to go. Their max effective 射击射程 is quite long so there is no need to worry about your shots not doing as much 伤害 as they could be. If there are multiple 坦克手们 trying to clear you off a point, try spread out so they struggle to clear you all quickly.
