自毁


see also:状态效果

Mechanics

自毁is a 状态效果 that is applied to a 坦克 either by pressing theDeletekey or by 其他 methods described below.

When applying the 状态效果 to a 坦克:

  • A countdown begins, during which a self-destruct iconappears above the 坦克, and the 玩家 sees a self-destruct warning pop up with a timer.
  • Self-destruction is indicated in the战斗 logwith a 特殊 icon.
  • Self-destructing takes away 10战斗分数, without affecting the 玩家's 经验值.


Self-destruction methods

  • The 坦克 will self-destruct if you press theDeletekey (default).
    • If your 键盘 does not have aDeletekey, you can also useCtrl+\
  • The 坦克 will self-destruct automatically when the 坦克 is flipped over unless the correspondingsettingis disabled.
  • The 坦克 will self-destruct after changing 装备 in 战斗 if anything 其他 than迷彩,皮肤or射击效果has been changed.
  • When taking 伤害 to yourself from your own shots - 火龙珠 or 溅射伤害.
    • This self-destruct is instantaneous and has no countdown.
  • Exit from the game 动感区域 of the 地图.
    • Self-destruct is also instant.
    • In格式战斗"跑酷 and Hyper" kill 动感区域 are disabled, so you can freely move around non-playable 动感区域, and self-destruction works only when you go completely beyond the entire 地图.
  • Automatically when 玩家 becomes剑圣inSolo 剑圣.
    • 10 reputation points, in this case, are not taken away.
  • Automatically when moving to the next gun in the 特殊模式 "军备竞赛".
    • 10 reputation points, in this case, are also not taken away.

Tips

  • The most 普通 cause of self-destruction is by flipping the 坦克. If you can not flip back on your own or with the 帮助 of 蓝队, it is better to self-destruct.
  • Shooting at an enemy that is self-destructing is only advisable when the 玩家 is quite confident that he can destroy him in less than 3 seconds, the time in which the self-destruction timer takes to count down. Otherwise, you just waste your time.
  • If the 玩家 needs to quickly return to their base, self-destruction is the fastest way. As a bonus, the 玩家 will be able to re-activate a full "set" of 道具, and from respawn will have full health and a few seconds of"抵抗" to any type 伤害. However, keep in mind you will still lose 10 reputation points.
    • Such a strategic self-destruct objective is not prohibited from being used in 格式战斗 (eg 经验值/BP) and esports competitions, although some 玩家 consider such an action unacceptable within etiquette.
