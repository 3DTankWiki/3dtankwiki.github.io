Rent a Spec Administrators

Rent-a-Spec Administrators control the activity of the group of Spectators, organize their work, and are responsible for the quality of their footage.

In the list of Forum Members their names are highlighted in pale red.

Rent a Spec Helpers

Rent-a-Spec Helpers are official spectators whose task is to record videos with their special spectator accounts for members of the community. A player may hire these Helpers by making a request on the official Rent-a-Spec Discord server.

Spectators also take high quality footage for devoted eSports players. These clips get showcased in Newsreels and Highlight videos posted on the official eSports Channel.

In the list of Forum Members their names are highlighted in light turquoise.

Rent a Spec Helpers' Paint
«Spectator»
Rent a Spec Helpers Candidates

Rent-a-Spec Helpers Candidates are users who decided to try out as Rent-a-Spec Helpers themselves. After passing a trial period and receiving positive reviews of their work, they will become full Rent-a-Spec Helpers.
