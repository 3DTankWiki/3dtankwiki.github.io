Beginning

TheEnglish Cybersport Channelstarted in April 2012 with one DJ, Snyper22. He streamed 每日 radio shows, lasting for around 4 hours, from 3 pm GMT to 7 pm GMT. They included music, contests with 水晶 prizes, and Snyper streaming 战斗 he was playing in. The shows were hugely popular. Snyper also streamed the first Cybersport tournament, the 3D坦克 World Cup, which started in May 2012 and went through to July 2012, on the Livestream channel. The Livestream channel was embedded into the 3D坦克 Cybersport section of the main page, and Livestream Procaster was used to do the streaming. The Cybersport 团队 was then expanded, and Cybersport switched to the new live stream and a new channel in August 2012. After this, it switched to another new channel in May 2013. Since it started expanding, Cybersport has streamed many 活动, and there have been many Cybersport Commentators, or “DJs” as they were more commonly known.

3D坦克 World Cup

The 3D坦克 World Cup was announced on May 10, 2012, and 团队 were able to sign up on the 14th of May. Each 团队 represented a country, and 22 countries took part. They were the USA, Jamaica, Canada, Saudi Arabia, India, Malaysia, Vietnam, Japan, Australia, New Zealand, Portugal, Great Britain, Holland, Italy, Germany, Poland, Estonia, Bulgaria, Ukraine, Moldova, and Russia. The competition was run on a last 团队 standing basis i.e. the last 团队 in the competition wins. Each country was given 5 “遗弃工厂” at the beginning, and each 遗弃工厂 'produced' 1 of the 5 道具 found in the 车库. For every 遗弃工厂 owned, each 团队 member got 10 of that 道具 at the start of each week. Any 玩家 from the 等级 of Warrant 军官 Three could take part. The prize for the winning 团队 was 100,000 水晶 (in the old economics, 1 million 水晶 now) and 2000 of each 道具.

The tournament was played in the following way:

  • Every Monday, each 团队 would 星级挑战 any 其他团队 and place a bet on a certain number of 遗弃工厂.
  • Each country could have a 最高 of 3 战斗 per week, meaning that it issued one 星级挑战 and could only be challenged by 2 其他 countries. All countries could only issue one 星级挑战 a week.
  • Each country was required to 星级挑战 and make a bet, and to accept all 星级挑战 without denial. The winner takes all (the whole bet).
  • During that particular week, all 团队 had to play their challengers before the next Monday.
  • For everyone's convenience, all matches were to be played at times agreed upon by the 团队队长.
  • Any country that was left without any 遗弃工厂 was automatically knocked out of the tournament.


Each 战斗 was played as 关注:

  • 二等兵战斗 [ON]
  • 互夺军旗模式
  • 5 玩家 and 5 substitutes
  • 60 min, meaning 30 min on one side and 30 min on the 其他. The 队长 create 35 min 战斗 to 允许 5 min preparation.
  • 道具 [ON]
  • Bonus Drops [ON]
  • 迷彩: for 玩家 - any 迷彩 is allowed

The tournament took place over 10 weeks and was jointly won by India and Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA). The final 战斗 between these 2 countries never took place due to technical issues (lags) and so the prize split between them.

Before Starladder

During Starladder

In the summer of 2014, 3D坦克 signed a contract with Starladder. Once Starladder was introduced Blitz 锦标赛 were replaced with Ranking Cups, Weekend Cups and 每日 Cups.

  • 每日 Ranking Cups allowed 玩家 in beginner 军团 to win 水晶 as well as earning ranking points to qualify for the league above. Each individual tournament took place on the same 地图 where the matches were played in 蜂王-激光炮格式 without any 防护. The top three 军团 of each cup were awarded a number of 水晶 depending on the number of participating 军团.

The top three 军团 receive 水晶 awards of 350 000, 200 000, 100 000 accordingly as well as $200 US for the winning 军团.

Up to 32 军团 could take part, at a time, and the tournament was based on the process of single elimination. Restrictions on 底盘, 炮塔, and 迷彩 did not exist. Prizes for the top three 军团 consisted of 120 000, 60 000, and 30 000 水晶, accordingly.

Ranking System

Participating in the tournament will give 军团 a rating. Ratings will change when taking part in tournament matches but also from violations against the rules. Rating changes depend on these conditions:

  • For participating in the tournament = 1 point;
  • For winning the 1/4 final = 1 point;
  • For winning 1/2 final 2 points;
  • For 胜利 in the final = 3 points;
  • For third place win = 1 point;
  • For failure to appear or refusal to fight = -2 points (penalty);
  • For violations. The 军团 can be fined rating points at the decision of the organizers

军团 calculation, of rating points for winning a 战斗, is conducted according to the formula: R new = 3 * (2-10 (M1-M2) / 400 ) + 1, where M1 - is the ranking of the winning 军团, M2 - in the ranking of the opposite 军团.
