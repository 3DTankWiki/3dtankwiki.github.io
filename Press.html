3D坦克报纸

Note
Due to a dramatic decrease in the popularity of the project, the Community Management has decided to close the Newspaper. New articles are no longer being published and the Reporters 团队 was closed. The articles can still be found in the 论坛.


3D坦克报纸曾不断为社区提供最新的新闻、事实和与游戏相关的信息，依靠一支才华横溢的团队。记者. These guys were dedicated 玩家 who wrote interesting articles and stories about aspects of the game and the 论坛, occasionally interview famous 玩家 and developers, and did many 其他 things to keep you up to date. Sometimes, they even used to 报告 on new trends that 3D坦克 was experiencing. You can always find plenty of articles in the Newspaper section of the 论坛.

All articles can be easily accessed from the 特殊 section of the 3D坦克论坛 called the3D坦克报纸.

3D坦克报的历史

Ever wondered how the 3D坦克 newspaper came to be? If so,thisis the article for you. If you don't fancy re-reading every article from Issue #1, this will take you through the history and development of the 3D坦克 Newspaper.
