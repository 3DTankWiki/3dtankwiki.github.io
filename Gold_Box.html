Working mechanics

Any 金箱子 drops on the 地图 10 seconds after the notification appears on the screen, except for the celebration of “Fool Days”, when the 金箱子 drops immediately after the notification. After the 金箱子 is dropped, it doesn’t disappear until it is picked up or the 战斗 is over. Its drop 动感区域 is shown on the地图from the moment when a notification appears.

金箱子 drop conditions are different for 匹配 and PRO-战斗, but in any 战斗 a 金箱子 can be dropped by a 玩家 using the “金箱子”道具.

金箱子 can drop in any匹配战斗 starting from 二级军士长, if there are more than 40 seconds until it is over.

On some 金箱子 drop 动感区域, the 金箱子 may not fall, but instead appear on the ground. Usually this happens if there are obstacles in its way.

In 跑酷战斗, 金箱子 can’t be dropped automatically since the 战斗奖励 doesn’t increase.

How to get a 金箱子道具

金箱子道具 can be purchased in the 车库in 4 packages for红宝石from 五级准尉. 1 金箱子 costs150. Also, 金箱子 can be received for activating兑换码or in official contests/活动. 金箱子 are added as a separate 道具 in the 车库 and can be dropped in any 战斗 by pressing the6钥匙. 金箱子 dropped by a 玩家 always give1 000and no 经验值.

What 金箱子 are for

金箱子 can be used with a lot of goals. They can be dropped in 匹配战斗 to distract 玩家 from a certain goal in the 战斗. Also, they can be used to 礼物其他玩家 some水晶on 特殊 occasions, for example, congratulating a 好友 with reaching a new 等级 or as a friendly act. 金箱子 are often used to drop them to yourself and get extra 水晶, after purchases in the 车库 are made.


Black 箱子 (幸运礼盒)


钥匙 in 战斗

This 幸运礼盒 is a 特殊金箱子 that drops randomly instead of a standard 金箱子, only in 匹配战斗. 玩家 who catches it get 1 普通钥匙and 1000 经验值instead of the standard1 000from a 金箱子. 幸运礼盒 can’t be dropped by a 玩家; instead it is randomly dropped by the 服务器. Notification for a 幸运礼盒 is the same as for a standard 金箱子. The 几率 for a 幸运礼盒 to drop instead of a 金箱子 is not disclosed by the developers.

Meteorite


Meteorites are a 特殊 mechanic of a 金箱子 dropping: a certain object falls from the sky, collides with the ground and explodes, and in the place of the fall a 金箱子 appears. This unique 金箱子 can only be seen during 特殊 festive activities like Cosmonautics Day.

The explosion also deals a lot of 伤害 to 玩家 nearby, usually destroying them.

Another variant of the meteorite 金箱子 is a Christmas tree, which falls on the 地图 in the same way. This 金箱子 only appears for the celebration of New Year.

The History of the 金箱子

金箱子 History

The community firstly heard of the appearance of an unusual 箱子 with 水晶 on December 3rd, 2009. It was announced in the 3D坦克视频周报 that on several 地图, a 金箱子 would appear once the 战斗奖励 reached a set number of 水晶. The first 坦克手 to catch a 金箱子 was the 玩家 with the 昵称 Noosya - history must know its heroes! The 金箱子 instantly became the main topic of discussion in聊天and in the论坛. Day and 夜晚坦克手们 shared their opinions, hypothesizing about certain patterns. The luckiest 玩家 shared the firstscreenshotsas proof that the 箱子 had been caught. Over time, people realized the place and time of their fallout.

Originally, the 箱子 contained 1000 水晶. Back before the economy update, this was a significant amount (worth 10,000 水晶 in the current economic system), especially for the lower 等级, and those who managed to get a 金箱子 were extremely happy. However, there were 玩家, mostlyBuyer, who wanted the value of the 箱子 to be lowered. The main argument was that, for the game economy of the time, 1000 水晶 was a lot, so why should one 购买水晶 if he or she could just catch 金箱子?

This situation lasted exactly a week, and on December 11, 2009, the developers reduced the value of the 金箱子 to 100 水晶.

Back then, the 金箱子 would fall at a random time within a few minutes of the 奖励 reaching a multiple of 700 水晶. However, after Update 78, the direct link to that specific 奖励 value was removed and up until now, the 金箱子 can fall at any point during the game, whenever the 战斗奖励 increases by at least 1 水晶. It was first announced in视频周报 #62. Some 玩家 are not too happy with the fact that the 金箱子 may not fall at all, but may fall several times in a short period of time. However, the developers should not be blamed for the "rigged" mechanism of the 金箱子 dropping out - the drop 几率 is completely random.

幸运礼盒 history

幸运礼盒 first appeared during the 2017 Black Friday celebrations, after which they were removed when the sale ended. 坦克手们 liked them so much that after the New Year holidays ended on January 9, 幸运礼盒 were added to the game permanently.

金箱子猎人中甲

Within a few months of its first appearance, almost every 玩家 knew where and when a 金箱子 would drop. The game seemed to have changed. 战斗 changed completely when their 奖励 got close to 700 水晶. Many 玩家 hoping to catch a 金箱子 would start to cluster around the drop 动感区域. In all 地图, the spot used to be near the center of the 地图. 团队 would fight to 控制 that particular area to increase their 几率 of getting 100 水晶. Activating all道具several minutes before the drop was a normal procedure. Some would argue that there was no point in this as this practice would often cost the 坦克手们 more than 100 水晶 in 道具 used, but no one cared about that. Their motivations were pleasure and 肾上腺素.

Thus, the introduction of the 金箱子 into the game made it possible to entertain 玩家 who were less interested with the usual gameplay. For some well-known 玩家, the game of 3D坦克 was limited to catching 金箱子, and over time, their numbers only increased. Interested clubs began to appear. Skype conferences were created with the aim of gathering lovers of hunting for 金箱子. Catching a 金箱子 is not an easy task and a lot of luck is involved if the 玩家 is alone, so working as a 小组 greatly increased their 几率 for success. Most often this was done by experienced 坦克手们 with good equipment and a solid reserve of 道具.

Some pros have abandoned their main 账号 and started secondary ones specifically for catching 金箱子 and spending 水晶 on 升级. 昵称 like "GOLDHUNTER" and "GOLD_IS_MINE" became quite 普通. At 低等级, not everyone knew about the existence of 金箱子, so it was quite easy for such “mults” to “harvest”. The only problem was the lack of big 奖励战斗, therefore the search process was not easy.
