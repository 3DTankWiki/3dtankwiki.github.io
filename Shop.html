特价优惠

特价优惠 are available in the 商店 during 活动. They are displayed at the top of the side panel. Such offers are always limited by time and sometimes only can be purchased once.

For example, 特价优惠 may include:

  • 通行证 for 活动 for access to mini-games
  • offers with discounts that contain in-game currency, 钥匙, and 道具
  • 每日红宝石通行证

Early Access

Main Article: Early Access

Early access is a section in the 商店 where newmodification等级 of炮塔,底盘and手榴弹can be purchased beforehand. All items can be bought a few等级earlier than they are available in the车库.

兑换码

Main article:兑换码


兑换码 are the 特殊 codes that can be activated in the 商店 to receive in-game items. The codes are made out of letters and numbers and can be received via different methods outside of the game.


Attention!Do not be fooled by offers to 购买兑换码 from 玩家 - it's an attempt to scam you!


Instructions for purchasing items in the 商店

Main article: Instructions for purchasing items in the 商店

Purchases for real money

Real currency in the 商店 is set once depending on the 玩家’s country of residence; it is impossible to change it except for certain 特殊 cases, in which the 特殊Problems with paymentsform should be used.

There are different ways to pay for offers available for real money:

  • Bank cards
  • PayPal
  • 礼物 cards

After choosing the 特殊 offer, it is required to first click “购买” in the appearing window. Then the payment systems will be shown, and the 玩家 needs to choose the one they will pay with. After pressing the “充值购买” button, the 玩家 will be sent to a separate window where they need to 关注 the instructions. At the end, click “Purchase” and the purchase will be considered complete.


In themobile version of the game, payments are made with “Google Pay”. 其他 payment ways are available there.

Purchases for in-game currency

If the offer is available for 坦克币, the purchase is easily confirmed when clicking on the offer.

Purchases in the 商店 that weren’t added to the 账号

If purchases weren’t added to the 账号 after paying, fill out a特殊 formor contact the tech support directly by sending them an email: 帮助@tankionline.com

Some payment methods need to process the purchase for some time (1-2 hours): a check of the payment method by the safety system is possible. So in case you have problems with purchases, the first thing you should do is to wait some time before contacting the tech support.

Under no circumstances share your 密码, 账号 data, 电话号码, or any 其他 confidential information on 论坛!

If 水晶, 红宝石, or 坦克币 disappear on the 账号 without a purchase being made, instructions from theI lost 水晶/经验值/items from my 账号page should be followed.
