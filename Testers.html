测试人员

see also:Assistants of administration and 测试服务器

Testers - a special group of players trained to detect bugs that may have escaped during development. They demonstrate knowledge and understanding of game mechanics. A true tester is observant, inquisitive, patient, technically proficient, able to think outside the box, and able to find answers to questions. They make other players jealous because they have permanent access to the test server. According to rumors, testers are among the most aware of game mechanics and awaited new things in the game. They rarely answer questions, because they aren’t sure not only about the privacy of information they know, but also its reliability. Despite what most players think, testers don’t fix bugs in the game. Their job is to find a mistake and tell developers about it. Also it’s useless to ask testers to make it rain gold boxes, kick an annoying player out of the battle, raise the battle fund, change your nickname, give a ban, remove a ban and block or unblock an account. The best thing to do when encountering a tester - not hinder what they do.

Testers

Testers are a special group of players trained to detect bugs that may have escaped during development. They demonstrate knowledge and understanding of game mechanics. A true tester is observant, inquisitive, patient, technically proficient, able to think outside the box, and able to find answers to questions. In the list of Forum Members, their names are highlighted in 米色.


测试人员候选人

Tester candidates are players who have decided to try their hand as testers. After undergoing the appropriate training, excellent work alongside the testers, they will become full members of the tester group.
