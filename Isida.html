磁力炮


see also:Indication of parametersand装备改造


The 磁力炮炮塔 has its own game features:

  • Used in melee combat. It is impossible to reach 红队 on distance
  • Repairs ally 坦克
  • 治疗蓝队 grants extra 经验值
  • Cylinder reloads slowly, but deals a lot of 伤害

玩家' opinions
Pros
Can 伤害 opponents as well as heal 蓝队. 经验值 can be gathered quickly through both damaging and 治疗坦克. Excellent 每秒伤害 rating.
Cons
Ridiculously short 射程. Can run out of fuel and takes time to recharge. It can only place its beam onto one opponent/ally at a time.

TABLE OF CHARACTERISTICS
  • 伤害 (hp/sec)— The amount of 伤害 dealt to an enemy 坦克 per second.
  • 伤害 (hp/tick)— The amount of 伤害 dealt to an enemy 坦克 per tick or 1/4 of a second.
  • 暴击伤害 (hp/tick)— The 伤害 dealt to an enemy 坦克 upon being impacted with a 暴击 hit. Unlike normal shots, the 伤害 that a 暴击 hit deals will not decrease after passing the 最高 or 最低伤害射程.
  • 暴击伤害几率 (%)— The highest 几率 that can accumulate for a 暴击 shot.
  • 治疗 (hp/sec)— The amount of a friendly 坦克's health restored per second.
  • 治疗 (hp/tick)— The amount of a friendly 坦克's health restored per tick or 1/4 of a second.
  • 暴击治疗 (hp/tick)— 治疗 given to a teammate on a 暴击 hit.
  • 暴击治疗几率 (%)— The highest 几率 that can accumulate for a 暴击 shot.
  • 炮弹重装时间 (sec)— The total required time in seconds to replenish 磁力炮's energy if it has been completely exhausted.
  • Charging (ms)— The amount of energy regenerated per second when the weapon is not used.
  • Energy consumption damaging (eng/sec)— The amount of energy used per second if 磁力炮 is used to 伤害 an opponent continuously.
  • Energy consumption 治疗 (eng/sec)— The amount of energy used per second if 磁力炮 is used to heal an ally continuously.
  • Amount of energy— The total amount of energy that can be expended for shooting.
  • Energy consumption without target (eng/sec)— The amount of energy used per second if 磁力炮 is used without a target within its conic angle.
  • Self 治疗 (% of 伤害 dealt)- The percentage of self-heal obtained from the 伤害 dealt to an enemy.
  • 射程 (m)— 最高射程 that 磁力炮 can deal 伤害. If the distance to the target is higher than this value, no 伤害 will be dealt.
  • 锥角 (deg)— The conic, outward 射程 in degrees that the beam spreads from the tip of the 炮塔. 伤害 can not be dealt to an opponent outside of this cone, and this beam can only target one 玩家 at a time.
  • Rotation 速度 (deg/sec)— This is the 最快速度 that the 炮塔 can rotate at.
  • Rotation acceleration (deg/sec²)— The acceleration of the 炮塔 reaching its 最高 rotation 速度.

Interesting Facts

  • The 炮塔 got its name after the ancient goddess Isis that became an example of the Egyptian ideal of femininity and motherhood. When the developers 团队 decided to create a new 炮塔 in the summer of 2009, they chose between an impulse 炮塔 and a 治疗炮塔. The idea for the impulse 炮塔 was for it to create a physical impulse that would push 红队 away. Anton Volkov and Alexey Khusainov were the main authors of the idea.
  • Due to the 炮塔's beam not matching the developers’ idea, it was changed a few times (for example in the 291 release). The final result can be seen in game.
  • The distinctive feature of 磁力炮 was its «Vampirism» - regeneration of own health by dealing 伤害 to 红队. This feature was removed on the 16th of February 2017 with the 441 release.
  • Up until the 23rd of June, 2018, 磁力炮 could remove the 冷冻 and 着火 effects from ally 坦克. This feature was removed as a result of balance changes. On May 16, 2025, this temperature regulation ability was reinstated.
  • If the 玩家 with the 磁力炮炮塔 is destroyed while 治疗 a teammate, points for 治疗 will be counted when attacking or 治疗 after respawning.
