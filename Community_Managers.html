Community Managers

see also:行政管理and行政助理


Who are Community Managers?

社区经理是一个在玩家和开发者之间建立和发展的桥梁的人。他们在行政助理中拥有比所有管理员更大的权力。如果你对社区管理方面有任何问题、询问或投诉，你应该把它发送给社区经理。
在游戏聊天中，社区经理用（CM）前缀标记，在论坛成员列表中，他们的名字被突出显示。red.

社区经理的工作是什么？

A CM's main job is communication within the 3D坦克 community between the 玩家 and developers. Their tasks are to announce official game updates and 新闻, send feedback & reported bugs to the developers, manage helper 团队, collect and use data, and plan and run 未来世界 big in-game 活动.

How do I contact a Community Manager?

The best way to contact a community manager is through the 论坛, however, make sure your question/comment/complaint can be answered by a helper or admin before contacting the CM.
