专家战场

Attention!It is not possible to complete任务in 专家战场. Additionally, 金箱子 will not be dropped by the 服务器 - only by 玩家 who have used the 道具. To do the above, you must play匹配战斗.


专家战场is a place where 玩家 fight according to 特殊 rules, earning水晶and经验值.

To get to this section, click on the big «Play» button in the menu and select «专家战场».

专家战场 are available from the 等级 of一级军士长.

战斗格式

see also:格式战斗


At the 专家战场 section, you are prompted to select one of the available 格式. Each 禁止 contains a counter of the number of currently available 战斗.

专家战场

Here you play by your own rules. Create 战斗 with any 设置, find 好友, and chase 红队. Fight or socialize - the choice is yours.

Classic

A long time ago, 3D坦克 started with this exact 格式. 防御模块, 装备改造, 过速装置, and 无人机 are disabled here. The true old-school 经验值.

激光炮大师

Only 激光炮, only hardcore. Here, 玩家 fight on the lightest 底盘 with the heaviest 炮塔. Absolute minimalism.

运动会

团队 fights where everyone is on equal footing. In this 格式, 升级 on your equipment do not have any effect; 防御模块, 装备改造, and 无人机 are disabled. Purely 能力-based.

跑酷

跑酷 with 坦克 is a thing! In this 格式, restrictions on access to the corners of the 地图 have been removed. The winner is the one who jumps the highest.

Hyper

In this 格式, the restrictions on using 道具 and moving around the 地图 are lifted. Be sure to replenish your 道具 before the 战斗.

战斗 list

After choosing a 格式, a list of 战斗 appears. By clicking on the 战斗, you can find their detailed information in the section on the right.

On the menu on the left (you can expand it by clicking thebutton), you can:

  • Find your 每周 limit on水晶 and经验值
  • Search for 战斗 by 地图 name
  • Sort 战斗 by模式
  • Create a new 战斗


In the top menu, you can switch between 格式.

Creating a 战斗

Creating a 战斗 using the «Create 战斗»button takes place in several stages: 格式 selection → 模式 selection → 地图 selection → detailed 战斗设置 on the right side of the screen.

Note

玩家 who have received a聊天ban are not able to create 战斗 until the end of the restriction.

战斗设置

On the right side of the screen, you can configure:

  • The name of the 战斗
  • The number of 玩家
  • The 战斗持续时间 in minutes
  • The number of kills/旗子/points/球 to end the 战斗 early
  • The 等级射程: 上士— 传奇

Reporting a 玩家

see also:Moderation System

In 匹配, a 玩家 can be reported if they, for example, use cheats or sabotage their 团队. To 报告 a rules violation, do the following:

  1. Open战斗统计数据with theTAB钥匙
  2. Click on the 玩家’s 昵称
  3. In the appeared window, select “报告 the gameplay”


If a 玩家 writes unacceptable words or insults 其他玩家 in 聊天, they can be reported. To do so, click on the red icon near the 战斗聊天 panel to open the history of messages sent in the 战斗, then click on their 昵称 and in thecontext menu, press “报告 the message”. Ourmoderatorswill then process the 报告 as soon as possible and make the decision.

Inappropriate or insulting names of PRO-战斗 can also be reported. To do so, select that 战斗 in the 战斗 list and press “报告战斗 name” near the Custom 选项.

Earning 水晶 and 经验值

In 专家战场, as is the case in 匹配, 水晶 and 经验值 can be earned. However, 玩家 cannot earn 水晶 in 跑酷战斗.

You can earn a limited amount of 水晶 and 经验值 every week. After exceeding the limit, even 金箱子 won't add any 水晶 to your 账号. The first time a 玩家 enters the game, a 7-days timer is started. After the timer ends, the next time the 玩家 enters the game, the 7-days timer is restarted.

The amount of the available 奖励 depends on your 等级. With each new 等级, the limit increases by 2800and 2100.vip 账号increases the available limit.

At the same time, you need to keep in mind that the 分数 (reputation points) and 经验值 are two different things. For the same action, a 坦克手 is awarded a different number of reputation points and 经验值.

特殊聊天

While in one of the 格式 lists, pressTABto open the corresponding聊天 channel. There you can find 蓝队 for 战斗 or 玩家 with similar interests.

Why can’t I enter the 战斗?

  • All 免费 places are already occupied. You can wait until a spot becomes available, but keep in mind that this may not happen or that another 坦克手 may get ahead of you and enter the 战斗 faster.
  • The selected 战斗 is a格式战斗; it has a restriction on the equipment used.

Trivia

  • To create a 专家战场 in the Flash version, you needed a «专家战场» 通行证 or a vip membership.
History of the «专家战场» 通行证
  • The 通行证 was introduced in early 2010 and was called «战斗 without 道具».
  • Until April 23, 2010, the subscription cost was 2 000 水晶 (20 000 at the current rate).
  • From April 23, 2010, the subscription cost was 1 000 水晶 (10 000 at the current rate).
  • On June 4, 2010, the entrance to each «战斗 without 道具» became possible to pay separately, and the subscription itself fell in price to 500 水晶 (5 000 at the current rate).
  • After update 208 of December 19, 2013, the 通行证 acquired its current name.
  • On December 11, 2014, the ability to create “专家战场” and participate in such 战斗 for 免费 without a subscription was disabled.
  • Configurable parameters in «专家战场» often change.
  • After the introduction of 每日任务, the subscription cannot be received as a 礼物 when entering the game (does not apply to the bonus after a month's absence).
  • Introduced into the game on October 21, 2015, vip 账号 received, among 其他 things, all the features of the “专家战场“ 通行证.
  • On November 24, 2016, having the 通行证 became a prerequisite for creating your 战斗.
  • On May 12, 2017, 专家战场 began to be displayed at the bottom of the list of 战斗, regardless of whether you have a PRO 解锁星级挑战奖励加成 or vip membership.
  • On October 20, 2017, the PRO 解锁星级挑战奖励加成 became available for purchase with the 等级 of 中士.
  • On July 20, 2018, the season ticket became available for purchase with the 等级 of 五级准尉.
  • On December 1, 2020, support for the Flash version of 3D坦克 ended, and the PRO 解锁星级挑战奖励加成 disappeared along with it.
