过速装置 - 侦察机 Radar

Main article:过速装置
侦察机 Radar

The 坦克 that activated the 过速装置 and all of its teammates get the ability to see indicators of location and health of enemy 坦克. Also during the activation of the 过速装置 all enemy 坦克, which are in a direct line of sight with the 坦克 which activated the 过速装置, get the “护甲-piercing” 状态效果.

Table of Characteristics
  • 防护 (hp)— 坦克's health. 伤害 dealt by a 炮塔 is subtracted from a 坦克's 防护. Once the 防护 gets to zero, the 坦克 explodes.
  • Top 速度 (m/sec)— 最快速度 the 坦克 can get to on a straight.
  • Lateral acceleration (m/sec²)— responsible for damping of lateral 速度. The less this parameter is, the easier the 坦克 can drift. The higher it is, the more difficult it is for a 坦克 to move sideways.
  • 转向灵活度 (deg/sec)— the 最快速度 at which the 坦克 can turn.
  • Turning acceleration (deg/sec²)— affects the time it takes a 坦克 to get to its 最高转向灵活度.
  • Anti-inertial turning acceleration (deg/sec²)— works like reverse acceleration but for left-right turns instead of moving forward/backward.
  • 重量— affects 坦克's stability, i.e. 冲击力 of hits, the 后坐力 of its own 炮塔, and partly, ability to move 其他坦克 (this parameter does not affect 坦克's 速度).
  • 启动加速度 (m/sec²)— affects the time it takes a 底盘 to get to its 最快速度 and ability to push 其他坦克.
  • Acceleration (m/sec²)— the time it takes for the 底盘 to build to top 速度.
  • Reverse acceleration (m/sec²)— anti-inertial acceleration that comes into force when a 坦克 is moving in one direction and a 玩家 presses the button of moving to another direction. It works until 速度 gets to zero. After that, the usual acceleration starts working.
  • Nitro acceleration (m/sec²)— the time it takes a 底盘 to get to its 最快速度 when the 速度提升 is activated.
  • 过速装置 charge per 战斗 point (%)— the percentage by which the 过速装置 charges per 战斗 point received.
  • 过速装置 charge per second (%)— the percentage by which the 过速装置 charges per second played.
  • 耐热性 (%)— the percentage of 耐热性 the 底盘 withstands.
  • 冰风暴抗性 (%)— the percentage of 冰风暴抗性 the 底盘 withstands.
  • 电磁脉冲抗性 (%)— the percentage of 电磁脉冲抗性 the 底盘 withstands.
  • 眩晕抗性 (%)— the percentage of 眩晕抗性 the 底盘 withstands.
  • 穿甲抗性 (%)— the percentage of 穿甲抗性 the 底盘 withstands.
  • 干扰器抗性 (%)— the percentage of 干扰器抗性 the 底盘 withstands.

Interesting Facts

  • 蜂王 was the first “second generation” 底盘, which was added into the 车库 on the 27th of April, 2010. This 活动 was the 原因 for a lot of discussions and feedback, both positive and negative.
  • When 蜂王 was first tested, it was made out of five 禁止. Four side parts were connected to the main one, each side part was lower and had tracks. Later 蜂王 was changed to the current condition because it was too futuristic and caused problems with 铁塔 and traction.
  • On the 28th of December, 2014 new 精英 New Year armament was added into the game - 蜂王 XT. It was equal to standard 蜂王 of the same modification, but with upgraded by 50% parameters. Owners of M2 and M3 modifications of 蜂王 XT had the ability to participate in 格式战斗 (whilst having a 50% advantage over 红队). The 底盘 could be rented up to the 12th of January, 2015.
  • 蜂王 was the first armament in the game to receive an updated 高 quality standard 皮肤. This happened on the 25th of December, 2020 with the release #633.
  • 蜂王 was the first 底盘 to have its 皮肤 updated to HD, getting the glow-up on December 25th, 2020.
