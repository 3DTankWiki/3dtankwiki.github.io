Clan Championship 8


Clan Championship 8 was announced on 25th February 2014, and ran until 11th April. For the first time, the prizes included money! First place received $6 000, 10 000 000 crystals and the Champion paint; second received $2 000, 10 000 000 crystals and the Silver paint; third received $1 000, 6 000 000 crystals and the Bronze paint. Champion has 22% against all turrets; Silver has 25% protection against Smoky and Thunder, 30% against Twins and 20% against Ricochet and Shaft. Bronze has 35% against Ricochet and Railgun, and 40% against Twins. The finals took place on 11th April, Clan Noobest vs Scorpions, and Tanki players could choose one of two paints representing the clan they thought would win. See here for reference. NooBest won, Scorpions placed second, and MIX third. These were all Russian clans. For the tournament table and information, see this link.

Clan Championship 9


Early in November 2016, the Clan Championship Season 9 (CC9) took place. The top 16 clans had been competing for their rightful place in the Blitz Tournament ratings for a few months while expecting the announcement of this tournament. Maps used in the tournament were: Red Alert, Bridges, Parma, Tribute, Molotov, Solikamsk, Barda, Osa, Kungur. Maps for the penalty: Parma, Sandbox, Forest.

Clan Championship 9 - Formats:

  • XP/BP
    • Hulls: Wasp (no more than three per team), Hornet
    • Turrets: Rail
    • Format: XP / BP
    • Paints: Any
    • Protection modules : N/a
    • Supplies: Off
  • Light
    • Hulls: Hornet, Wasp (not more than 2 per team)
    • Turrets: Rail, Smoky (not more than 1 per team)
    • Paints: Any
    • Protective modules : 35% resistance against Smoky (not more than 2 per team), 35% resistance against Railgun (not more than 2 per team); no more than one of these protections per player
    • Micro-upgrades: Off
    • Supplies: Off
  • Medium
    • Hulls: Light, Medium (no more than 3 per team)
    • Turrets: All except Isida, no more than 1 of each turret type per team
    • Paints: Any
    • Protection modules : Any other than the unique prize modules
    • Mode: CTF
    • Micro-upgrades: Off
    • Supplies: Off
    • Alterations: On
  • Hard
    • Hulls: All, Heavy (Mammoth, Titan) - not more than 2 per team
    • Turrets: All (no more than 2 players with the same turret per team), Isida only on light hulls
    • Paints: Any
    • Protection modules: Any other than the unique prize modules
    • Mode: CTF
    • Micro-upgrades: Off
    • Supplies: On
    • Smart supplies : Off
    • Alterations: On

The clans were split up into two groups where each clan played 14 games. After the group stage, half of the clans advanced to the quarter finals. In the playoffs, the process was based on double elimination which caused clans to be eliminated after two losses.

Clan Championship 9 - Results

  • 1. Synergy - Champion, Gladiator, 30 million crystals, Spectrum M-A module (25% resistance to all turrets and mines), 6 months of premium, and 20 000 Rubles in vouchers (~$350US)
  • 2. Can’t Stop - Silver, Gladiator, 20 million crystals, Spectrum M-B module (20% resistance to all turrets and mines), and 10 000 Rubles in vouchers (~$175US)
  • 3. Eternity was awarded the paint Bronze, Gladiator, 10 million crystals, Spectrum M-C module (15% resistance to all turrets and mines), and 5 000 Rubles in vouchers (~$90US)
  • 4. Vavilon was awarded the paint Gladiator and 5 million crystals
  • 5-8. Chaos, Next, Invincible, and Revolution were awarded the paint Gladiator and 1 million crystals
  • 9-16. Arm Power, All Kind (ex Fame), GGWP, Chicos, WOODPECKERS, Regrets, Deadly Army, HARD SHOW, STAR WARRIORS - 50 000 crystals per player.

Clan Championship X


Clan Championship, the most important tournament of the year - where only the TOP-16 clans from the Russian Blitz rating can participate. This is the tournament where the clans fight for the most expensive eSports paints in the game; Champion, Silver and Bronze. The winning clan receive the title "The best eSports clan"!

Clan Championship X - Progression:

  • Group stage: In the group stage, clans will be split up into four groups (A, B, C, D) with no more than four clans in each group. Clans in groups will be sorted by earned points (for a win a clan will receive 3 points, for a draw 1 point, for a loss – 0). The teams who finished first and second in their groups, will participate in the play-off stage.consists of two stages: Clans play one match against each clan in their group in first group stage and one match against each clan in their group in second group stage. The “host” status alternates in group stages. In group stage a match consists of four rounds.
  • Playoffs: Double Elimination; A loser of each match in upper bracket is immediately eliminated from upper stage to lower bracket, but a loser of each match in lower bracket is immediately eliminated from the tournament.
  • Finals and Grand Finals: five battles in different formats and on different maps are held, each consisting of four rounds. The winner of the match is determined by the results of the rounds in sum of five battles;
