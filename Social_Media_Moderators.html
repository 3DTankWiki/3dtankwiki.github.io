Social Media Administrators

Social Media Administrators supervise the activities of the group of helpers in the social media and organize their work. They handle complaints about errors in the work of the moderators while also monitoring the order of the social networks.

In the list of Forum Members their names are highlighted in pale red.

Social Media Helpers

Social Media Moderators keep Tanki’s social media communities orderly and pleasant. They maintain the communities, help players, monitor social networks, and make sure that the rules are respected.

In the list of the Forum Members their names are highlighted in bright yellow.

Social Media Moderator Candidates

Social Media Moderator Candidates are players who decided to try helping the game as Social Media Moderators. After passing a trial period and receiving positive reviews of their work, they will become full Social Media Moderators.
