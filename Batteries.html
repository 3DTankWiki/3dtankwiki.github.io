道具



另见:过速装置和无人机

道具, 或者Consumables是可以在战斗中使用的消耗品。其中一些允许你暂时提高你的坦克的某个特征。例如，你可以在一定时间内加速你的坦克，充电。过速装置并放下一个金箱子 or throw a 手榴弹.

In battle, the amount of supplies available and their current status are displayed at the bottom of the screen on a special panel. Each supply has its own sound of the beginning and end of work, as well as the visual effect of availability for use. The default supply activation keys are 1,2,3,4,5和6, as well as Shift for Nuclear Energy and F为手榴弹。

Also, supplies fall from the sky right during the battle to specially marked points (drop zones). Tankers usually refer to such supplies as drops. Picked up supplies immediately take effect and cannot be stockpiled or stored in the garage. If you activated a supply and at the same time or a little later (but before the end of the activated supply) picked up the same supply-drop, then the effect of the supply will be extended. The supply drop rate is 84 seconds (based on a supply pickup).

Dependent Cooldown

Dependent Cooldown — the period of time in which it is impossible to activate a supply if another supply is activated.

例如，如果«伤害提升»道具被激活，则可以在5秒后激活«护甲提升»。道具的冷却和充能在坦克复活时重置。拾取的掉落物不影响冷却。

Dependent Cooldowns can be removed by wearing one of two drones: «亥伯龙神»或«危机».

In 匹配 battles, the Dependent Supplies option is enabled by default. In 玩家创建的战斗，这个选项默认是禁用的，让玩家可以选择一次性激活所有道具。

自动道具激活

You can choose a supply that will be 自动地 activated when either its effect ends or its cooldown is over. For that you need to press the needed supply activation button. After the supply's effect or cooldown is over, it will automatically be activated. Also, the system automatically activates 当坦克的生命值低于25%时，使用维修工具和道具。

  • Automatic activation of the supply can be canceled by pressing the activation button again.
  • Next supply that will automatically be activated can be changed by pressing the corresponding supply activation button.
  • Automatic activation can be switched off in the 设置一般情况下，它不可用于神谕者和危机无人机.
