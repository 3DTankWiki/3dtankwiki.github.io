Project Administration

这些包括开发者、社区经理和专业支持用户。

在游戏的聊天中，社区经理用红色字母组合标记。(СМ)


聊天助手

Main article:聊天管理员

Chat Administrators

Chat Administrators are an elite unit of the defenders of order, who perform the same functions as Chat Moderators, but with greater powers, including the authority to ban violators from the chat forever. Chat Administrators handle complaints about the work of their team.

聊天管理员

Chat Moderators are the all-seeing eyes of the Administration. They are always on the front line, right in the game, and make sure that flooders, swear words, "jocks" and other rule breakers don't spoil the spirits of good-hearted tankers.

聊天版主候选人

This includes users who have decided to try themselves as chat moderators. After proper training, a probationary period, and positive feedback, they will become full-fledged chat moderators.

Battle Moderators

主条目:Battle Moderators

Battle Administrators

Battle Administrators control the activities of the group and organize their work. They handle complaints about errors in the work of the moderators on top of monitoring the order of the game.

战斗版主

专门训练的专业人士专门对抗违规行为——破坏和黑客。如果您有第三方软件使用或团队背叛的证据，可以在论坛的“游戏破坏者”部分创建一个帖子进行报告。


论坛助手

Main article:论坛管理员

Forum Administrators

Forum Administrators manage activities of the Forum Moderators, handle complaints about moderation errors, as well as monitor the order of the Forum.

Forum Moderators

这些勇敢的战士保持着论坛的秩序：他们不知疲倦地清理关闭话题的“沼泽”，打扫“杂乱无章”的话题的“厩房”，同时删除无关内容，以专注于更重要的细节。如果有人突然决定在论坛上捣乱，因为“在互联网上一切皆有可能”，请呼叫版主，他们会迅速解释沟通的规则，永远和随时都能解释。

Forum Moderator Candidates

Forum Moderator Candidates are users who have decided to try out themselves as moderators of the Forum. After proper training, a probationary period, and positive feedback, they will become full-fledged moderators of the Forum.


电子竞技助手

主'article:eSports Helpers

电子竞技管理员

The eSports Administrators coordinate and ensure the quality and regularity of eSport activities in the community. They are the leaders of the group of eSport helpers and supervise their work. If you have any complaint about the work of the eSport helpers, you should contact them.

eSports Officials

电子竞技官员的任务是确保电子竞技比赛期间一切正常运作。他们还负责处理团队投诉，并帮助玩家解决与电子竞技相关的问题。

You can reach out to them using the TankiSport Support tickets

eSports Content Helpers

电子竞技内容团队报道比赛及整个电子竞技社区。这个大型小组负责撰写比赛评论，以及拍摄、编辑和设计电子竞技视频。

电子竞技内容助手候选人

这些包括决定尝试作为3D坦克电子竞技内容助手的用户。在完成适当的培训、试用期和对他们工作的积极反馈后，他们将成为正式的电子竞技内容助手。


Wiki Editors

Main article:Wiki Editors

Wiki Administrators

They supervise the activities of the group of editors, organize their work, and are responsible for the quality of the information they publish.

Wiki Editors

One of the units of the information troops of Tanki Online, whose task is to provide tankers with the latest information, as well as keep in order a treasure trove of knowledge - Tanki Online Wiki.

Wiki Editor Candidates

This includes users who have decided to try their hand as wiki editors. After appropriate training, a probationary period, and positive feedback on their work, they will become full-fledged editors of the wiki.


Social Media Moderators

Main article:Social Media Moderators

社交媒体管理员

Social Media Administrators supervise the activities of the group of helpers in the social media and organize their work. They handle complaints about errors in the work of the moderators while also monitoring the order of the social networks.

Social Media Moderators

3D坦克的社交媒体社区通过一组版主保持秩序和良好的氛围。社交媒体版主维护这些社区。

社交媒体版主候选人

他们是决定尝试作为社交媒体管理员来帮助游戏的玩家。在通过试用期并获得对他们工作的积极评价后，他们将成为正式的社交媒体管理员。


Testers

主'article:Testers

测试管理员

Tester administrators monitor the activities of the test group and organize work on the test server.

Tester

Not a single new product, not a single solution, even the most successful at first glance, gets into the game without testing. It is the testers who are the first to try out all the innovations, help identify errors and debug all the mechanisms so that they work clearly and flawlessly.

Testers Candidates

测试候选人是决定尝试作为测试员的玩家。在接受适当的培训并与测试员一起开展出色的工作后，他们将成为测试小组的正式成员。


租赁规范

Main article:租赁一个专家助手

Rent-a-Spec Administrators

租赁专员管理员控制观众小组的活动，组织他们的工作，并对他们拍摄的画质负责。

Rent a Spec Helpers

An official Rent-a-Spectator's task is to record videos with their special spectator accounts for members of the community. A player may hire these Helpers by making a request on our official Rent-a-Spec Discord server! Spectators also take high quality footage for our devoted eSports players - these clips get showcased in Newsreels and Highlight videos posted on our official eSports Channel!

Rent a Spec Helper Candidates

决定自己尝试成为租赁专家助手的用户。在经过试用期并获得工作积极评价后，他们将成为全职租赁专家助手。


广播团队

主条目:广播员和评论员

Broadcasting Team Leaders

广播管理者协调英语广播员和评论员团队的工作。如果您对小组中助手的工作有投诉，您应与他们联系。

Commentators

评论员提供3D坦克电子竞技和社区活动最激烈战斗的现场评论。他们的工作是在直播中娱乐观众，并在直播期间为社区创造有趣的内容。他们是社区的公共声音！

广播公司

Broadcasters are an essential component of the livestream team. They are the workforce who physically bring the live camera footage to viewers; without their work it would be impossible to host any broadcasts.

评论员候选人

他们是决定尝试作为评论员的用户。在经过试用期并获得对他们工作的积极评价后，他们将成为正式的评论员。


Translators, Proofreaders, Game translators

Main article:Translators, Proofreaders and Game Translators

Translators, Proofreaders, Game translators Administrator

翻译、校对和游戏翻译的管理员执行与翻译、校对和游戏翻译相同的功能，但拥有更大的权限，并监督小组的工作。

翻译者

The Translators provide subtitles for videos on the EN YouTube channel of the game. They translate into a wide array of languages, making the V-LOGs accessible to more players.

Proofreaders

校对员负责信息和公告的准确性。他们会消除官方公告和我们媒体来源中的语法错误。

游戏翻译者

游戏翻译负责将游戏语言翻译成多种语言，包括任务、物品、设备以及普通玩家每天看到的一切。

翻译者，校对候选人，游戏翻译

这些是决定尝试作为校对员、翻译员和游戏翻译员的用户。经过试用期并获得对他们工作的正面评价后，他们将成为完全的内容助手。
