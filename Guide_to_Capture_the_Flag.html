Scoring

Since 旗子 are at the centre of this 模式, it makes sense for 旗子 capturing and returning to be worth the most points. Capturing the 旗子 generally gives you the most points, but the amount you get depends somewhat on the distance travelled too. Because of this, it makes a lot of sense to back up the 旗子-bearer. If they are killed, or are weak, you can be ready to take the baton immediately and keep it moving towards a capture as fast as possible.

If the enemy 旗子 is returned, all is reset. That is to say all the 旗子-holding that you've done has gone to waste and none of those 玩家 will get any points!

Returning a 旗子 also gains you points - and it depends on how far the 旗子 is from your base. Returning a 旗子 that's right next to your base will give you just a measly point.

Offence

There are various roles you can take up within a 互夺军旗团队, and one of the most important is offence. Without attacking aggressively and trying for the enemy 旗子, how can you expect to win?
