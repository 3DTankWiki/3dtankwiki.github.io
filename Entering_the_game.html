Entering via official Mobile App

TO Mobile is available to download for 免费 in the Google Play store (an app that comes with all Android smartphones). Simply go to the Play Store and search for "3D坦克", press "Install", and then 关注 the on-screen instructions. Alternatively, you can use the link below which will take you directly to the Play Store.

  • Android App download

Features

TO Mobile 有许多独特的功能。其中一些是好的和积极的，例如免费项目和特殊活动，而其他的一些则不那么理想，例如不可用的战斗列表。以下是该应用程序最主要的积极和消极特征列表。

  • It is possible to "set" 道具 that are on 冷却 to automatically activate when they are available; this can be done by tapping them during their 冷却. This feature gives a significant advantage to Mobile users as it 允许 them to keep their hands 免费 to use the 控制;
  • 有一个水平的 "瞄准辅助"；如果一辆坦克在你的炮塔路径上，即使你瞄准他们的右边/左边，你的射击也可以击中目标；
  • 活动 unique to TO Mobile such as the "初学者的游行"是可用的。"
  • 特价优惠独特于移动版本，例如First Purchase Bonus" and "Piggy Bank".

  • 控制可能需要一些时间来适应；
  • 图标一开始可能会让人困惑；
  • 专家战场 can't be accessed (neither the 战斗 list);
  • Reporting violators using the 投票 system isn't possible;
  • 兑换码 can't be claimed in the Mobile version;
  • Both PC and Mobile 玩家 play together in the same 战斗;
  • 军团 system hasn't been added yet;
  • Mobile 玩家 cannot see 战斗聊天;
  • 该应用仅在安卓设备上可用。


The developers are actively working towards adding as many "missing" features as possible and overall improving TO Mobile.
