About 高原


Overview:高原 is quite a complicated 地图 - it has various height 等级, is quite cramped at certain places, and requires a specific set of 能力 to play in. It is a medium-sized 地图 and an excellent training place for mastering close-defensive and offensive tactics. It offers a nice 经验值 in the fast-paced gameplay which only a minority of 玩家 notice and take for good. It is also a nice and popular place for parkouring. Small to medium ranged 炮塔 will thrive here, but due to the vast 宇宙 between both the bases, longer-ranged 炮塔 such as激光炮or镭射炮can sometimes pop up too.

死亡混战

“Camping” Tactic

Camping in 高原 is a good tactic, as long as you're using镭射炮,激光炮or马格南. See, the thing is, if you're using something else than these 炮塔, you'd be better doing mid-fielding rather than sniping; mainly because of the 地图's very own 大小. For instance, if you want to snipe with something like雷暴炮or轰天炮, I'd suggest you ditch your spot and go all out in the middle.

Furthermore, sniping requires some basic sense of picking targets and making sure they die before they do what their role is in the 战斗. For example, if you get the dilemma of shooting between a 坦克 that's coming for you and a 坦克 that weaker, but far away, 射击 the one that's weaker first. You'll steal the kill, decreasing the 几率 of that person to win while simultaneously taking out the person that's coming for you.

As for道具, I recommend using Double 伤害 to increase your 伤害 output; though you should keep some Double 护甲 in case the whole 战斗 decides to end you once and for all.

Additionally, you can choose between a wide array of camping spots in 高原, be it behind the house in the blue base or this area in the red.

“Rending” Tactic

Basically, if you got nice equipment, such as离子炮/轰天炮/雷暴炮/火龙珠配对的维京/泰坦你可以在地图中心与大多数红队一起暂时停留。无论是“药物战斗”还是“非药物战斗”，你都必须与其他队友保持配合 - 有效利用道具，搭配掉落的道具箱子，以及在中间的维修工具，还有在红色基地围栏后方掉落的双倍伤害和护甲箱子（根据该地图的团队模式）是获得最佳收益的关键。如果你快要被摧毁，请不要犹豫离开“运动场”，尽量在死前杀掉尽可能多的敌人，以免失去道具。不推荐道具不足或装备较弱的玩家。

这是一种策略，在这种策略中，你需要靠着正确的装备预测应该瞄准哪个红队。激光炮/雷暴炮/滑膛炮有时候冰风暴/火焰炮with蜂王/黄蜂轻甲is a perfect combination for this role. You have to have a fast mettle and fingers, much knowledge of the 统计数据 of the 红队' equipment. "Going All Out" Tactic

全力进攻是高原死亡混战中人们使用的最佳战术之一。基本上，它的运作方式是选择一个伤害高，但炮弹重装时间较慢的炮塔。青春中这样的炮塔的例子有：轰天炮,火箭炮and离子炮. Just grab a heavy 底盘, such as泰坦, and wreck everyone you see in your path.

Do note that if you want this tactic to work, you'll have to use your道具. You'll face a lot of people while doing this tactic, so changing 职务 every now and then is also a strategic recommendation.

在团队对战中，最大的目标是击杀敌人而不被杀死。这意味着需要有足够的磁力炮in your 团队 to fill up even the slightest of lost health. Most people tend to go all out in this 格式, trying to end the game as early and quickly as possible; but that doesn't work all the time. You need three basic things to win a 高原团队对战: cover, bigger 伤害 output than the enemy, and磁力炮. Although, as in 死亡混战, it is suggested to use one of the more efficient equipment. As with every good 团队 out there, your 团队 has to work together for a guaranteed win.
