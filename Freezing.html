Mechanics

冷冻is a 状态效果 that reduces 坦克速度 by up to 66% and 炮塔 rotation 速度 by 10%. The time of the effect depends on how much the 坦克 is frozen (from 1 to 10 seconds). The effect also nullifies all 伤害 boosting effects while applied to a 坦克.

When the 状态效果 is applied on a 坦克:

  • 速度 and 底盘转向灵活度 is reduced proportionally to the temperature decrease
    • The lowest temperature leads to a 66% reduction
  • 炮塔转向灵活度 is reduced proportionally to the temperature decrease
    • The lowest temperature leads to a 10% reduction
  • When the 坦克 is frozen, it returns to the normal temperature with the 速度 of 2.5% per tick (¼ of a second) or 10% per second
    • Not all ways of inflicting the 状态效果 guarantee the application of its 最高 effect
  • 伤害提升道具 of a frozen 玩家 is still active, but does not grant the 伤害 boost
  • Inflicting the 着火状态效果 shortens the time to increase temperature


Features of the 冷冻 status-effect:

  • Using the «维修工具» 道具 instantly removes the «冷冻» 状态效果 and makes youbrieflyimmune to further applications of the effect, as well as 其他 negative effects.
  • 冰风暴's 防护防御模块does notreduce the effects of 冷冻
  • 圣骑士’s过速装置does notprotect from the 冷冻 effect
  • The 冷冻 effect disables the 伤害 boost from the 肾上腺素装备改造


坦克 with «冰风暴» can be recognized by a 特殊 icon. If the enemy is affected by the 状态效果, the text prompt«冷冻»will appear above it.

Ways to apply the 状态效果

冰风暴

Direct attacks from 冰风暴 apply the «冷冻» 状态效果 on the enemy 坦克. In addition, there are装备改造in the game for 冰风暴 that alter the parameters of the effect.

过速装置十字军

冰风暴 opponents in the blast 半径 for 4 seconds and applies the 状态效果 护甲-Piercingon them.

过速装置独裁者

冰风暴 all 红队 in a 20 meter 半径 for 10 seconds.

装备改造

There are several 炮塔装备改造in the game that can inflict the «冰风暴» 状态效果.

手榴弹雪球

雪球 is an 活动手榴弹that can only be obtained during certain 活动. When it comes into contact with the ground or another static object, it explodes, dealing unblockable chaos 伤害and the 冷冻状态效果 to surrounding 红队.

防护 against the 状态效果

维修工具

Using a 维修工具 applies the«Polarizer» 状态效果 for a very short time, providing immunity from all 状态效果.

磁力炮

治疗 a teammate using 磁力炮 heats it up by 1 temperature per second if it's currently 冷冻.

剑圣

The 剑圣 is immune to all negative 状态效果.

过速装置猛犸象

Activating the 猛犸象过速装置 removes all negative 状态效果, and the 坦克 becomes temporarily immune to them.

抵抗

Immediately after respawning, all 坦克 with the «抵抗» 状态效果 are immune to all negative 状态效果.

装备改造

There are底盘装备改造in the game that protect the 坦克 from the 状态效果 «冷冻».

If a 坦克 has the «冰风暴 Immunity» 装备改造装备, attempts to 冰风暴 it will fail, and the text hint«Immunity»will appear briefly above the 坦克.
