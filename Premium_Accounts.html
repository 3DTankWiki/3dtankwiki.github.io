Ways of receiving

VIP 账号 may be received in several ways:

  • Purchase in the Game 商店
  • Activating a兑换码that contains VIP 账号
  • Receiving in certain金箱子在特殊活动中（例如：“愚人节”）

最简单的方法是在商店购买一个VIP账号。为此，你需要打开商店，该商店标有特殊图标。大厅，进入«VIP 账号»部分，然后选择所需的优惠。

特殊任务

With an active VIP 账号, you receive a 每周 bonus: 7普通 keys, which can be collected in the «特殊任务» tab.

  • The 任务 will first be available the day after activating the VIP 账号.
  • 新的任务总是在游戏服务器重启后的星期五发布。（每天发生在02:00 UTC)
  • The 任务 is only available if the VIP 账号 is active at the time of logging into the game
  • When the 服务器 restart, the old 任务 will disappear and a new one will be given. If you do not enter the game and do not click «Claim» on the 任务, the bonus will not be credited automatically
