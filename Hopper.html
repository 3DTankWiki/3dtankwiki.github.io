Interesting Facts

  • 霍珀 first appeared in the game on September 18th, 2020, as the exclusive property of lead developer Opex-Rah. The following day, it was released publicly for all 玩家 in a 特殊礼包. It is the second non-tracked 底盘 in the game.
  • The release of 霍珀 was quite a surprise, being first mentioned only a week prior to release, in视频周报 #270.
  • The 底盘 could only be received as a part of a 特殊 offer up until the 2nd of October, 2020, but with that day’s restart it became available for 水晶 for all 玩家.
  • 霍珀 can be controlled while in air! This feature is widely used in 跑酷.
