星级挑战

see also:星级挑战 Archive

A星级挑战is a limited time 活动 that consists of many 等级, where reaching each new 等级 grants the 玩家 additional 奖励. To progress through the 等级, 玩家 must earn a 特殊 currency, Stars, in the game. 星级挑战 are unlocked at 中士.

星级挑战 progress can be seen on the right side of the screen in the game's大厅— it shows the number of Stars earned, Stars needed to reach the next 等级, remaining time of the 星级挑战, and the next 等级奖励. When you reach a new 等级, you'll receive a notification. Afterward, you can collect the 奖励 by clicking on it.

解锁星级挑战奖励加成

A 解锁星级挑战奖励加成 is a 特殊通行证 that grants access to vip 奖励 for each 等级 of the 星级挑战. You can purchase a 解锁星级挑战奖励加成 at any time while the 星级挑战 is in progress.

Once you have purchased a 解锁星级挑战奖励加成, you will instantly unlock all additional 奖励 for the 等级 of the 星级挑战 that you have already completed. You can claim those 奖励 by manually clicking them.

You can navigate to the purchase of a 解锁星级挑战奖励加成 directly from the 星级挑战 window. The 通行证 is sold in the 商店 for700and is validfor only one current 星级挑战, after which it is withdrawn from the 车库. The 通行证 may also be sold as part of the 特殊 section in the 商店.

Stars

Main article:Economy

Starsare a 特殊 resource which can be earned during an ongoing 星级挑战 in匹配战斗 and by completing 任务. They accumulate throughout the whole 持续时间 of a 星级挑战, and their amount resets at the end of it.

战斗

Stars are earned in 战斗 depending on the results of each 玩家 at the end of the 战斗:

If the 战斗 ends early within three minutes, the 玩家 will not receive any stars. In a 战斗 lasting from 3 to 13 minutes, the 玩家 can get up to 3 stars. At the same time, the 最高 number of stars in a standard 战斗 is 3 — the 玩家 will receive them all if the 战斗 lasts more than 11 minutes. In a 战斗 lasting more than 13 minutes, for example, in 特殊模式, the 玩家 can get up to 8 or 12 stars.

精英通行证

The 精英通行证 is a 特殊 subscription which unlocks access to 奖励 for each 等级 of the separate 活动. These 等级 are completed by earning and buying the same stars with the same rules as in the usual 星级挑战. You can 购买 the 精英通行证 at any time during the 活动. It is unlocked at 上士.

Once you have purchased 精英通行证, you will instantly unlock all additional 奖励 for the 等级 of the 精英通行证 that you have already completed. You can claim those 奖励 by manually clicking them.

On the right of the game大厅you can check your current progress in each 活动: the number of stars earned, how many stars are required to complete the current 等级, when the 活动 ends, and what the 奖励 for the current 等级 are. Each time you complete a 等级 you will receive an in-game notification.

You can purchase the 精英通行证 right in the 活动 section. Unlike the 解锁星级挑战奖励加成, the 精英通行证's price varies per 活动. Like the 解锁星级挑战奖励加成, it is removed after each 活动 ends.

Attention!You must earn 50 Starsfor each new 等级 of the current 精英通行证.
