Guide to Siege

see also:Advice from experienced 玩家


Introduction

Siege is a new 模式 in 3D坦克 that is based on 控制点模式, yet a bit different and may be even harder than 控制点. Unlike 控制点, there's only One point on the entire 地图. Each 团队 needs to do its best in order to secure the point. Once a point is captured, a new point appears on the 地图. The 团队 that captures the most points wins!

Scoring

Point capturing

As explained above, in order to win, your 团队 has to be the 团队 with the most points captured. So obviously capturing points are worth the most points, right? It depends on how you look at it. For each point you and your 团队 captures, you receive 50 points. But, if the opponent 团队 is as good as your 团队, capturing points will not be an easy task. So you may not even capture points at all the whole round.

Destroying 坦克

As in every 其他 game 模式, destroying enemy 坦克 are worth points as well, 15 for a kill and 5 for an assist. This means that the 坦克手 that finished the kill off gets the most points, yet everyone else get 5 point even if they just hit the dead 坦克 once. The most important 坦克 to kill are those go are trying to capture the point, once they are down, aim for 露营者!


治疗

There is One more way to gain points in this 模式, using 磁力炮. 磁力炮 can be used to 治疗 in every 团队模式. The amount of points you gain is proportional to the actual amount of 伤害 healed. 磁力炮 are very useful in this 模式, since everyone is grouped in One place, both sides take a lot of 伤害, 磁力炮 can 帮助 your 团队 win a fight for the point and also prepare you to the next fight!

Offence & Defense

Have you ever heard the saying "The best defense is a good offense? so this is how it works in this game 模式. The point should always be attacked by your 团队. Even if you can't capture the point, prevent the enemy 团队 from doing so.

Now, to the most important thing, which combos are the best for this game 模式? A lot of changes were made in 3D坦克 and One of them was the the unique 过速装置 for each 底盘. In the past, most people would use 维京, and maybe 泰坦 once in a while. Nowadays, the meta is completely different.

底盘

For 底盘, I have a coherent opinion. The best Three 底盘 for this game 模式 are 黄蜂轻甲, 猎人中甲 & 泰坦. The 过速装置 of the 黄蜂轻甲 is perfect for this game 模式 as everyone is grouped in one place, the 黄蜂轻甲 can place the 炸弹 and kill pretty much everyone on the point, but there shouldn't be too many 黄蜂轻甲 because 黄蜂轻甲 is the 底盘 with the lowest amount of health which puts the 团队 in a big disadvantage.

With the recent changes, 猎人中甲 has became one, if not the best 底盘 in the game. 猎人中甲's 过速装置 can 冰风暴 you in the place while also neutralize you from your 道具. The 猎人中甲's 过速装置 can be a game changing move when the enemy 团队 is close to capturing the point.

Last but not least, 泰坦! The 泰坦's 过速装置 is very useful when your 团队 is close to capturing the point, the big shield bubble that the 泰坦 creates prevent the enemy 团队 from damaging you badly, most of their shots are useless. Besides that, 泰坦 has lots of health which is a big bonus when clashing the enemy 团队 on the capture point.

炮塔

For 炮塔, I'd say it depends on the 地图 and the 防护 the enemy 坦克 have, but close 射程炮塔 like 冰风暴 & 火焰炮 will always be effective near the point since, once again, everyone is grouped and the 伤害 these Two 炮塔 can deal to a few 玩家 at once is deadly. On top of that, there should always be a 玩家 or Two camping with long 射程炮塔 - 镭射炮 or 激光炮 work fairly well, but 马格南 and 电磁炮 are generally the best due to their large 溅射伤害.


Camping

Even though your 几率 to win are higher when there are more 玩家 in the capture point, your 团队 should have at least one 露营者, a 玩家 who hides and shot enemy 坦克 from far away, with 镭射炮 for example. The 原因 having a 露营者 will benefit your 团队 is, when a 玩家 hits the enemy 团队 before they even reach the capture point, the 玩家 either kills them or forces them to use a 维修工具. Either way your 团队 already have advantage
