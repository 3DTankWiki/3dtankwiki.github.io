有效地在边塞角斗场中游戏指南

see also:地图指南


关于边塞角斗场


概述： Parma is a medium sized map with an open design and small but frequent areas of cover. The relatively symmetrical layout of the map makes it a popular choice, particularly for XP format. Parma battles are fairly rare at low ranks, however they’re more popular at high ranks, mainly in the form of XP battles.

战斗模式:死亡混战, 团队对战, 互夺军旗, 控制点

大小: 10v10, 20 Players

战术

死亡混战/团队对战

露营

Despite the open design of Parma, the small but frequent areas of cover make camping an effective strategy. The raised area around the edge of the map provides multiple areas of cover, along with views across the entire map, including the enemy’s spawn in many cases. Mid to long range turrets such as 雷暴炮,马格南和激光炮建议允许从地图各处进行有效的狙击。短射程炮塔在边塞角斗场的绝大多数遭遇中可能会无效。

地图两侧的小墙段通常是露营时最受欢迎的选择。这些地方是有效的，尽管建议使用小巧轻便的底盘，以确保底盘的边缘不会从墙后突出。一个精确的敌军狙击手将能够利用这一点。

此外，地图的每个角落都有四个塔。这些塔格外有用，因为如果你能躲在正确的塔后面，你将可以沿着敌人出生区域的长度看到。这里通常会排成一排几个坦克，激光炮如果你的攻击时机把握得当，这是一种毁灭性的武器。道具例如速度提升可以用来快速进出掩护区域，同时躲避敌方狙击手。伤害提升在对手有机会反击之前，快速消灭他们也可能是非常有用的。

近距离战斗

The layout of Parma makes any form of close quarters combat a difficult practice, however there are numerous walls in the middle area of the map, which make a close-ranged assault possible if you make use of the cover to evade snipers and sneak up on your enemies from below. If you can cross the map, and reach the ramps at the base of their spawn area, you will likely guarantee yourself several kills if you make use of supplies and surprise the enemy as they respawn.

互夺军旗

Defense

In Parma, the opposing team’s base areas are symmetrical meaning the same attack/defense strategies apply to both the red and blue teams in CTF.

防御的关键在于分散并利用地图边缘的墙体部分。太多时候，多个玩家会试图在塔附近直接围绕他们的旗子进行黑色营地。这不仅减少了机动性，还使你容易被消灭。激光炮 who approach from the sides. If the defenders are spread out along the wall sections, they will have several angles of sight across to the enemy’s side of the map. This often means that an enemy will reverse behind a wall to evade fire, but then protrude from the other side of the wall and make themselves visible to one of your teammates. The particularly small sections of wall in Parma make this a frequent occurrence, especially if the enemy is using a larger hull.

如在《死亡混战/团队对战》部分的‘露营’中提到的，建议使用射程更长的炮塔进行防守，而较小的底盘可以用于躲藏在掩体后，尽管这是否抵消了护甲少的缺点还值得讨论。

Attack

在边塞角斗场进攻可能是一个困难的练习。广阔的开放宇宙的地图通常会导致大部分团队停留在一个区域，横跨地图进行狙击。这意味着当偶尔有玩家尝试协调进攻时，他们会在接近之前容易被消灭。最重要的战术是作为一个小组进行攻击。守卫者的高位会保证你的队友在你前进时被击倒，但如果几个玩家同时前进，抓住旗子的机会就会出现。带着旗子最快的逃生路线是直接穿过地图的中间。这看起来可能像是自杀，因为红队会从他们的出生点轻松看见你，然而，大多数有用的道具掉落区域恰好也位于地图的中心，包括维修工具此外，你的团队的守卫者应该能够向前推进并提供支持，以防你在逃脱时被消灭。

通常情况下，建议使用轻底盘以有效攻击。道具如速度提升和护甲提升 can also be useful in extending your survival.

控制点

Parma CP is made up of five control points; quite a large number considering the layout of the map. The positioning of these points means that at the beginning of the battle, points C and E will likely go to the red team, with points B and D going to the blue team. This leaves point A to be contested. Due to this, the centre of Parma is usually the point at which the majority of conflict will be taking place between the two teams. As is usual with CP, short or mid-range turrets along with generous supply usage and medium or heavy hulls is likely to be the best way to fight for sought-after control points. An alternative tactic would be to try and flank around the combat in the centre of the map, and go for the points closer to the enemy team’s spawn area. Enemies here will likely be in a hurry to travel back to the centre of the map, and with some stealth, it may be possible to capture one of their points and gain your team the upper hand.
