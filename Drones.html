上将 Information

Nearly all 无人机 are sold for水晶在车库，在«无人机»选项卡。他们没有修改，但是它们的参数可能是升级。

Every 无人机 except for 布鲁特斯 has its own set of unique characteristics.

每个无人机都有一个唯一的模型，因此可以将其与其他区分开。而且，他们继承了迷彩of their “owner”. It is impossible to destroy a 无人机 using 坦克 equipment.

Usage in 战斗

To use a 无人机, it must be 装备 in the 车库, just like 其他 equipment. Only one 无人机 can be 装备 on a 坦克. 无人机 appears above a 坦克 in 战斗 and always 关注 it. The presence of a 无人机 above a 坦克 means that it is ready and can be used at any moment.

无人机 effects automatically work when a 道具 is activated or adrop 箱子is picked up (except for 危机 and 危机 XT).


注意力！独裁者's过速装置doesn't give extra 道具 boosts if you already have 机械师, 守卫者, etc. 装备.

支持蓝队的其他声誉点

机械师无人机 spreads its effect on teammates in a 半径 and earns you声誉点if you share a 维修工具 with at least one teammate, even if the 维修工具 didn't benefit them.

道具冷却 time reduction

All 无人机 (except for 布鲁特斯) can decrease the 道具冷却 time written in its characteristics. For active 无人机, it influences only one 道具, but for 亥伯龙神 and 危机, all 道具冷却 times are decreased.

额外的工具使用

All 无人机 except for 布鲁特斯 and 神谕者 have an increased 道具 usage. 无人机 that work from activating a 道具 or picking up adrop 箱子(except for 危机 and 危机 XT) also use a few units of the 道具 from 玩家’s reserve mentioned in the characteristics.

无人机 Classification

每个无人机属于三个专业课程之一：

  • 被动：不仅在某些情况下，一直在工作，而且在后台工作。
  • Active: These 无人机 are triggered only when activating a specific 道具 or picking up the respective 道具箱子 and they provide an additional 道具 boost to the 道具, based on the 无人机's characteristic.
  • Removed - these 无人机 were removed in a 少校 game update, and most were converted into 装备改造 that can be applied to your 底盘.

Removed 无人机

前补丁＃721on 13th April 2023, 10 more 无人机 with various 机械师 existed in the game. All these 无人机 were removed from the game to simplify the 无人机 system. 玩家 received水晶 compensationfor these 无人机. In addition to these 无人机, 电池道具 was removed from the game too. It was required to use 无人机, and was consumed automatically by them.

机械师 of 5 of these 无人机 now exists in the form of底盘装备改造: «工程师», «驱动器», «救生员», «工兵» and «爆破手».
