Drones

General Information

Nearly all drones are sold for Crystals in the Garage, in the «Drones» tab. They don’t have modifications, but their parameters can be upgraded.

Every drone except for Brutus has its own set of unique characteristics.

Every drone has a unique model so it can be distinguished from others. Moreover, they inherit the paint of their “owner”. It is impossible to destroy a drone using tank equipment.

Usage in Battles

To use a drone, it must be equipped in the garage, just like other equipment. Only one drone can be equipped on a tank. Drone appears above a tank in battle and always follows it. The presence of a drone above a tank means that it is ready and can be used at any moment.

Drone effects automatically work when a supply is activated or a drop box is picked up (except for Crisis and Crisis XT).


Attention! Dictator's overdrive doesn't give extra supply boosts if you already have Mechanic, Defender, etc. equipped.


Additional Mechanics

Some drones have one or more additional work mechanics:

Supply Boost

The upgrading level of drones affects the boost of the «Boosted Damage» and «Boosted Armor» supplies.

  • Boosted Armor supply's effect strength: (50 → 100), %
  • Boosted Damage supply's effect increase: (50 → 100), %

Additional reputation points for supporting allies

Mechanic drone spreads its effect on teammates in a radius and earns you reputation points if you share a repair kit with at least one teammate, even if the repair kit didn't benefit them.

Supply cooldown time reduction

All drones (except for Brutus) can decrease the supply cooldown time written in its characteristics. For active drones, it influences only one supply, but for Hyperion and Crisis, all supply cooldown times are decreased.

Extra supply usage

All drones except for Brutus and Oracle have an increased supply usage. Drones that work from activating a supply or picking up a drop box (except for Crisis and Crisis XT) also use a few units of the supply from player’s reserve mentioned in the characteristics.

Drone Classification

Each drone belongs to one of three specialization classes:

  • Passive: Works all the time, and in the background, not just in certain cases.
  • Active: These drones are triggered only when activating a specific supply or picking up the respective supply box and they provide an additional supply boost to the supply, based on the drone's characteristic.
  • Removed - these drones were removed in a major game update, and most were converted into augments that can be applied to your hull.

Removed Drones

Before Patch #721 on 13th April 2023, 10 more drones with various mechanics existed in the game. All these drones were removed from the game to simplify the drones system. Players received crystal compensation for these drones. In addition to these drones, battery supply was removed from the game too. It was required to use drones, and was consumed automatically by them.

Mechanics of 5 of these drones now exists in the form of hull augments: «Engineer», «Driver», «Lifeguard», «Miner» and «Blaster».
