A Guide to 手榴弹

手榴弹 are a unique道具that 允许 additional combat potential when facing 小组 of 红队 or single targets at short 射程. By default, theF钥匙用于扔他们。

This article covers the function of 手榴弹 in the game, as well as some tactics to add a new, challenging aspect to the game.

History

手榴弹 were released on September 8th, 2023, in a unique gamemode .

功能

手榴弹 are 特殊道具that dealunblockable伤害 to anyone hit. They can destroy地雷and change targets of enemy aim. They ignore all moving objects and bounce off of 地图 geometry.

提示

  • The trajectory of a 手榴弹 is influenced by your 坦克's 速度 when you throw it. To hit farther targets, begin accelerating before holdingFfor a more precise throw.
  • Avoid using 手榴弹 at melee-射程, as they deal heavy self-伤害.
  • 手榴弹可以被发射，所以一定要明智地使用它们来干扰敌人的瞄准 - 尝试在与炮塔的战斗中把一些扔在你面前，而不采用穿透机制。雷暴炮.
  • In addition to this, you can 射击 your own 手榴弹. The same tips apply here, but if you find your 手榴弹 just about to miss a target, a well-timed shot from your own 坦克 can at least deal some 溅射伤害.


对抗近战-射程炮塔

火焰炮, 冰风暴, & 磁力炮

Avoid using 手榴弹 when these 炮塔 are close enough to 伤害 you, unless you feel lucky enough to not die of 溅射伤害 or any of the 炮塔伤害 after your 坦克 is softened by the 手榴弹.

特斯拉

在使用手榴弹对抗特斯拉时，保持在近战炮塔射程之外。由于炮塔的标准球闪电能力移动速度慢，你可以在更远的射程上更快地打击它们。你的手榴弹速度要快得多，影响范围也更大.

反短射程炮塔

滑膛炮

把手榴弹扔到你身后，然后跑开。如果他们最终击中手榴弹，他们将受到巨大的伤害，这将让你可以把敌人当早餐吃。在更远的射程内，当他们受到伤害衰减时，直接把手榴弹扔向他们。

离子炮 and 火龙珠

手榴弹 can 禁止 these 炮塔' shots, but do mind the 伤害 from them afterward. It's best to pick them off at mid-射程 to not take 伤害.

Against Medium-射程炮塔

轰天炮

Between shot 冷却, try tossing a 手榴弹 so that it will be in front of your 坦克 when the enemy is about to fire. This gives you a second 几率 at 瞄准 at your target, and can sway the outcome of a 城镇角斗场 between 坦克.

雷暴炮 and 火箭炮

Try to get them to 射击 your 手榴弹. With 火箭炮, however, try throwing your 手榴弹 when you see 齐射 rockets heading towards you, and then run away.

极速炮

你可以通过向极速炮用户扔手榴弹来迷惑他们，因为他们的炮塔很可能会锁定手榴弹。这使你能够至少多打一枪，这可能会大幅改变局势。

Against Long-射程炮塔

马格南

Only use 手榴弹 when they are close enough to accidentally 射击 themselves with their 炮塔. 马格南 is a dangerous 炮塔 that should not be trifled with, unless that shot is directed towards their own 坦克.

激光炮

Hide.

镭射炮

Throw a 手榴弹 outside of their line of sight if they are scoped to throw off their aim and deal some 伤害. If you're skilled enough, try throwing a 手榴弹 to 禁止 a scoped shot.

电磁炮 & 蝎子

Their 齐射 are very dangerous. Throw 手榴弹 at them from different angles so as to not immediately get locked on and shot at. You can throw off their aim by hitting them with a 手榴弹 or by throwing one in the path of their 炮塔 aim.
