终结者

Long 射程炮塔. Holding down the 射击 button will launch a barrage of guided missiles to attack the target.

Super heavy 炮塔 designed specifically for the “剑圣” 底盘. The two 激光炮炮塔 with increased rate of fire are accompanied by heavy close-射程 rocket launchers.

It is not possible to use it with 其他底盘. According to rumors, a shot can blow the 炮塔 off the 坦克.

Description in 车库

终结者 is a 特殊炮塔 that can fire in two different 模式:

  • short press of the 射击 button will 射击 one of two paired激光炮by turns, which have a higher rate of fire then the standard 激光炮;
  • holding the 射击 button will create a laser pointer like that of the火箭炮炮塔 and after locking onto a target the 炮塔射击 six homing rockets. Shooting single rockets isn’t possible.

This 炮塔 has a unique type of 伤害 , that can’t be lowered by any 防御模块.

This unique 底盘 is impossible to purchase or permanently obtain in any 其他 way. This armament with the剑圣底盘 as a part of the “剑圣” 坦克 is intended only for“剑圣” 战斗模式. There is one exception - unique 坦克 can be used by developers of the game for testing and organizing 活动.

玩家' opinions
Pros
Incredibly 高伤害 with a quick reload. The 激光炮 can 伤害坦克 through 其他坦克, thanks to the 高 piercing 伤害. Also features a very 高冲击力 and an unlimited 射程. The alternative 射击模式 is a volley of homing missiles. They can inflict 溅射伤害 on multiple 红队 at once or to 伤害 an enemy out of sight.
Cons
Delay before the shot. In the volley 射击模式, a target must remain in the sight of the laser until the targeting reticle is fully locked. The rockets also risk self-inflicting massive 溅射伤害.

防御模块

Main article:防御模块

Like雪人,番茄炮, andB0-NK, there is no 防御模块 that provides 防护 from 终结者's 普通 (non-暴击) hits.

犰狳

«犰狳» 防御模块, Which protects the 坦克 from the 暴击 hit of the 终结者炮塔.

Spectrum

Unique 防御模块, Which protects the 坦克 from all standard 炮塔.

Table of Characteristics
  • 普通伤害 (hp)— The 伤害 dealt to an enemy 坦克 per one shot of the 激光炮 or one rocket within the 半径 of max 伤害.
  • 暴击伤害 (hp)— The 伤害 dealt to an enemy 坦克 per one 暴击 shot of the 激光炮.
  • Charge time (sec)— The amount of time in seconds it takes for the weapon to fully charge after 射击.
  • Shot delay time (sec)— The time in seconds required for 激光炮 to initiate projectile launch after hitting 宇宙.
  • Penetrating 启动加速度 (%)— The percentage of 伤害 that a 坦克 in line behind the first receives upon being hit. The 伤害 from the first 坦克 to be struck is multiplied by the percentage, resulting in the approximate value of the 伤害 to the second. If there is a third 坦克 behind the second, that value will be multiplied by the same percentage, and the result of that number will be the 伤害 dealt to the third 坦克, and so on.
  • Max 暴击几率 (%)- The highest possible 几率 of a 暴击 hit on a shot.
  • 冲击力— The physical impact of a 炮塔's projectile on enemy 坦克 upon contacting them.
  • Explosion impact— The physical impact on enemy 坦克 in the 伤害半径 from rocket's impact point.
  • 后坐力— The physical impact of the 炮塔 upon its own 坦克 when shooting.
  • 炮弹重装时间 (sec)— The 暂停 required between shots to prepare the rockets for launch. This time remains the same regardless of whether a single rocket or a 齐射 was fired last.
  • 瞄准 time (sec)— The amount of time required for the targeting laser to be pointed at another 坦克 in order to lock on and launch a full 齐射 of missiles. If the circular green reticle disappears, but the red, 箱子-like reticle stays active, the 瞄准 time is paused. If the red, 箱子-like reticle disappears, then the target is lost and the 瞄准 time resets.
  • 瞄准 recovery time (sec)— The amount of time, if the circular green reticle is removed due to losing sight of a target, that the second red reticle will remain on-screen to maintain lock-on progress.
  • 射程 (m)— The 最高 distance that the rocket can reach. Rockets explode when they reach their 最高射程.
  • 最小弹丸速度 (米/秒)— The initial, slow 速度 which a rocked has right at the moment it is launched. This increases rapidly and steadily after being fired until it reaches the 最高 projectile 速度.
  • Shell acceleration time (sec)— The amount of time in seconds it takes to reach the max projectile 速度 from the min projectile 速度 (i.e. the moment it launches).
  • Max projectile 速度— 发射火箭可以达到的最高速度。
  • Shell angular velocity (deg/sec)— This is the 转向灵活度 at which the 齐射 rockets are able to change direction in order to keep pointed on the target they were locked onto.
  • Rockets per 齐射—— 一次齐射可以发射的火箭数量。
  • 暂停在齐射火箭之间— The required time between each rocket's launch to eject another during a full 齐射 launch.
  • Projectile 半径 (m)— The invariable 半径 of the rocket from its core to its outer surface.
  • Max 伤害半径 (m)— The distance from the projectile's impact point at which enemy receives 最高伤害. If the distance to the target is greater, the 伤害 constantly decreases until the 最低伤害半径.
  • Average 溅射伤害半径 (m)每次射击的平均半径为溅射伤害。
  • 平均溅射伤害 (%)— The percentage of normal 伤害 dealt when a 坦克 is within the 最高伤害半径 but does not hit the projectile.
  • 半径 of 最低溅射伤害 (m)— 敌人收到最低溅射伤害的投射物冲击点的距离。如果冲击点到目标的距离更大，将不会造成溅射伤害。
  • Weak 伤害溅射 (%)— the % or normal 伤害 dealt at a distance from the projectile's impact point, equal to 最低伤害半径.
  • 旋转速度 (度/秒)— This is the 最快速度 that the 炮塔 can rotate at.
  • Rotation acceleration (deg/sec²)—— 炮塔达到其最高旋转速度的加速度。
  • 自动瞄准向上 (度)— The angle 射程 that auto-aim will effect if a target is above the 炮塔's horizontal plane.
  • Auto-aim downwards (deg)— The angle 射程 that auto-aim will effect if a target is below the 炮塔's horizontal plane.


Interesting Facts

  • 终结者 was added into the game on the 20th of July, 2018, when the “Solo 剑圣” 模式 was added.
