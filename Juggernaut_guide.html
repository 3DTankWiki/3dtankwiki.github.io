In 团队剑圣, you can also play the same as you would in Solo 剑圣, but you do have teammates that can 帮助 you manage a safe amount of HP by destroying any 红队 coming to destroy you. Tips and Strategies

The 剑圣底盘 has a unique 过速装置, as mentioned earlier, which reloads approximately every 40 seconds. 这很重要，因为你必须非常有策略地使用它，尽量不要在每次受到一些伤害（大约四分之一的生命值）时使用，而是尽量在你的生命值低于一半时使用，或者当有某种激光试图锁定你时使用（如果你的生命值低于满值，它们可能会一击致命）。Likewise, if you find yourself close enough or have the opportunity to move towards some 红队 or even the enemy 团队’s 剑圣, you can use the 过速装置 to try and flip them! What you want to do is, as soon as the enemy 剑圣 uses their 过速装置, check the percentage of your 过速装置 (or time it with seconds, but this is much harder) when their 过速装置 is gone check to time how much longer your 过速装置 will take to recharge, and how long you have until the opponent has a recharged 过速装置 again. 例如，如果对方的剑圣在我的过速装置充满70%时使用他们的过速装置，而我则达到100%，我知道对方的过速装置只会有30%，因为两个过速装置的充电速度是一样的。This is very useful to know so you can calculate if you are in danger of dying or not, then lock on to them for the full 火箭炮齐射 and finish them off with a few 激光炮 shots.
