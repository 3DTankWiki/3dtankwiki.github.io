Mechanics

眩晕— is a 状态效果 that immobilizes the 坦克 for a few seconds and also prevents it from 射击, activating道具and过速装置, or self-destructing.

When applied to the 坦克状态效果:

  • Unable to move, turn the 底盘 and self-destruct
  • Unable to turn the 炮塔, 射击 and reload
  • Unable to activate道具,手榴弹and 过速装置.
    • The 持续时间 of the 状态效果 is excellent for each method of its application


Features of the «眩晕» 状态效果::

  • Active 道具 are not deactivated, and 箱子 from the sky (including a金箱子) can still be taken if the 坦克 is in the drop location at the time of the 眩晕
  • Levitating 坦克 still retain their inertia and may «fly» some more distance after deprivation
  • After the 状态效果 is applied the capture of points in控制点and围城大战模式 continues, and the 旗子 picked up in互夺军旗and突袭模式 is not reset
  • 过速装置 charge under 眩晕 continues to accumulate


A 坦克 with «眩晕» can be recognized by a 特殊 icon. If the enemy is affected by the 状态效果, the text prompt«眩晕»appears above it.

Ways to apply the 状态效果

过速装置黄蜂轻甲

This small 底盘's stinger is a devastating N2 炸弹. In addition to a brief电磁脉冲 , the 炸弹眩晕 andBurns all 坦克 caught within its blast 半径. Additionally, 黄蜂轻甲's 炸弹 jams all 红队 that are in a direct line from the 炸弹 (not shielded by any buildings or landscape features) and within 60 meters for 9 seconds.

过速装置霍珀

霍珀过速装置 can 眩晕 and dealchaos 伤害to nearby 红队.

过速装置猎人中甲

猎人中甲's 过速装置眩晕 all 红队 within a certain 半径 for a few seconds and also applies the 状态效果«电磁脉冲».

装备改造

There are several炮塔防御模块in the game that can inflict the «眩晕» 状态效果.

Protecting against 状态效果

剑圣

The 剑圣 is immune to all negative 状态效果.

过速装置猛犸象

Activating 猛犸象过速装置 removes all negative 状态效果, and the 坦克 becomes temporarily immune to them.

过速装置圣骑士

Activating the 圣骑士过速装置 removes all negative 状态效果 and the 坦克 becomes temporarily immune to them.

Polarization

Polarization effect is applied when using the维修工具, as well as when activating the 圣骑士's过速装置or the 独裁者's 过速装置.

抵抗

Immediately after respawn all 坦克 with the «抵抗» 状态效果 are immune to negative 状态效果.

装备改造

There is a底盘装备改造in the game that protects the 坦克 from «眩晕».

If you try to apply the 眩晕状态效果 on an opponent with the 装备改造 «Immunity from 眩晕», the text prompt«Immunity»appears above them briefly.
