坦克战争

另见:战争和星级挑战


坦克战争是一场战争这场战争发生在2019年9月1日至29日，非常类似于Demolition 战争because there were no 团队 and 玩家 had to perform various tasks/任务.

要参与战争，必须购买特殊迷彩。坦克 1.0. This was available for purchase from the 1st of September in the 商店 for 10并一直持续到战争结束。

Outcome

之前的战争是基于哪个小组收集了最多的星星。这个概念在这里没有被遵循。这场战争的目标是完成三项，或理想情况下四项可以在上找到的任务。specific page. These tasks could only be done in 匹配战斗.

奖品

There were four "tasks" and there was a small prize for each completed task. Of the four tasks, one was a 特殊 task. If you performed all three normal tasks, you get the unique 迷彩坦克 2.0. But if you also completed the fourth task, you got 300坦克币. Thus, there were a total of five prizes.
