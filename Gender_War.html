Gender 战争

see also:战争and星级挑战


The Gender 战争 was a战争that took place from March 2nd to Augusth 28th, 2019. The goal of the participants in this 战争 was to collect as many stars as possible in their own faction - which was randomly assigned. The faction that has collected the most stars at the end wins.

To participate in this 战争, you had to purchase one of the faction 迷彩 for 5 000 水晶. The 迷彩 automatically assigned the 玩家 to the chosen factions. The 迷彩 was purchasable from the 2nd of March and remained available for purchase until 28th of August.

The factions

There were 2 factions -GentlemanandLady

Outcome

As mentioned above, the task of participants was to collect stars in 匹配战斗. The faction that had the most stars when the 战争 ended won. In the end, theLadyfaction won the 战争, earning 417 stars on average.

Prizes

Everyone who bought the faction 迷彩 was able to choose one of the given animated 迷彩. After the contest ended all of those 玩家 who were in the winning faction received the 迷彩 what he/she selected.
