Ways to get 皮肤:

It isimpossibleto 购买皮肤 for 水晶.


皮肤can be 装备 in the车库: select the desired 炮塔 or 底盘 and choose the desired 皮肤.

History of the appearance of 皮肤

轰天炮 XT

主'article:轰天炮 XT

轰天炮 XT was added into the 商店 on February 14th, 2011 and was removed from the 商店 and 车库 of 坦克手们 who bought it on February 24th, 2011. 轰天炮 XT was different due to upgraded characteristics: fire rate and 转向灵活度 were equal to 轰天炮 M3’s, and 伤害 was equal to 雷暴炮 M3’s.

蜂王和激光炮 XT 首次出现在

Unique 蜂王 and 激光炮 XT (that were available in all modifications from M0 to M3, depending on the 等级) were available for rent for a small period of time during the New Year 2014-2015. They had upgraded characteristics and a unique appearance. At first, the price of rent was quite 高 (half a million 水晶 for an M3 套件), but it was lowered every day up to the last day of the 活动 - on the last day of celebration the prices were 100 times lower than when the sales began. On January 12th, 2015 the rent 持续时间 was over and New Year 精英 items were removed from the 商店 and 车库.

М0 М1 М2 М3

Second appearance of 蜂王 and 激光炮 XT.

Six months later, on July 14, 2015, 蜂王 and 激光炮 XT were returned to the game (only in the M3 modification and with standard characteristics) to become 珍奇 unique prizes for大元帅(and later传奇) 为完成每日任务。第一个收集到两个XT物品的幸运人是德国玩家I.ReSpAwN。

Appearance of 雷暴炮, 火焰炮, 维京, and 黄蜂轻甲 XT.

On September 10, 2015, XT versions of 雷暴炮, 火焰炮, 维京, and 黄蜂轻甲 were added to the game (also only in the M3 modification). Like 蜂王 with 激光炮, these items have only visual differences from the standard versions and at that time could only be obtained for completing 每日任务.

获得购买 XT 的能力的出现

On October 21, vip 通行证 was introduced into the game and the ability to directly purchase items from the XT line was unlocked for its owners. At the same time, the possibility of obtaining XT items through 每日任务 was still there. Later, this option was removed from the game.

Appearance of 极速炮 and 猛犸象 XT

On December 25th, 2015, with the beginning of the New Year 活动, the new XT combination was added to 任务 and 车库 - 极速炮 and 猛犸象 XT.

Separation of XT 皮肤 and equipment

On June 7th, 2018, an update was released, where 炮塔 and 底盘 XT were no longer separate items in the 车库: they were separated into standard equipment and appearance 选项 - 皮肤. This allowed the continuation of the XT series and the creation of completely new 皮肤.

Continuation of the "golden" XT-series

On August 10th, 2018, the new long awaited XT 皮肤 was added into the game - 轰天炮. It could be received by purchasing a 特殊 offer in the 商店 and from 幸运礼盒. The addition of the 轰天炮炮塔 started the work on the logical completion of the XT series of 皮肤: as time went by, every 炮塔 and 底盘 had one.

遗产皮肤

维京 and 雷暴炮 were the first to get 遗产皮肤. They could be received by getting into TOP-10 of your faction by earning stars in the战争.统治. 遗产 series were continued in the last 奖励 of 星级挑战 №2 and №3, in which 玩家 could receive 激光炮 and 蜂王遗产 accordingly.

After that, 火焰炮 DC was available for purchase only once: on Black Friday November 23rd, 2018. New 皮肤 of this series were added for the celebration of Halloween in 2021, when a new version of the 皮肤 for 火焰炮 and 黄蜂轻甲 was added, and for the celebration of Halloween in 2022 皮肤, for which 维京 and 滑膛炮 were added.
