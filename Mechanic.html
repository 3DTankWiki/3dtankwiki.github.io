车库 description

Boosts the «维修工具» 道具's effect.

This 无人机 consumes additional units of the «维修工具» 道具. It improves the repairing ability of your 坦克. It decreases 冷却 time of the «维修工具» 道具. It also lets you repair teammates nearby and gives you extra points for that. Upgrading this 无人机 boosts the effect of «伤害提升» and «护甲提升» 道具.

Everyone who considers it their duty to support 其他 in 战斗 deserves great respect.

Basic effects

  • «护甲提升» 道具's effect strength:(50 →100), %
  • «伤害提升» 道具's effect increase:(50 →100), %

The 升级 table can be found on the无人机page

Effects

  • Reload:1 sec
  • Bonus HP:(1000 →3000), hp
  • 治疗半径:(20 →30), m
  • Reduction of 维修工具冷却:(10 →20), sec
  • 维修工具 consumed: +2
  • Reputation points gained if you share 维修工具:4

Pros and Cons

Boosted immediate 治疗 is useful when 坦克 is continuously taking 伤害 (In such situation 治疗 over time disappears)
Works well in situations where a lot of teammates are grouped at one place especially in围城大战or in the defending 团队 in突袭and the 无人机 user get 4 additional reputation points for it
允许 for more frequent use of the 维修工具
Consumes维修工具道具 rapidly
