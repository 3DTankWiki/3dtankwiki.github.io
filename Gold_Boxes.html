战斗中的金箱子

Working mechanics

任何金箱子会在通知出现在屏幕上10秒后掉落在地图上，除了“愚人节”的庆祝活动，当时金箱子会在通知出现后立即掉落。金箱子掉落后，除非被捡起或战斗结束，否则不会消失。它的掉落动态区域显示在地图 from the moment when a notification appears.

金箱子掉落条件对于匹配和PRO战斗是不同的，但在任何战斗中，玩家都可以通过使用“金箱子”掉落金箱子。道具.

金箱子可以在任何地方掉落匹配 battle starting from 二级军士长, if there are more than 40 seconds until it is over.

在一些金箱子掉落动感区域时，金箱子可能不会掉落，而是直接出现在地面上。通常这种情况发生在有障碍物阻挡时。

在跑酷战斗中，金箱子无法自动掉落，因为战斗奖励没有增加。

如何获得金箱子道具

Gold box supplies can be purchased in the 车库 in 4 packages for 红宝石来自 五级准尉一个金箱子费用150此外，激活后可以获得金箱子兑换码 or in official contests/events. Gold boxes are added as a separate supply in the garage and can be dropped in any battle by pressing the 6钥匙。玩家掉落的金箱子总是会给予1 000 and no experience points.

What gold boxes are for

金箱子可以用于很多目的。它们可以在匹配战斗中被丢弃，分散玩家对某个目标的注意力。此外，它们还可以用于赠送给其他玩家一些东西。水晶在特殊场合，例如庆祝好友达到新等级或作为一种友好的行为，金箱子常被用来掉落给自己，并在车库购买后获得额外水晶。

Keys in battle

This container is a special gold box that drops randomly instead of a standard gold box, only in matchmaking battles. Player who catches it get 1 普通钥匙 and 1000 经验值而不是标准1 000 from a gold box. Container can’t be dropped by a player; instead it is randomly dropped by the server. Notification for a container is the same as for a standard gold box. The chance for a container to drop instead of a gold box is not disclosed by the developers.

Megagolds


水晶 Megagold

水晶 Megagold是一个特殊的金箱子，奖励坦克手以10 000 and 5000 经验值. 这个箱子在任何匹配战斗中都可以获得，但仅在某些节庆活动期间可用。水晶megagold替代标准gold掉落的几率未知，因为它掉落的几率非常低。


红宝石 Megagold


红宝石 Megagold

Ruby Megagold与水晶megagold相同，但拾取它会带来10和1000 经验值. This box is available in any 匹配 battle, but only during certain festive activities.




Meteorite


Meteorites are a special mechanic of a gold box dropping: a certain object falls from the sky, collides with the ground and explodes, and in the place of the fall a gold box appears. This unique gold box can only be seen during special festive activities like Cosmonautics Day.

The explosion also deals a lot of damage to players nearby, usually destroying them.

Another variant of the meteorite gold box is a Christmas tree, which falls on the map in the same way. This gold box only appears for the celebration of New Year.

History

The History of the Gold Box

Gold Box History

社区首先在2009年12月3日听说了一个不寻常的箱子出现，箱子上有水晶。据3D坦克视频周报报道，在多个地图上，当战斗奖励达到一定数量的水晶时，一个金箱子将会出现。第一个抓住金箱子的坦克手是昵称为Noosya的玩家——历史必须铭记它的英雄！金箱子立刻成为了主要讨论的话题。聊天和在论坛. Day and night tankers shared their opinions, hypothesizing about certain patterns. The luckiest players shared the first 截图作为证明，箱子被捕获。随着时间的推移，人们意识到了他们闹翻的地点和时间。

最初，箱子里包含1000水晶。在经济更新之前，这是一笔可观的数量（在当前经济系统中价值10000水晶），尤其对于低等级玩家而言，那些成功获得金箱子的玩家非常开心。然而，也有玩家，大多数是...Buyer，谁希望箱子的价值被降低。主要论点是，在当时的游戏经济中，1000水晶是很多，所以为什么一个人要购买水晶，如果他或她可以直接捉到金箱子？

这种情况持续了整整一个星期，直到2009年12月11日，开发者将金箱子的价值降低到了100水晶。

Back then, the Gold box would fall at a random time within a few minutes of the fund reaching a multiple of 700 crystals. However, after Update 78, the direct link to that specific fund value was removed and up until now, the Gold Box can fall at any point during the game, whenever the battle fund increases by at least 1 crystal. It was first announced in 视频周报 #62. Some players are not too happy with the fact that the Gold Box may not fall at all, but may fall several times in a short period of time. However, the developers should not be blamed for the "rigged" mechanism of the Gold Box dropping out - the drop chance is completely random.

Container history

Containers first appeared during the 2017 Black Friday celebrations, after which they were removed when the sale ended. Tankers liked them so much that after the New Year holidays ended on January 9, Containers were added to the game permanently.

金箱子猎人中甲

Within a few months of its first appearance, almost every player knew where and when a Gold Box would drop. The game seemed to have changed. Battles changed completely when their fund got close to 700 crystals. Many players hoping to catch a Gold Box would start to cluster around the drop zone. In all maps, the spot used to be near the center of the map. Teams would fight to control that particular area to increase their chances of getting 100 crystals. Activating all 道具在掉落之前的几分钟是一个正常的程序。有些人会争辩说这没有意义，因为这种做法通常会让坦克手们花费超过100水晶的道具，但没有人在乎这些。他们的动机是享受和肾上腺素。

因此，金箱子被引入游戏，使得那些对常规游戏玩法不太感兴趣的玩家能够得到娱乐。对一些知名的玩家而言，3D坦克的游戏限制在于捕获金箱子，随着时间的推移，这类玩家的人数只增不减。开始出现感兴趣的俱乐部。为了聚集喜欢捕捉金箱子的人，创建了Skype会议。单独捕捉一个金箱子并不是一件容易的事情，如果玩家是孤单的，很多运气因素会影响结果，所以组队工作极大地提高了他们成功的几率。通常，这是由装备良好、道具储备充足的经验丰富的坦克手进行的。

一些玩家已经放弃了他们的主账号，开始创建专门用来抓取金箱子和花费水晶进行升级的副账号。昵称如“GOLDHUNTER”和“GOLD_IS_MINE”变得相当普通。在低等级时，并不是每个人都知道金箱子的存在，因此这样的“多账号”很容易“收割”。唯一的问题是缺乏大型奖励战斗，因此搜索过程并不简单。

There were times when catching Gold Boxes became almost a new cult in Tanki Online - a kind of "gold rush". This was the name of the largest and most popular topic on the forum at the time. 1896 pages, 37 thousand messages, millions of views - an absolute record on the forum! 这个主题的创建是为了将捕捉金箱子的截图和视频集中在一个地方。这种汇集可以帮助初学者和更有经验的玩家。每天都有数十个截图在主题中发布，许多与这个爱好相关的方面被讨论。为了帮助玩家，制作了关于捕捉金箱子的指南，并提供了每张地图上捕捉箱子的示例记录。此外，在这个主题中，还记录了按名称统计的箱子数量，并编制了金捕手的排名。

The event took place from July 20th to 26th, 2011. A Gold Box worth 1000 crystals appeared on a certain map after reaching a certain fund, and players had only one week to figure out which map it was on. The trick was that the box did not drop at the usual fund multiple of 700, but instead at multiples of 1000 (- though no one knew about this beforehand).

The discussions on the Forum didn't stop for days. Players were sharing ideas, and some gifted artists posted intriguing photoshopped screenshots of the new Gold Box. Since it was logical to assume that the Gold Box dropped on the less popular maps, players were specifically examining the forgotten ones and crossing out the ones where nothing was found on the “Golds list”.

到第一天结束时，已经有许多猜测。一些玩家在想这是否是火车站。许多玩家在等候700-800的水晶奖励，结果只得到通常的100水晶金币，然后放弃了——这是一个错误。甚至有人发帖分享了掉落金箱子的准确位置的截图。Flanagun, which led the players to examine “Molotov” once again. By the morning, players had already figured it out and every minute a new battle was created on the “Molotov” map. The mystery was solved and the last thing to do was to catch as many Gold Boxes as possible.
