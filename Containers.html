幸运礼盒


A 幸运礼盒 is a type of 奖励 item with randomised contents. They can be opened using 钥匙 of same type. All earned and purchased 钥匙 can be found in the corresponding menu, accessed from the main大厅screen:幸运礼盒

Specifics of 钥匙

  • One 钥匙 = one 幸运礼盒Upon using a 钥匙 to open a 幸运礼盒, the 玩家 will receive one 奖励, randomly chosen from a pool of possible 奖励. The prize is chosen and added to the 玩家’s 账号 the moment the 幸运礼盒 opening animation begins.
  • 钥匙 never turn "old". After you obtain a 钥匙 of any type, you can keep it 永久. For example, you can collect史诗钥匙 for a long time, and spend them during any 特殊活动, when you like the contents of 史诗幸运礼盒.
  • If you are opening multiple 幸运礼盒 at once, clicking on the«Skip»button will 速度 up the opening animation by 4 times.
  • Unique items already owned by the 玩家 cannot be found in 幸运礼盒 again.


Available 幸运礼盒

普通幸运礼盒

The most basic 幸运礼盒 in the game. It is opened using 普通钥匙, which can be obtained from:

  • 每日, 每周, and 特殊任务奖励
  • 星级挑战tiers
  • 特殊活动 and minigames
  • Catching幸运礼盒金箱子in匹配战斗
  • 兑换码
  • Participating in 锦标赛 and 活动

Up to 15 幸运礼盒 can be opened at once. If you have fewer than 15 钥匙, clicking «Open» will open all 幸运礼盒 in one batch.

Discontinued 幸运礼盒

Some 幸运礼盒 were discontinued and are no longer possible to obtain. Such 幸运礼盒 typically became available for a limited time during 特殊活动. If the 玩家 kept any such 幸运礼盒 in their storage, their preview image was “greyed out” but they retain their original 奖励 pool and may be opened at any time.

XT 幸运礼盒


XT 幸运礼盒

These 幸运礼盒 were first added for the3D坦克 Trip 2019 活动. 玩家 could obtain the 幸运礼盒 either by visiting one of the 活动 in person and collecting a card with the兑换码. Later, such 幸运礼盒 were rewarded for reaching the final tier of星级挑战or they could be purchased in some 商店礼包.

XT 幸运礼盒 included one random XT皮肤(not all 皮肤 were presented in XT 幸运礼盒), or 20 000 水晶 in case all the 皮肤 available in the 幸运礼盒 were already on the 账号.

Pitstop 箱子


Pitstop 箱子

The first time those 箱子 with essential items for any 坦克手 appeared in the game during a "Discount Weekend" 活动 in March 2020.

Pitstop 箱子 included水晶,道具and金箱子. They could appear in the game 商店 during some festive 活动. 1 箱子 costed 30坦克币.

道具幸运礼盒


道具幸运礼盒

道具箱子 were 特殊幸运礼盒, exclusive toTO Mobile. One 箱子 could contain 3 random prizes, which could include:

  • 2 units of «护甲提升», «伤害提升», «速度提升», or «地雷» 道具
  • 1 unit of «维修工具» 道具
  • 150, 250, or 500水晶

A new 箱子 was rewarded every 6 hours for 免费.

Outdated 幸运礼盒

Sometimes 幸运礼盒 were archived. This happened when the prize pool changed substantially, such as new 装备改造 or 皮肤 being added to 超级礼盒, or 奖励 amounts getting adjusted in 每周任务礼盒. When a 幸运礼盒 was archived, all unopened instances of this 幸运礼盒 on all 玩家账号 became “greyed out”.

This system prevented any potential abuse from saving up large amounts of 幸运礼盒 and removed the unfair advantage from older 玩家. After December 22nd 2023, with release of 钥匙, all existing 幸运礼盒 were automatically opened or converted to 钥匙 depending on the 玩家's choice and as such, old 幸运礼盒 no longer exist. Interesting facts

  • 幸运礼盒 were added to the game on November 24th 2017, during the «Black Friday» 活动. They were sold in the 商店 until November 28th.
  • During 2017 Black Friday, 幸运礼盒 could also be earned in 战斗, by catching the then-new black 金箱子. Every single 服务器-dropped 金箱子 was a black 箱子 on that day.
  • On January 10th 2018, 幸运礼盒 were added to任务chain 奖励, and the black金箱子became a permanent feature.
  • On June 7th 2018, large 水晶 packs and XT 皮肤 were added to 幸运礼盒.
  • On October 30th 2018, 每周任务礼盒 were introduced. These 幸运礼盒 rewarded 玩家 with 水晶 and 道具 for completing 每周任务.
  • On October 10th 2019 the previously added 坦克币幸运礼盒 were renamed to their latest name 硬币箱.
  • From December 20th 2019 to January 9th 2020, the exclusive «坦克币坦克» 迷彩 could be received if a 玩家 found 100 000 坦克币 inside a «硬币箱».
  • On March 20th 2020 pitstop 箱子 were added.
  • On April 1st 2020 the XT 幸运礼盒 was replaced with the 皮肤礼盒.
  • On April 24th 2020 超级礼盒 were first added.
  • On April 29th 2020 幸运礼盒 were updated to include new装备改造.
  • On January 29th 2021 道具幸运礼盒 were removed fromTO Mobile, and a new礼盒碎片mechanic was added instead, allowing 玩家 to claim one standard 幸运礼盒 for every 100 礼盒碎片 collected.
  • On September 1st 2021, 皮肤礼盒 were removed from星级挑战. The final 黄金奖励奖励 instead included one specific 皮肤 for each星级挑战.
  • The星级挑战lasting from December 9th 2022 until January 1st 2023 had an updated 皮肤礼盒 added back as the final tier 奖励.
  • On March 17th 2023, 电池 were removed from 幸运礼盒.
  • On December 22nd 2023, 钥匙 were added to the game. Until this update, you were able to obtain 幸运礼盒 and open them. From this update you instead obtain 幸运礼盒钥匙, which are used to open 幸运礼盒. This update also removed the "old 幸运礼盒" mechanic, which archived 幸运礼盒 whenever a new version of the 幸运礼盒 with updated 奖励 was released.
