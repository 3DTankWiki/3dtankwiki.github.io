状态效果

see also:Heads Up Display

状态效果— are effects that affect the state of the 坦克. Under the influence of the actions of the enemy in the 战斗坦克 may catch fire, 冰风暴 or stop, may disappear the ability to turn on 道具 or use 过速装置, etc.

Basic Information

状态效果 are divided into positive and negative. Positive 状态效果允许 you to become a slightly stronger fighting vehicle, and negative indicates that the 玩家's capabilities on the battlefield are temporarily limited.

In combat, positive 状态效果 are appliedunderthe 坦克's health bar in the same row as active道具, and negative 状态效果 are appliedabovethe health bar. Also by default, when negative 状态效果 are applied for a second, colorful inscriptions appear on the 玩家's screen to complete the picture of what happened. You can disable the text prompts in the设置.


Positive 状态效果

抵抗

Main article:抵抗
«抵抗» is applied to the 坦克 after it appears on the spawn and materializes.

The 状态效果允许 to ignore 100% of any incoming 伤害, and complete 防护 from negative 状态效果 as well.

增压

Main article:增压
«增压» increases the probability of inflicting暴击伤害to 100%, i.e. every shot under the 状态效果 is 暴击.

Polarizer

Main article:Polarizer
«Polarizer» protects against some negative 状态效果 and removes previously applied 状态效果.

Negative 状态效果

着火

Main article:着火
«着火» takes several units of 每秒伤害. Time of the effect depends on how much the 坦克 is heated (from 1 to 10 seconds).

冷冻

Main article:冷冻
«冷冻» reduces the 速度 of the 坦克 up to 20%, and 炮塔 rotation 速度 - up to 80% of the initial. Time of the effect depends on how much the 坦克 isfrozen(from 1 to 10 seconds). The effect, in addition to slowing, disables the effects of伤害 enhancementfor the 持续时间 of the 冰风暴.

电磁脉冲 (电磁脉冲)

Main article:电磁脉冲 (电磁脉冲)
«电磁脉冲» disables and 禁止 the use of 道具 for a few seconds, and sends the 维修工具 and 地雷 into a forced recharge if the remaining recharge time is less than the 持续时间 of the imposed 电磁脉冲.

眩晕

Main article:眩晕
«眩晕» causes the 坦克 to be immobilized for a few seconds, unable to 射击, activate 道具 and 过速装置 or self-destruct.

穿甲 (穿甲)

Main article:护甲-Piercing (穿甲)
«穿甲» deactivates any 防护 enhancers installed on the 坦克: the "护甲提升" 道具, increased 防护 from无人机,防御模块,过速装置. That is, in spite of all the above enhancers, when the 状态效果 is imposed, the 坦克 will take full 伤害.

干扰器

Main article:干扰器
«干扰器» 禁止 the ability to use过速装置, as well as disables the无人机and its enhanced effects for the 持续时间 of the 状态效果. The suppression effect does not affect the already activated 过速装置 of the opponent.

Self-Destruction

Main article:Self-Destruction
«自毁» is inflicted on a 坦克 when either pressing theDELETE钥匙, or after changing in a 战斗 if something besides the迷彩,皮肤or射击效果was changed. In a few seconds after the effect was activated, the 坦克 self-destructs and is returned to spawn.
