雪人

雪人 is a 特殊炮塔 that fires deadly 雪球 into nearby 红队. It was accidentally created during a strong blizzard before Christmas. One of the old马格南prototypes was thrown away and left on the junkyard. Attempt to clear the barrel from snow lead to realization that snow that was loaded into the prototype could be used as ammunition. As a result it was decided to place it on a 底盘 and decorate it with a 雪人 with a carrot, lighted branches and a bucket.

玩家' opinions
Pros
Can 射击 while being behind cover. Decent 炮弹重装时间. On 暴击 hits, it activates 眩晕, 穿甲, 干扰器, and 电磁脉冲状态效果.
Cons
Short 射程. Small 溅射半径. Self-伤害.


防御模块

Main article:防御模块

Like终结者,番茄炮andB0-NK, there is no 防御模块 that provides 防护 from 雪人's 普通 (non-暴击) hits.

犰狳

«犰狳» 防御模块, Which protects the 坦克 from the 暴击 hit of the 雪人炮塔.

Spectrum

Unique 防御模块, Which protects the 坦克 from all standard 炮塔.

Table of Characteristics
  • 伤害 (hp)- the 伤害 dealt to an enemy 坦克 per one 普通 shot of the 炮塔 within the max 溅射伤害半径.
  • 暴击伤害 (hp)- the 伤害 dealt to an enemy 坦克 per 暴击 hit of the 炮塔 within the 暴击伤害溅射半径.
  • 冲击力- the physical impact of a 炮塔's projectile on enemy 坦克 upon contacting them.
  • Explosion impact- the physical impact and 伤害 on the 坦克 within a 半径 of where the point of the projectile landed.
  • 后坐力- the physical impact of the 炮塔 upon its own 坦克 when shooting.
  • Reload (s)- the time in seconds required to reload the 炮塔 so it may fire again.
  • Rotation 速度 (deg/s)- this is the 最快速度 that the 炮塔 can raise and lower the barrel.
  • Rotation accelertation (deg/s²)- The acceleration of the 炮塔 reaching its 最高 rotation 速度.
  • 暴击几率 (%)- 几率 of a 暴击 hit on a shot.
  • Amplification (s)- the time required to set the 最高 charge 等级.
  • Number of steps of charging- means that the process of charging the shot happens with steps being x = 100% / number of steps.
  • Projectile 速度 min. (m/s)- the 速度 of the projectile when at a 最低 charge.
  • Projectile 速度 max. (m/s)- the 速度 of the projectile when at the 最高等级 of charge.
  • Projectile gravitation- the effects of 铁塔 on the projectile.
  • Projectile 半径 (m)- the 半径 from the center of the projectile to its boundary.
  • Shot angle (deg)- The angle that the 炮塔 can 射击 at, for 雪人炮塔 it's fixed at 30º.
  • Max 溅射伤害半径 (m)- The distance from the projectile's impact point at which enemy receives 最高伤害. If the distance to the target is greater, the 伤害 constantly decreases until the 最低伤害半径.
  • Average 溅射伤害半径 (m)— The average 半径 of the 溅射伤害 for every shot.
  • Min 溅射伤害半径 (m)- The distance from projectile's impact point at which enemy receives 最低伤害. If the distance to the target is greater, 伤害 will not be dealt.
  • Average 溅射伤害 (%)— The percentage of normal 伤害 dealt when a 坦克 is within the 最高伤害半径 but does not hit the projectile.
  • Weak 伤害 (%)- the % or normal 伤害 dealt at a distance from the projectile's impact point, equal to 最低伤害半径.

Interesting Facts

  • The 炮塔 was released on December 24th, 2021 as part of a 特殊模式 "雪球 fight".
  • The 炮塔 was also used by game assistants on January 8th, 2022 for the特殊活动抓住雪人。
  • On 暴击 shots the 炮塔射击 a yellow 雪球 instead of a white one. It also activates穿甲,电磁脉冲,干扰器 and眩晕状态效果.
