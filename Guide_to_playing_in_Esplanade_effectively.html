海滨中心有效游戏指南

关于海滨中心


概述：海滨中心 is a relatively versatile 地图. It is a medium to large 地图, and is very dynamic, meaning that it can be played with pretty much any 底盘 and 炮塔 combo and playing style. Its most 普通 game 模式 is 互夺军旗, but is not played very often.

战斗模式:死亡混战, 团队对战, 互夺军旗, 控制点

大小:12v12, 24 玩家

战术

死亡混战

“露营”战术

In 死亡混战, as always, camping is the best strategy. The best camping spots are all enhanced by having a few 地雷, either to cover your back, or to 禁止 the only way for 红队 to get to you.
一个适合露营的好地方是在靠近旗子入口的隧道中的任意一条，可以看到走廊。当在隧道里时，最好有一个中等到重型的底盘，例如猎人中甲,维京,独裁者,泰坦or猛犸象along with a medium to long 射程炮塔, such as离子炮,火龙珠,滑膛炮,轰天炮,雷暴炮,激光炮和镭射炮. As most of these camping spots will require an additional line of defense, 地雷 will be extremely useful for blocking up the end that is nearest to the 旗子. Doing this will mean that 其他坦克手们 will come around the corner and BOOM! You will need at least three 地雷 to ensure a full blockade. If you do not have 地雷 this camping area is not so effective, as you can be caught looking the wrong way.

Another good place to 黑色营地 is on the top 等级 of the 地图, around where the tunnel comes out. This is an especially good place to 黑色营地 as there is only one way to get into it, and the top path is well used by 坦克手们. A medium to heavy 底盘 is the most effective option for camping in the top region.极速炮,激光炮,马格南and镭射炮是使用的最佳炮塔，因为它们具有巨大的有效射程。这个地点可能是黑色营地的最佳区域，因为它不需要地雷（虽然它们可能非常有帮助）来露营，而且你真的不会被突袭。

第三个露营地点在幸运礼盒旁，靠近隧道出口和控制点的位置。这个区域很强大，因为它有很多可以攻击的位置，但不幸的是，这意味着也有很多攻击你的方式。为了应对这一点，你可以使用地雷来禁止进入的道路，但人们仍然可以从上面跳下来，或者从周边平坦的地方向你射击，因此这个区域可能会有风险。如果在这里露营，建议使用装甲坚固的底盘，如猎人中甲,维京,独裁者,泰坦or猛犸象paired with a medium to long 射程炮塔, such as轰天炮,雷暴炮,极速炮,马格南,激光炮或镭射炮.

“Hit and Run” Tactic

Another strategy is the Hit and Run strategy. As stated by the name, this involves moving around almost all the time attacking everyone. You will almost certainly need a few drugs to pull this off effectively, especially double 伤害, but it can just about guarantee a top placing in the table.

The key to pulling this tactic off effectively is to get out of the enemy’s line of fire. Try to 伤害 them while driving around them, staying behind the 炮塔 if possible. The ideal equipment is probably a quick, sturdy 底盘 such as黄蜂轻甲,蜂王,维京或猎人中甲partnered with a 炮塔 that has 高伤害 and a reasonably quick reload 速度, such as火焰炮,冰风暴,磁力炮,滑膛炮or轰天炮.

团队对战

在海滨中心团队对战中，你要使用与死亡混战相同的战术，只是有更多的露营者、更少的地雷和一些磁力炮。要有一个磁力炮or two handy is very helpful so that your 团队 is able to replenish its troops on the battlefield. In this 模式 of play, it is recommended using medium 底盘 and long or medium 射程炮塔, with only a few faster 坦克 with冰风暴分散和减慢敌方坦克，以便更危险的高伤害坦克可以击败他们。不要忘记磁力炮!

互夺军旗

防御

Again, camping would be the best strategy for defense in 海滨中心. Nothing more secure than a fat Mammy-离子炮 sitting on the 旗子 with an 磁力炮 as his right-hand man, a pile of drugs ready to be used and a whole maze of 地雷 surrounding him. To put it simply, do this and the 红队 will seriously struggle to even 伤害 you, let alone take the 旗子.

Another method you can try for defense is to have a蜂王or维京paired with冰风暴, hanging out around the 旗子 so any 坦克手们 trying to get past will find it even harder to get away.

Attack

In attack, there are really only four main ways to get to a 旗子 and return. You can either go through the two tunnels, up the top, around the 地图, or a mixture of the three.

对于底盘，建议使用轻型底盘，例如蜂王or黄蜂轻甲这样你就可以快速进出敌人的基地，而不会受到太多伤害。在攻击时，最好是成对或更多小组进行。这意味着如果他们锁定了其中一个，一个可以趁着另一个的炮弹重装时间，偷偷拿到旗子。你需要尝试选择队友最多的路线，因为如果你失去旗子，他们会在附近捡起它。

控制点

Basically for this game 模式 on this 地图, you want to get the three 控制点 up the top. Once you have gotten these three points, all you need to do is hold them, and you will win. To capture these three points, you will need to storm the top and 禁止 off the spiral tunnel. While storming, have a well-balanced 团队. This means you will need a mix of short, medium and long 射程炮塔 with light, medium and heavy 底盘. Once you have captured the top, the best way to 禁止 them off is have 2 泰坦 side by side with an磁力炮后面。
