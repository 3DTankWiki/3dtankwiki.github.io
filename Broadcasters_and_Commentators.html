Broadcasters and Commentators

see also: Assistants of administration and In depth: Content helpers


Note
The Broadcasters and Commentators team comes under the general umbrella term of Content Helpers - which are further composed of the following groups: Designers, Assistants, Translators, and Proofreaders as well as the Game Translations team.
Broadcasting Team Leader

Broadcasting Team Leaders coordinate the work in the team of English Broadcasters and Commentators. If you have a complaint about the work of the helpers in the group, you should contact them.

In the list of Forum Members their names are highlighted in pale red.

Commentators

Commentators provide live-action commentary of the fiercest battles at the pinnacle of Tanki eSports and community events. Their job is to entertain viewers on broadcasts and to create interesting content for the community during broadcasts. They are the public voice of the community!

In the list of Forum Members their names are highlighted in blue.

Broadcasters

Broadcasters are an essential component of the livestream team. They are the workforce who physically bring the live camera footage to viewers; without their work it would be impossible to host any broadcasts.

In the list of Forum Members their names are highlighted in blue.

Commentators Candidates

Commentators Candidates are users who have decided to try out as Commentators. After passing a trial period and receiving positive reviews of their work, they will become full Commentators.
