What does an Event Helper do?

«

Event Helper’s job is to organize daily events. Everything starting with the ideas, until the realization of events is handled by Event Helpers and Organizers. If you will ever have a question regarding events, they will be more than happy to help you out!

»
— Nikk, Former Events Administrator



«

An Event Helper dedicates his time to organize interesting and successful events. We make sure that events are nicely, properly organized and the most important thing is that players enjoy. Along with that, they have an opportunity to earn some extra crystals as long as they follow the rules. We also ensure that winners are declared fairly.

»

«

An Event Helper works along with event organizers to make the event fun for participants and ensures that all participants follow the rules and guidelines. They also answer queries about events.
