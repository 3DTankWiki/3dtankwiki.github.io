  • The «肾上腺素»装备改造for each 炮塔 increases both fixed 伤害 and 暴击伤害;

Accumulation of 暴击 hit 几率

  1. When a 坦克 spawns into the 地图, the 玩家 starts with a setinitial 暴击几率. For most 炮塔, the initial 暴击几率 is 0%, which means that the first shot cannot be 暴击.
  2. Each normal hit on an enemy 坦克 increases the current 几率 by暴击伤害 step, if the最高暴击几率has not been reached yet.
    • For most 炮塔, these parameters (step and 最高几率 value) are constant.
    • There are also 装备改造 that can reduce or increase the step of the 几率 of 暴击伤害;
    • There is no exact limit to the number of hits between 暴击 shots in the standard 炮塔 setup, but some 装备改造 can turn the accumulated 暴击伤害 into stable 伤害, for example: the «精确弹药装载» 装备改造 for 轰天炮;
    • 溅射伤害 also counts as a hit to an enemy 坦克 and participates in the accumulation for the 几率 of a 暴击 hit, but for some 装备改造, 溅射伤害 can not be 暴击.
  3. After a 暴击 hit the 几率 to inflict 暴击伤害 with the next shot is reduced to theconsecutive 暴击几率, which is usually same as the initial value, however, sometimes it may differ.


The above algorithm is ignored when the 状态效果 增压is applied to the 坦克: under this effect all the 伤害 dealt by the 炮塔 becomes 暴击. This 状态效果 is applied by独裁者'sand蜂王's过速装置.

Calculation of the 防护 and 伤害 factor

A 炮塔 deals 100% of its standard 伤害 without any effects, while a 底盘 has 100% of its standard health without any effects. As described above,道具,无人机,防御模块, and装备改造can add further factors.

A伤害 multiplierof 250% causes 250% of the standard 伤害 to the enemy. The防护 multiplierindicates how much 伤害 a 坦克 actually takes: a 防护 multiplier of 40% reduces incoming 伤害 to 40%. Factors from状态效果(道具, 无人机) are additive to the total factor, while factors from 防御模块 and 装备改造 have a multiplicative effect. The exceptions are 肾上腺素 and 露营者装备改造, which are technically implemented as 状态效果 and therefore also count additively.

Example:

  • 玩家 1:
    • 100% normal 伤害
    • 100% more 伤害 from the "伤害提升" 道具
    • C% more 伤害 from a无人机
    • D% more 伤害 from an装备改造
      →伤害 Multiplier= (100% + 100% + C%) × (100% + D%) =X%
  • 玩家 2:
    • 100% normal 防护
    • 100% more 防护 from the "护甲提升" 道具
    • C% more 防护 from a无人机
    • D% more 防护 from a防御模块
      →防护 Multiplier= (100% - D%) / (100% + 100% + C%) =Y%


For example, if 玩家 1 is using "圣剑 Rounds" 装备改造 with "伤害提升" 道具 and "助推器" 无人机 and 玩家 2 is using "犰狳" 防御模块 with "护甲提升" 道具 and "守卫者" 无人机. All are assumed to be max 等级.
[玩家 1]: Base 伤害 (暴击) = 1740
[玩家 1]: 伤害 Multiplier = (100% + 100% + 25%) × (100% + 150%) = 562.5%
[玩家 1]: Accumulated 伤害 (rounded) = 1740 × 562.5% = 9788
[玩家 2]: 防护 Multiplier = (100% - 50%) / (100% + 100% + 90%) = 17.24%
[玩家 2]: 伤害 Received (rounded) = 9787 × 17.24% = 1688
