战斗 Interface Structure

Main Information

战斗 interface is a great assistant for any 坦克手. It tells the 玩家 very important information: for example, their amount of health,炮塔reload, and过速装置charge. It also notifies the 玩家 about a lot of extra information, for example: inflictedstatus-effects, activated道具, 战斗统计数据, time before the end of the 战斗, and nearby 坦克职务.

The most important information is shown as two horizontal lines in the center of the screen: theblueone shows the amount of health left and theyellowone indicates the reload of the 炮塔. Two full lines mean that the 坦克 is completely ready for 战斗.


Above or below those lines (depending on the chosen interface design), information aboutstatus-effectsinflicted to the 坦克 is shown with icons. It should be noted that in团队战斗there are alsobluelines above your 蓝队 that show their amount of health, which 帮助 interact with them in 战斗.

At the bottom of the screen, there is a panel with information about 坦克启动加速度-ups available for activation and already activated -道具and过速装置. In some interface variants this panel also contains information about 装备装备改造for 炮塔 and 底盘.


At the top of the 战斗 screen, there is information about the 战斗 - 分数 and time left until its end or any 其他 conditions. Information about the current 经验值 value, 等级 of your 账号, and hotkeys are located there. There is an equally interesting and useful interface element for more skilled 玩家 - theradar.


During the gameplay, extra information can be seen that may 帮助 the 玩家 in the 战斗. For example, information about charged 过速装置 and inflicted status-effects. To make 瞄准 easier, a 特殊 dot can be added to the middle of the screen by activating the“在屏幕中间显示一个点” setting. Proper usage of all information may significantly increase the 玩家's efficiency in the 战斗.

聊天in 战斗允许 communication between 其他玩家 in 战斗. It is accessible with theEnterkey.

Interface variants

The 战斗 interface exists in two variants: main and alternative. To switch to the alternative layout, activate the«屏幕中央的战斗界面» setting. In the same section, the 战斗画面尺寸 can also be set in percentage. The difference between the two interface variants is the 职务 of main elements of information and radar on the screen.

Themainvariant’s feature is its compactness. The middle part of the screen is 免费 of any 战斗 interface elements.

The bottom panel of 启动加速度-ups is split into two parts and is logically placed on the sides of the game window, making the middle part of the screen 免费 for health and reload bars and status-effects indicators. Radar is placed in the upper left part of the screen.

This variant of the interface focuses the 玩家’s attention to the 地图 in favor of covering their own 坦克 with interface elements.

MAIN 战斗 INTERFACE VARIANT

Thealternativevariant puts all of the elements to the middle part of the screen, raising health and reload bars to the 坦克. On the bottom of the screen, indicators of 过速装置 charge, 道具, and radar are laid out compactly.

This interface variant 允许 concentrating on the indicators of the 坦克’s condition, using the middle of the screen for this.

Main 战斗 interface elements

暂停 menu

The “战斗” or “暂停” menu can be opened with theESCorPkeys in the computer version of the game. In themobile version, there is a button in the top right corner of your screen for that. Using this menu, it is possible to enter the车库,changeequipment, open the设置, invite好友, or leave the 战斗. The timer shows the time you have left for the 暂停 until you are automatically removed from the 战斗.

战斗统计数据

To open the 战斗统计数据 window, which shows up as a semi-transparent window in the middle of your screen, hold theTabkey. There the following information is presented about each 玩家 in the 战斗:

  1. 等级
  2. 昵称
  3. 战斗力
  4. 装备炮塔with an 装备改造
  5. 装备底盘with an 装备改造
  6. Reputation points
  7. “Kills”/”Deaths” indicators


Hovering the cursor over a 玩家 will reveal their 防护防御模块, and after the 战斗 their 奖励 (their part of 水晶 from 战斗奖励 + bonus fromvip 账号if they have one, and stars earned for the current星级挑战).

战斗 details

战斗 details are placed in the upper part of the screen:

  • Time left until the end of the 战斗
  • Current 分数
    • in 团队模式: ally 团队分数 and enemy 团队分数
    • in solo 模式: your own 分数 and best 玩家’s 分数
  • Status and 团队’ ownership of points (if the 战斗 is played in控制点and围城大战战斗模式)

Display of 团队战斗 details

In专家战场, the bottom right corner will display the 战斗奖励, which is the total amount of 水晶 within the 战斗 that will be divided between all 玩家 when the 战斗 ends.

Important notifications

Important notifications appear in the middle of the screen. They notify the 玩家 about important 活动, and they trigger when:

  • The 旗子 is taken or delivered (互夺军旗)
  • A base is attacked (突袭)
  • The 球 will drop soon (橄榄球)
  • A goal is scored (橄榄球)
  • Enemy 剑圣 has joined the 战斗 (团队剑圣)
  • A 玩家 receives a new 炮塔 (军备竞赛)
  • A金箱子or one of its variants will be dropped soon
  • A 玩家 has taken a金箱子or one of its variants

战斗聊天

Main article:聊天

The战斗聊天is placed in the bottom left corner of the screen. It is activated with theENTERkey in the computer version of the game or with a button in the top right corner in themobile versionof the game. It shows 玩家' messages and duty messages, for example bans and 报告 confirmations. While in 团队战斗模式, the 聊天 can be switched between 团队 and 上将聊天 by pressing theTabbutton.

Radar

Main article:Radar Field

Radar is the graphical element in 战斗. It shows all 坦克 in a 90 meter 半径 from the 玩家. It can be used to see 坦克 in 高 places and behind corners of different obstacles. This can give a huge advantage in the game.

坦克 highlighting

In certain 战斗 situations 坦克 can be highlighted. They get a color contour. The color of the contour depends on different factors.

红队 are highlighted when the 玩家 points the 炮塔 at them. It shows which 坦克 will be hit. The distance which the color of the contour will be shown depends on the 炮塔's capability to hit targets at different distances. Enemy highlights with a melee 炮塔 can be seen only in a few meters, and long 射程炮塔 see it in hundreds of meters. Colors of the contour also differ in width and color.

Icons in 战斗

In the 战斗, most of the information is shown with icons. They are separated into different types, which you can read more about in the sections below:

  • 活动 log is shown in the top right and left corners of the screen. It shows 昵称 of active 玩家, but they disappear in a few seconds.
  • Goal indicators are shown in the 玩家’s field of view and point at the location of a certain object on the 地图. For example, a 玩家 moves with a 球.
  • Status-effects are shown above the 坦克’s health bar or 昵称. They affect the condition of a 坦克.

Main article:状态效果

Status-effects are shown above the 坦克’s health bar or 昵称. They affect the condition of a 坦克.

If a status-effect is inflicted on an enemy 坦克, the corresponding icon will appear above it. If the enemy has immunity to the effect, the “Immunity” text appears.
