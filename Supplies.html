道具



see also:过速装置and无人机

道具, orConsumablesare consumable items that can be spent in 战斗. Some of them 允许 you to temporarily increase one or another characteristic of your 坦克. For example, you can 速度 up your 坦克 for a certain amount of time, charge过速装置and drop a金箱子or throw a手榴弹.

In 战斗, the amount of 道具 available and their current status are displayed at the bottom of the screen on a 特殊 panel. Each 道具 has its own 声音 of the beginning and end of work, as well as the visual effect of availability for use. Thedefault道具 activation 钥匙 are1,2,3,4,5and6, as well asShiftfor 核能 andFfor 手榴弹.

Also, 道具 fall from the sky right during the 战斗 to specially marked points (drop 动感区域). 坦克手们 usually refer to such 道具 asdrops. Picked up 道具 immediately take effect and cannot be stockpiled or stored in the 车库. If you activated a 道具 and at the same time or a little later (but before the end of the activated 道具) picked up the same 道具-drop, then the effect of the 道具 will be extended. The 道具 drop rate is 84 seconds (based on a 道具 pickup).

Dependent 冷却

Dependent 冷却 — the period of time in which it is impossible to activate a 道具 if another 道具 is activated.

For example, if the «伤害提升» 道具 is activated, then «护甲提升» can be activated after 5 seconds. 冷却 and recharge of 道具 are reset with the respawn of the 坦克. Picked up drop does not affect the 冷却.

Dependent 冷却 can be removed by wearing one of two 无人机: «亥伯龙神» or «危机».

In匹配战斗, the Dependent 道具 option is enabled by default. In玩家-created战斗, this option is disabled by default, giving 玩家 the option to activate all 道具 at once.

Automatic 道具 activation

You can choose a 道具 that will beautomaticallyactivated when either its effect ends or its 冷却 is over. For that you need to press the needed 道具 activation button. After the 道具's effect or 冷却 is over, it will automatically be activated. Also, the system automatically activates维修工具道具 when 坦克's health is below 25%.

  • Automatic activation of the 道具 can be canceled by pressing the activation button again.
  • Next 道具 that will automatically be activated can be changed by pressing the corresponding 道具 activation button.
  • Automatic activation can be switched off in the设置. It is generally not available for the神谕者and危机无人机.

Features of the drop and drop 动感区域

  • The crates remain on the 地图 until someone picks them up. However, this was not always the case; once upon a time, crates simply disappeared into thin air if no one picked them up.
  • At the end of the 战斗 all crates are removed from the 地图.
  • If you use a «金箱子», it will fall exactly 10 seconds after the notification.
