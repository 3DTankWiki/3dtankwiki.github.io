双塔遗迹有效游戏指南

另见:Guides for 地图


About


Overview:双塔遗迹是一个中等地图。我们可以将其视为更大的。边塞角斗场. 这不是普通的，它在服务器上很少见，主要在RU1中被发现。它在经验值/BP战斗方面颇有名气。它的战斗奖励中等，因为这是一张中等规模的地图。
战斗模式:死亡混战, 团队对战, 互夺军旗, 控制点
大小:10v10, 20 玩家

Tactics

死亡混战/团队对战

死亡混战模式在这个地图上相当困难，因为你必须到处观察，并且由于地图的密度，要保持非常警觉。

Camping

有很多地方可以藏身，包括墙壁，它们在地图中间太多了。选择一个宽阔开阔、视野良好的地方，但如果需要的话要有足够的保护。一个好的例子是两侧的高点（如图所示）。它们周围也有所有三个道具掉落的动态区域，以及通向它们的易于禁止的坡道。

Hit and Run

肇事逃逸有几种形式。我将介绍一些适用于双塔遗迹的形式。

  • 火龙珠+蜂王/猎人中甲/维京: This combo works really well in 双塔遗迹 as there are many walls. You can hit people so easily by using these walls while remaining fully protected from them.
  • 滑膛炮+黄蜂轻甲/蜂王/维京: This combo works anywhere. It deals insane amounts of 伤害, force and fear into your 红队. These 底盘 let you really shot how powerful滑膛炮是。
  • 火焰炮/冰风暴+黄蜂轻甲/蜂王/维京在双塔遗迹，这些组合也是另一种神奇的事物。这些狭窄的路径意味着它的短射程完全无关紧要，同时也使得你的红队很难逃脱。

互夺军旗

互夺军旗模式非常困难，因为它的道路狭窄且地形难以记忆，这个模式需要大量的思考和良好的策略。
The Red and Blue 旗子 are not beside each 其他, so you will need to be on the move for quite some distance. You need some teammates behind you especially磁力炮帮助你击杀对手或治疗你。这个行动的最佳炮塔是磁力炮,火龙珠,雷暴炮,冰风暴,滑膛炮with蜂王,维京,猎人中甲,独裁者or泰坦永不放弃是胜利的关键。
Defending is the easy part. Just slap a猛犸象with轰天炮或火龙珠在旗子上面，放几个地雷，然后给他一个磁力炮好友 and you are sorted. As long as the 红队 don’t attack in packs you should be fine.

控制点

双塔遗迹 has five 控制点 points, each in very different parts of the 地图. Points B and C are in the 高 towers on either side of the 地图, and points A,D, and E are in the center points. The center points have a tendency to change hands very often, as they are all close together, whereas B and C are out of the main access area. These points can be the difference in a 战斗, but the main focus should be on the center 3.
These three points, even with the update, will give the 团队 with them the win. However, due to their constant fluctuation, it is necessary to have have at least either B or C. Each of these points is near either many Blue or Red spawn points, making these easier captures, and even easier to hold.

战术：
双塔遗迹有很多很多墙壁，造成火龙珠's bounce shots very effective. This 允许 one to clear out a point without taking 伤害. However, make note of differences between the vertical walls and the earthen walls, so as to not screw up your shot.马格南can also be used hit 红队 without having to face them, hiding behind the walls.
Also,雷暴炮溅射伤害 ability can use any surface to deal 溅射伤害 within its 半径 of 伤害. Moreover, it can, like火龙珠, clear out a point. This is effectively useful when a point is heavily occupied and one has道具开。
Finally, 双塔遗迹 has a plethora of walls, so while being wary of火龙珠is important, taking cover in this 地图 is vital, due to it being a relatively small and dense 地图 for a medium 地图. 坦克手们 can be closer than they seem. So the brief moment of cover can mean the difference between life and death.
