Help Site Consultants Administrators

Help Site Consultants Administrators performed the same functions as Help Site Consultants, but were also in charge of keeping the site clean and up-to-date, as well as dealing with complaints regarding Consultants.

In the list of the Forum Members their names were highlighted in carmine.

Help Site Consultants

Help Site Consultants were responsible for the Help Site - the hub of information in Tanki Online. It was a place where players could find answers to questions via the Knowledge Base, or if necessary, ask their own.

Help Site Consultants were incredibly knowledgeable players who brought their expertise to the table. They maintained the site, ensuring that good topics were being posted and players' questions were answered.

In the list of the Forum Members their names were highlighted in mauve taupe.

Help Site Consultants' Paint

Help Site Consultant Candidate

Help Site Consultant Candidates were users who decided to try out as Help Site Consultants themselves. After passing a trial period and receiving positive reviews of their work, they would become full Help Site Consultants.
