Forum moderators

see also: Assistants of administration, In depth: Forum moderators and Forum


Forum Administrators

Forum Administrators manage activities of the Forum Moderators, handle complaints about moderation errors, as well as monitor the order of the Forum.

In the list of the Forum Members their names are highlighted in pale red.

Forum Moderators

Forum Moderators are valiant warriors who keep the forum in order: they tirelessly drain the "swamps" of off-topic discussions, clean up the "Augean stables" of "littered" topics, while deleting irrelevant content to focus on more important details. And if someone suddenly decides to make a mess on the forum, because "everything is possible on the Internet," call a moderator, and they will quickly explain the rules of communication.

In the list of the Forum Members their names are highlighted in orange.

Forum Moderator Candidates

Forum Moderator Candidates are users who have decided to try out themselves as moderators of the Forum. After proper training, a probationary period, and positive feedback, they will become full-fledged moderators of the Forum.
