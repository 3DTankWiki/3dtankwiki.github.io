see also: Assistants of administration and In depth: Event Helpers


Note
This position no longer exists.
Events' Administrators

Events Admins performed the same functions as Event Organizers and Event Helpers but were empowered with greater authority. If you wanted to file a complaint about an Event Organizer or Helper's work, you had to send it to an Admin.

In the Game Chats, the Event Admins' work accounts were marked with a golden icon , and in the list of the Forum Members their names were highlighted in hollywood cerise.

Administrators' Paint
«Flame»
Events' Organizers

Event Organizers were the players specialized in organizing official Tanki Online Events. If you ever had a question regarding Events, those were the players you needed to speak to.

In the Game Chats, the Event Organizers' work accounts were marked with a silver icon , and in the list of the Forum Members their names were highlighted in neon pink.

Events' Organizers, Broadcasters & Helpers Paint
«Neon»
Events' Organizers Candidates

Events' Organizers Candidates were users who decided to try out as Events' Organizers themselves. After passing a trial period and receiving positive reviews of their work, they would become full Events' Organizers.

Events' Helpers

Event Helpers assisted Event Organizers on the battlefield and helped make events fun and enjoyable for players. Events could not be done with just the Event Organizers, which is why the work of Event Helpers was also needed.

In the Game Chats, the Event Helpers' work accounts were marked with a bronze icon , and in the list of the Forum Members their names were highlighted in neon pink.

Events' Helpers Candidates

Events' Helpers Candidates were users who decided to try out as Events' Helpers themselves. After passing a trial period and receiving positive reviews of their work, they would become full Events' Helpers.

Events' Evidence Broadcasters

Event Evidence Broadcasters were an important part of events, making sure that everything happening during events was documented. Their job was to record the whole event, especially if Event Organizers were forced to remove violators. Event Broadcasters had to communicate and cooperate intensively with the Event Organizers.

In the list of the Forum Members their names were highlighted in piggy pink.
