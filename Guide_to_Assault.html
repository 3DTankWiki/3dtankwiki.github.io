Introduction

突袭模式 is a very unique 模式 in 3D坦克. In fact, this is the only 模式 where each 团队 has a different way to earn points. A little bit confused? No worries, read on! The blue 团队 has a base which they need to protect. The blue 团队 receives points for each kill they get. The red 团队 has a few 旗子 in their base which they need to drop in the blue base. The 团队 that earns the most points wins!

Scoring

旗子 capturing

The attacking 团队's main task is to capture as many 旗子 as possible, so it just makes sense that 旗子 capturing will be worth the most points. For each 旗子 captured, the 团队 receives 5 points and the 玩家 who captured the 旗子 receives 50 战斗分数. Unlike 互夺军旗 however, the enemy 团队 won't be trying to steal your 旗子 as it does not exist. Everyone's eyes will be on the 旗子 holder, so capturing the 旗子 will be twice as hard. It's very important to acknowledge that there is more than 1 旗子 in the 地图. A lot of great tactics were created such as the distraction tactic, a 坦克手 from the red 团队 takes the 旗子 and gets the enemy 团队's attention, while the enemy 团队 is trying to eliminate the 旗子 holder, another member of the red 团队 sneaks from the behind with the 其他旗子 and capture it with ease.

Another easy yet effective tactic is attacking together. Trust me when I say it is a lot harder focusing on multiple targets rather than 1. When you attack together, the blue 团队 will have to focus on a few targets. Not only they will have a hard time killing you, your 几率 to capture at least 1 旗子 will be much higher!

Destroying 坦克

For the blue 团队, this is the main source for points. For each kill they get, the 团队 receives a point, and like in 其他模式 the 玩家 who finished the enemy 坦克 receives 15 战斗分数 and everyone else who assisted receive 5 战斗分数. The good thing about it, is you do not have to start looking for 坦克手们 to kill, everyone from the 其他团队 will come right to your base in order to 互夺军旗, this is when you have to take action and destroy them. A great way to do say before the red 团队 reaches your base is by sniping them with long ranged 炮塔. You'll either kill them or force them to use their 维修工具, now you are already in an advantage.

炮塔

There's a lot of flexibility with the 炮塔 that are useful in 突袭模式, but here are some suggestions:

Attacking:

  • 火焰炮 and 冰风暴 are good for attacking the crowd of 守卫者 when you dive in
  • 磁力炮 is good if you like to attack in 小组 - keep your teammates healed up as they approach!
  • 离子炮 (especially with the 稳定等离子体装备改造) and 火龙珠 are great for pushing 红队 out of your way.
  • With their wide 溅射半径, 马格南 and 电磁炮 are excellent at clearing the base out from a distance.
  • 激光炮, 雷暴炮, and 火箭炮 are also good for attacking a 小组 of 守卫者 from a moderate distance.
  • +极速炮 with the 燃烧带装备改造 can set multiple 守卫者 aflame, weakening them for your final arrival.

Defending:

  • 冰风暴 is helpful for slowing down 红队 trying to drive into the base, especially if you use the 震动冻结装备改造.
  • You may want to steer clear of weapons with large 溅射伤害, since the base can get very crowded and you don't want to hit yourself. 马格南 and 电磁炮, with a 高 shot or distant snipe, do not present as much risk as one might expect.
  • 磁力炮 is an excellent choice, as it can keep your teammates healed while also serving as a final heavy 伤害 dealer to anyone who gets too close. The 支援纳米机器人装备改造 is fantastic if you trust your teammates to handle the direct combat with attackers.
  • 离子炮, 火龙珠, and 滑膛炮 are all excellent choices for using 冲击力 to prevent an enemy from reaching the base. 稳定等离子体is recommended for 离子炮 to avoid 溅射伤害.
  • 激光炮, though a bit unwieldy at close 射程, is great for weakening 红队 who aren't too close yet, and can 射击 through teammates who may be in the way.


底盘

Though each 团队 has a specialized task to complete, there's a surprising amount of flexibility in what is useful. In fact, every 底盘 in the game has unique merits for playing in 突袭模式, and the 底盘 you choose is often the most important factor that can determine your success.

Attacking:

Every 底盘 has its strengths and weaknesses for attacking. Which is best? That's hard to say, and depends on the situation and your own style. Here are some advantages and drawbacks of each:

Guide to Attacking with each 底盘
  • 黄蜂轻甲
    • The fastest 底盘 in the game, 黄蜂轻甲 is ideal for traversing quickly across the 地图. With a 速度提升, you can reach the base before the 守卫者 are ready for you for an easy attack at the beginning of the 战斗.
    • 黄蜂轻甲 is ideal for taking unusual and unexpected routes into the base, since it's 速度 makes up for the extra distance necessary
    • 黄蜂轻甲's 过速装置 is an excellent way to clear out the base, but don't plan on surviving there for three seconds while waiting for it to go off!
    • 黄蜂轻甲 is light on its tracks and doesn't turn or accelerate too quickly, so maneuvering is tricky. If you can, it's best to find a straight line (or a ramp to jump off!) that takes you from the entrance to the base, but be careful: if you get hit or run into someone, you may end up on your side or flipped.
  • 蜂王
    • 蜂王, though slightly slower than 黄蜂轻甲, is more maneuverable and stable - it will take a bit longer to reach the base, but you might find it easier to attack once you're there.
    • When using a 炮塔 like 火焰炮 or 冰风暴, 蜂王's 过速装置 makes clearing out 红队 a breeze - and can reveal and hidden 守卫者.
  • 霍珀
    • 霍珀's 过速装置 is your best 好友 for attacking - it can both 眩晕守卫者 and 允许 you to leap over 地雷 and in-the-way 红队.
    • If you don't have an 过速装置 ready, ramming into a defending 坦克 on the ground may let you drive over them, but if someone is too big, you'll have a lot of trouble getting by with so little 重量 and pushing 启动加速度.
  • 维京
    • The fastest of the medium 底盘, 维京 can get you to the base pretty quickly, and it's extra health will come in handy once you're there.
    • 维京's 过速装置 will have no trouble getting even the most heavily-armored 守卫者 out of the way, but don't count on having it ready very often.
    • 维京 is light for a medium 底盘, and a bit hard to maneuver - especially when getting shot at with 高冲击力. Be wary of your surroundings, or you'll get knocked off course!
  • 猎人中甲
    • Though a bit slower than 维京, 猎人中甲 is very stable and maneuverable - excellent qualities for the final approach.
    • 猎人中甲's 过速装置 is one of the best for incapacitating 守卫者: after activation, anyone in 射程 will be powerless to stop you. Be careful, though: it's easy to get killed during the 1.1-second delay.
  • 独裁者
    • 独裁者 will take a while to get to the base, but its wide tracks and 高重量 are helpful for getting past stubborn 守卫者.
    • It's best to attack with a 小组 when using 独裁者, since your 过速装置 will benefit your teammates immensely.
  • 十字军
    • While not terribly impressive in terms of 速度 or agility, 十字军 can drive over 低守卫者 like 黄蜂轻甲, 蜂王, and 维京.
    • 十字军's 过速装置 is great for 射击 into the base - the 护甲-piercing effect it applies will let you destroy whoever survives the explosion with ease.
  • 泰坦
    • You'll have a slow but well-armored journey to the base with this one. Take care to stay hidden while approaching, though: even a heavy 底盘 won't make it to the base if you give the 红队 time to 射击 at you before you arrive.
    • Once there, 泰坦's incredible 重量, pushing 启动加速度, and HP will let you push through a thick crowd of 红队, and take a beating while doing it.
    • If you can get close enough before dying, putting up 泰坦's dome 过速装置 can not only 帮助 you attack the base and destroy 守卫者, but can also 帮助 your teammates survive their final approach. However, nearly every 其他底盘 in the game has a counter to 泰坦's 过速装置 - you may find yourself blown up, deactivated, or armorless sooner than you like.
  • 猛犸象
    • With such a slow 底盘, you'll be hard pressed to even pick up a 旗子 before it's snatched up by a teammate, and even harder pressed to get to the base before getting waylaid.
    • However, if you can bring a 旗子 across the 地图, you should have no trouble with the last bit - you can push past pretty much anyone and take a lot of punishment.
    • 猛犸象's 过速装置, though short-lived, is very hard to counter, and can get you a few kills in addition to an easy capture.
  • 阿瑞斯
    • 阿瑞斯 doesn't have the 重量 or pushing 启动加速度 of the 其他 heavy 底盘, but it's smaller 大小 can let you get between 守卫者.
    • 阿瑞斯's 过速装置, though only effective if used on 等级 ground, can keep you (and a teammate or two as well) healed almost to the point of invincibility while you drive into the base, and destroy any 红队 who get in your way.


Defending:

You also have a lot of 选项 for defending the base. Below are some tips for using each 底盘.

Guide to Defending with each 底盘
  • 黄蜂轻甲
    • Being small and light, you likely won't be doing a lot of camping at the base with this one - use its 速度 to fight attackers in the midfield, before they reach the base.
    • If you time it right, 黄蜂轻甲's 过速装置 is a fantastic last-resort defense against any attacker.
  • 蜂王
    • 蜂王 will also do well fighting abroad, though its stability and agility make it reasonably well-suited to hanging out at the base with a 炮塔 like 激光炮.
    • 蜂王's 过速装置 will 允许 you to spot 红队 sneaking up from unexpected directions, and the 护甲-Piercing effect it applies will seriously weaken arriving 坦克' ability to survive long enough to complete an attack.
  • 霍珀
    • Like the 其他 light 底盘, 霍珀 is often used for countering lone 红队 around the 地图 rather than hanging out in the base.
    • The 眩晕 effect of 霍珀's 过速装置 is instant, works in all directions, and will cause most 坦克 to drop their 旗子 - it's a near-surefire way to prevent a successful attack.
  • 维京
    • 维京 is a good all-rounder: fast enough to cruise around the 地图 if the base is secure, but strong enough to hang out in the base if needed.
    • Leave shoving matches to 其他底盘, since you're so flat that many attackers will drive right over you.
    • Though it's slow-charging, 维京's 过速装置 can take down virtually any enemy that's in sight - a frightening and spectacular defense.
  • 猎人中甲
    • 猎人中甲 is a bit bulky, but very maneuverable, so placing yourself well can make it very hard for 红队 to drive past you.
    • The combination of a 眩晕 and 电磁脉冲 delivered by 猎人中甲's 过速装置 is a fantastic counter to attackers. Make sure you're in 射程, and that you activate a bit earlier than you might think you need to because of the 1.1-second delay.
  • 独裁者
    • One of the finest defending 底盘, 独裁者 is huge, heavy, and can push hard: park on or near the base, or in the middle of an entrance, and it'll be very hard to get past you.
    • 独裁者's 过速装置 can tip a close match, or turn a 胜利 into a domination. The 治疗 and activation of all 道具 for you and your teammates (including all-important 地雷!), combined with the 50% 过速装置 boost you give your teammates, is perhaps the most helpful thing you can contribute as a 守卫者.
    • As a bonus, you get points for activating your 过速装置 in a crowd of teammates, and 冰风暴 any attacker that's too close.
  • 十字军
    • Due to its hovering nature, 十字军 is best used a little ways outside the base, where you'll be able to attack attackers without fear of them driving under you.
    • Be careful with the 溅射伤害 of your 过速装置! It's a good way to counter a 团队 that attacks in 小组, but it can cause you to self-destruct if you use it too close to you.
  • 泰坦
    • Your 团队 will thank you for putting up a giant protective dome with your 过速装置 - it's well-suited to the crowded quarters of the base.
    • A heavy 底盘, 泰坦 is, well, heavy. It's also big. Great for obstructing 红队.
  • 猛犸象
    • 猛犸象 is the king of bulk, 重量, and 启动加速度 - it's unparalleled 大小 and steadiness will stop most 红队 in their tracks, even just inches from the base if that's where you're parked.
    • 猛犸象's 过速装置 is a good last-resort defense: you can't attack the base if you're vaporized when you touch it!
  • 阿瑞斯
    • Though 阿瑞斯 is smaller and more easily pushed around compared to 其他 heavy 底盘, its added maneuverability 允许 it to navigate the crowded base well.
    • 阿瑞斯's 过速装置 takes a few seconds to do its work, but it's a good way to heal several teammates or take out an approaching enemy.

Another important consideration is your 底盘装备改造! For defending, you'll mostly be best with重型结构: you'll be much harder to push past. However, make sure to assess your 红队: if you're getting 道具 deactivated frequently, for example,抗电磁脉冲 would be best; if someone keeps 穿甲 you,攻强免疫 is best, and so on. 抗热 combined with 极速炮's 燃烧带 is a widely-used combination.

For attacking, you'll likely find that重型结构 works great for light and medium 底盘: your 坦克's added momentum can let you coast into the base with ease. For a heavy 底盘, you'll probably want something more suited to that specific 战斗, since you're already hard to push off course. On any 底盘,抗眩晕 is excellent for attacking, since it stops you from dropping the 旗子 when hit by a 猎人中甲's or 霍珀's 过速装置.

无人机

One last important piece of equipment to consider is the 无人机 you use. Though it's not necessary to use a 无人机, there are some that you'll find quite helpful. Here's the rundown:

无人机 in 突袭
  • 布鲁特斯is a cheap and simple way to give yourself a bit more "oomph" on either 团队.
  • 亥伯龙神is good for jumping into action quickly, since it lets you activate all 道具 at once.
  • 危机is fantastic for attacking quickly, and can come in handy where extra 护甲 or 伤害 are needed.
  • 突袭is decent if you find yourself defending the base without much teammate assistance.
  • 驱动器is excellent, since 过速装置 are a 暴击 part of success in 突袭模式, but you do need to be getting kills to use it.
  • 奔跑者is useless on the defending 团队, but can get you quickly and safely across the 地图 (though perhaps not quite to the base) on the attacking 团队.
  • 助推器is great for taking out problematic attackers and problematic 守卫者 alike, and has the added benefit of not cooling down DD after activating another 道具.
  • 爆破手is lots of fun attacking on a small 底盘, since you'll get some good kills even when you don't quite make in with your 旗子, and can also be a last-resort way to thwart an attack.
  • 骑兵is alright if you have the lower hand on the defending 团队, but its effects usually wear off too quickly to be useful attacking.
  • 工兵is a fantastic and flexible way to protect the base - with proper 道具 activation, you can plant a 地雷 every 10 seconds, and they'll stay there until someone hits them or the 战斗 ends.
  • 守卫者is very popular for attacking due to its 护甲 boost and lowered DA 冷却, and can also come in handy for defending an overrun base.
  • 救生员can save an otherwise unsuccessful attack, even from 伤害 as severe as 黄蜂轻甲's 炸弹.
  • 破坏者is great for defending, but be careful: you only get the effect every 40 seconds, and the 地雷 do go away if you die.
  • Mechanicis well-suited to the close quarters of a defending 团队 or a 小组 of attackers.
  • 工程师can give your whole 团队 a bit of a boost at once, and is most useful for attacking, since the attacking 团队控制 most of the 地图's area (and thus drop 动感区域)
  • 供应, like mechanic, is excellent for use in the 小组 of 坦克手们 so commonly found in 突袭.
  • 魔术师is uncommonly used, but is nice for preparing a 小组 for an attack or helping your fellow 守卫者 recover from one.
  • 露营者can keep you happy when used a ways outside the base, where you won't get hit as much.
