在辽源有效玩的指南

关于辽源


Overview:辽源是一张非常不受欢迎的地图。当天有一场战斗能够持续大部分时间，那真是个珍奇的日子，尽管这个阶段有独特的方面，是在其他地方找不到的。辽源虽然在丰度上不及多边形和小岛的等级，但仍然提供了丰富的游戏选项，为支持每一个炮塔和底盘提供了机会。
战斗模式:死亡混战, 团队对战, 互夺军旗, 控制点
大小: 20 players, 10 vs 10

Tactics

死亡混战/团队对战

Scope balances on the border between being a medium and large map. A massive open space sprawls between two cordoned bases, leaving each side and the no-man's-land clearly defined. There is a long underpass that connects the 2 sides as well. Aside from the apparent routes across the field between several entrances and exits on the ramparts, there is a small network of tunnels underground that travel beneath the stage. As this stage can support all kinds of metallic life, there's no surefire winning combo right off the bat, as the situation can change drastically between individual players. However, a few manage to just stick out among the multitude of other combinations.

  • 黄蜂轻甲 and 滑膛炮–黄蜂轻甲/滑膛炮 always fares well on Scope due to the excellent tandem functions of massive damage output by 滑膛炮合适的炮弹重装时间，以及无与伦比的灵活性和快速从一个地方移动到另一个地方的能力，能够逃脱紧急情况。这种组合适合地下通道和地图的基地，因为速度将允许你快速通过，而高伤害输出将允许你严重伤害或击杀任何你遇到的敌人，特别是如果你心中保持对哪些坦克之前受过伤的记录。在基地中使用这个组合的最佳方式是悄悄绕到你红队的背后，从后方攻击他们，然后退回到隧道中，为另一个入口让路。出其不意是毋庸置疑的。滑膛炮/黄蜂轻甲's best factor, so possibly a paint that can help you blend in with the surroundings would serve you well. Lead is great for the field, whereas Carbon and Rhino have potential to keep you well hidden in the shadows.
  • 维京 and 火龙珠由于明显的原因，火龙珠 can pretty much own anyone they so choose in the tunnels. Add the stability and suitable balance of health and speed of 维京, and a considerable threat is born. High projectile speed and the uncanny ability to fight from behind blind corners means that you're going to be difficult to confront directly, especially in the tunnels where missing is something that requires effort to accomplish. You're not limited to the confines of the subterranean complex, however; the planar design of the bases means there are still plenty of opportunities to fire a few crafty shots and knock off the aim of a few light tanks. Find some Double Damage crates, and the raw power alone will be enough to quash enemies that dare stray near.
  • 维京 and 镭射炮– 在一张名为辽源的地图上，可以认为拥有辽源的武器应该几乎没有问题。镭射炮在这一方面毫无欠缺，充分利用每栋建筑前宽广的空地，以严厉打击侵犯其基地的入侵者。待在建筑物上本身要比维京/镭射炮 for a maximum targeting range. Viking also doubles its effectiveness as a stable hull by being able to occasionally outrun an attacker if needed. Hiding your laser is the key to not being immediately discovered. You will need to find an object that is very close to you that can hide your laser, so you don't wind up scaring away all of your potential targets.

互夺军旗

Both bases in Scope are quite well covered. Each construct is built like a castle: few entrances, high walls, and plenty of ways to attack from above. These traits make the buildings easy to defend, but at the same time, it also means that the tunnels become far more favorable. Dividing your attention between the lower entrances and the main gate is necessary to properly defend your side. A 猎人中甲/离子炮 with good aim will do for blockading an area and patrolling, but if you're looking for a more stalwart approach, 猛犸象/雷暴炮 has been known for being nigh undefeatable with a few supplies. As for attacking, you would usually want to take the underpass to get to the base. The open areas in the middle can be used as well, but often there are quite a few 镭射炮 and 激光炮 patrolling that area or scouting from directly above. It’s recommended that you use 蜂王 or 黄蜂轻甲对于进攻性动作，因为其他底盘的速度不足以快速进出并拿到旗子。和 outrun the other team if needed. However, medium hulls are great at supporting the attack and blocking off the enemy as needed. Any turret can perform well; it just depends what you are comfortable with and what you are trying to do. For example, 火焰炮,雷暴炮和滑膛炮 are great for clearing the area around the flag, 火龙珠能够从掩体后面发起攻击，并且轰天炮或激光炮 is best for attacking the enemies at a range. If you decide to use 镭射炮, remember that you're their lifeline in the event of a retreat. This means clearing the way for them or taking out pesky opponents is a must when the opportunity becomes available.

控制点

蜂王或黄蜂轻甲 is great for getting to those control points that are closer to the enemy base, but have a 维京 follow up quickly to 保持 the point under your team's control. When partnered with a powerful turret that can lay into opponents quickly, such as 火焰炮,冰风暴 or 离子炮, you become both fast and extremely deadly. Even 磁力炮可以潜在地削弱红队并增加自身的生存能力，但它们可能更适合支持角色而非纯粹的进攻角色。一旦团队之间的分数确定，真正的战斗就会确保，通常是在中央队伍之间的推搡。在这种战斗模式中，中场球员更为重要，使得独裁者 to roam the fields with any moderately ranged weapon and wreak havoc upon anyone who dares pass through the enemy gates.
