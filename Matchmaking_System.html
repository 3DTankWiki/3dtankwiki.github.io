匹配

匹配 (匹配)is a system of automatic selection of 战斗 for 玩家, the task of which is to provide approximately equal conditions for all participants in the 战斗.

Features:

  • The 玩家's 团队 is alwaysblue, the opponent is always on thered团队
  • Completing任务and星级挑战counts only in 匹配战斗
  • Only in 匹配战斗 can you get stars after the end of a 战斗.
  • 金箱子,钥匙, and Megagolds can only drop in 匹配战斗.
  • At 低等级, 匹配 teammates and opponents are bots.

How to start playing

In the大厅, the«Play»button is available to the 玩家. By pressing the large«Quick 战斗»button in the window that opens, the search for a random 战斗 begins. If a 玩家 uses this option, 1 bonus staris added to the 奖励 at the end of the 战斗.

To select the required 模式, you need to click on«模式»in the same window.

The 玩家 can choose one of the game 模式 in 匹配:Quick 战斗- enter the 战斗 with a random模式from those available by 等级;
团队 Deatmatch(团队对战) — available from 三级军士长;
控制点(控制点) — available from 新兵;
互夺军旗(互夺军旗) — available from 一级军士长;
Siege(围城大战) — available from 一等兵;
剑圣(团队剑圣) — available from 中士;
橄榄球(橄榄球) — available from 二级军士长;
突袭(突袭) — available from 军士长.

匹配地图

The 大小 of 地图 in 匹配 and the number of 玩家 on them depend on the 等级 of the 玩家, as well as the number of 玩家 in the 小组. For example, a beginner at the beginning of a career path will not come across the柏林巷站地图, and a 小组 of three 玩家 will not get to the高原地图.

A list of all地图available in 匹配 is presented in the 地图 article.

小组

Main article:好友 system

In 匹配, you can play with 好友 if you create a temporary 小组.

小组 mechanics features:

  • All fighters of the 小组 get into 战斗 at the same time and for one 团队
  • Creation of a 小组 is available from the 三级军士长等级.
  • 小组 can have a 最高 of 2 玩家.
  • 其他 member of the 小组 can be up to 20 等级 higher or lower than its 创建者
  • When searching for a 战斗, 小组 are queued according to the 等级 corresponding to the highest 等级 among the 小组 members
  • Canceling a search by any member of the 小组 stops the search for the entire 小组
  • Only the 创建者 of the 小组 has the right to invite 玩家 to it, remove members, dismiss the 小组 and start the 战斗 search. For the rest of the 小组 members, the game buttons in 匹配 are blocked while they are in the 小组
  • A new 战斗 is necessarily created for the 小组, that is, the 小组 cannot get into the 战斗 that has already begun

Reporting a 玩家

see also:Moderation System

In 匹配, a 玩家 can be reported if they, for example, use cheats or sabotage their own 团队. To 报告 a rules violation, do the following:

  1. Open战斗统计数据with theTAB钥匙
  2. Click on the 玩家’s 昵称
  3. In the appeared window select “报告 the gameplay”


If a 玩家 writes unacceptable words or insults 其他玩家 in 聊天, they can be reported. In the 战斗聊天 it is required to click on the red icon near the 聊天 panel to open the history of messages sent in the 战斗. Then click on their 昵称 and in thecontext menupress “报告 the message”. Then ourmoderatorsstep in: they will process the 报告 as soon as possible and make the decision.

匹配 mechanics

When pressing the game buttons in 匹配, the 玩家 queues up to search for a 战斗. The 战斗 search can be canceled by clicking the button in the pop-up window.

Once there are enough 玩家 in the queue, 匹配 will create a 战斗 for them on a random 地图 from the list below. The 战斗 will be guaranteed to have the模式selected by the 玩家 (or one of the 模式 available by 等级 if the 玩家 has selectedQuick 战斗).

匹配 creates a 战斗 with certain 等级 limits. If, upon reaching a certain waiting time, 玩家 are not enough to create a 战斗, 匹配 can expand the 等级-limits of the created 战斗 so that more 玩家 of different 等级 can take part in it. However, the 团队 remain equal among themselves in terms of the 玩家'战斗力: if a 玩家 who is too weak or too strong suddenly appears in one 团队, 匹配 will assign the second such 玩家 to another 团队.

If there are 免费 places in the 战斗 that has already begun, 匹配 can add new 玩家 to it. Adding 玩家 is impossible if there is little time left before the end of the 战斗, but it remains possible if the 战斗 is about to end with an early 胜利 of one of the 团队.

At the end of the 战斗, all 玩家 are removed from it, after which the 战斗 itself disappears.

The 玩家 who clicks the «Continue» button on the 战斗 end screen is automatically placed in a new 战斗 search with the same 模式设置 as the previous search. The 玩家 in the 小组 who clicked the «Continue» button enters the 大厅 and waits for the 战斗 to start from the 小组队长. The 队长 of the 小组, when clicking on the above button, automatically starts the search for a 战斗 for the entire 小组.

Earning points and 奖励 distribution

The 分数 of each 坦克手 in a 匹配战斗 grows the faster, the more actively he performs the main tasks of the 模式 - capturing 旗子 to互夺军旗, capturing points in控制点, and so on.

At the same time, you need to keep in mind that the 分数 (reputation points) and 经验值 are different indicators. For the same action, a 坦克手 is awarded a different amount of reputation points and 经验值 in the progress of the 等级. When completing每日 or 特殊任务, it is the reputation points that are taken into 账号, which can be seen in the 统计数据 window by pressingTab.

Bots

In 匹配战斗, bots can be present among 蓝队 and opponents in all game 等级. In the lower 等级, 战斗 are only against bots, not against 其他 real 玩家. At higher 等级, bots only appear if the 战斗 search time is significantly exceeded. For 防护, bots use unique a防御模块 with a 防护等级 of 10% to 60%. From 元帅等级 are bots 装备 with 爆破手装备改造.
