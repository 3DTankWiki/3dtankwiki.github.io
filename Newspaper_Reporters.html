Newspaper Reporters

see also: Assistants of administration, In depth: Reporters and Press


Note
This position no longer exists.
Reporters Administrators

Reporters Admins performed the same functions as Reporters but were empowered with greater authority. If you wanted to file a complaint about a Reporter's work, you had to send it to an Admin.

In the list of the Forum Members their names were highlighted in purple heart.

Reporters

Reporters revealed secrets, conducted investigations, and shared the latest news on the pages of the Tanki Online Newspaper. If something interesting, unusual, or important happened, you would always know about it from them. And if you found something worth mentioning, you could tell a reporter about it as soon as possible!

In the list of the Forum Members their names were highlighted in light slate blue.

Reporters' Paint
«Reporter»
Reporter Candidates

Reporter Candidates were users who decided to try out as Reporters themselves. After passing a trial period and receiving positive reviews of their work, they would become full Reporters.

In the list of the Forum Members their names were highlighted in light slate blue.

Interesting Facts

The Reporter paint is no longer given to Newspaper Reporters as the position was closed in 2021. The paint is still in the game and is given to Wiki Editors.
