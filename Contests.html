The majority of 坦克手们 (and probably yourself, too) are always looking for some great ways to earn cool prizes; and one way to do so is by participating in contests! The Contest Organizers 团队 regularly creates fun and entertaining contests with great prizes for 玩家 to enjoy. These contests sometimes involve creativity, while 其他 involve intellectual brainpower!

In this article, we will discuss contests with you, letting you know everything that must be known, including how to participate, information on prizes, etc., and as well as clear up any confusion that you might have on the subject. Let’s jump straight in!

Basics

Before we get into the specifics, we need to know a bit about what contests are, how they are carried out, and 其他 basic information. The most important thing to know is that contests can be divided into two categories: '跑酷 Contests' (testing 玩家’ 能力 in the 跑酷 realm) and '普通 Contests' (which 允许玩家’ creativity and intelligence to shine). If you don’t know how to 跑酷 well, or you have never tried it before, there’s always a 普通 contest for you to take part in. On the 其他 side, if you feel creativity is not your strongest attribute, or you simply like landing on top of buildings, 跑酷 contests are the best option for you. There’s a contest for every personality, you just need to find out which one fits you the best!

Examples

Now that we know the basics, let’s list a few examples of recurring contests that have been popular successes in the recent past:

  • Masters of 跑酷- This contest is for all 跑酷 enthusiasts, or for those who want a 几率 to receive a very 珍奇 and 特殊迷彩. In this contest, 团队 of four 玩家 create a one-minute montage, showcasing their 跑酷能力 and creativity. The best of the best are rewarded a 特殊迷彩, which will remain in their 车库永久. They can also take part in live performance to win the contest's main prizes.
  • The GAME- 这个比赛通常作为每年生日庆祝活动的一部分，或者是每年年初的新年庆祝活动而举行。在这个比赛中，组织者会设置一系列长长的难题，考验每个参与者的智力。如果你想挑战自己的智力，或者你喜欢解谜，那么这个比赛非常适合你！
  • 先生/小姐 TO- This is a popular contest that involves real-life film-making. The goal of this contest is to film a video in-person, following a specific 3D坦克主题, and incorporate parts of your life to make it interesting. If you are a creative individual, or you enjoy making in-real-life videos, you will find this contest admirable.

Information About Contests

There are three main areas you should regularly check for information about contests:

  • The 3D坦克 Homepage- For some of the larger contests (Masters of 跑酷, 地雷 Art, etc.), the administration will make a post on the homepage with the contest details.
  • The 论坛- This is the area where you can find all the in-depth information about contests you need, and as well as the instructions and link (if needed) to submit your entry. If you have any questions about a particular contest, you can also ask them in the contest topic that suits your question (for example, if you have a question about Masters of 跑酷, you should ask it in the overarching Masters of 跑酷 topic), and one of the Contest Organizers will guide you.
  • In-game “新闻” Tab- With every new contest comes a new alert in the “新闻” tab that is found in-game. This will 帮助 notify you as soon as a contest comes out, so it is recommended to check this regularly.
