地图主题

When creating a battle, maps can be filtered by these: Most maps are presented in various themes that influence the textures and 天空盒，因此战斗的氛围。当creating a battle, maps can be filtered by these themes:

主题 Icon Decription
夏天 Default theme
夏天的傍晚 Dusk, reddish light and long shadows
夏天的夜晚 Nighttime atmosphere with dimmed lighting
秋天的日子 Autumn colored flora
冬天 地图上的雪和冰（对驾驶动态没有影响）
冬天的夜晚 地图上的雪和冰，以及夜晚的氛围
宇宙 减少了铁塔和宇宙背景


Not every map has all of the themes. In 匹配, following themes are available:

  • «夏天»
  • «夏天的傍晚»
  • «夏天的夜晚»
  • «冬天»
  • 《秋天的日子》
