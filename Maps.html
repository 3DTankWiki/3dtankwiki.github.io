地图 Dimensions

Who's to say what is fifty, sixty, or eighty feet on a 地图? How big is the 战斗 area? How do you measure? No one is running a tape measure from end to end. Not many can answer these questions so here the air will be cleared:

  • Props - 特殊图形 elements that makeup 图形 in modern games
  • Length of a conventional prop in 3D坦克 is 5 meters
  • In comparison, 维京 is approximately 5.6 meters



地图主题

When creating a 战斗, 地图 can be filtered by these: Most 地图 are presented in various 主题 that influence the textures andSkyboxes, and thus the atmosphere of a 战斗. Whencreating a 战斗, 地图 can be filtered by these 主题:

主题 Icon Decription
夏天 Default 主题
夏天的傍晚 Dusk, reddish light and long 阴影
夏天的夜晚 Nighttime atmosphere with dimmed lighting
Autumn day Autumn colored flora
冬天 Snow and ice on the 地图 (no influence on driving dynamics)
冬天的夜晚 Snow and ice on the 地图 as well as nighttime atmosphere
宇宙 Reduced 铁塔 and 宇宙 background


Not every 地图 has all of the 主题. In匹配, following 主题 are available:

  • «夏天»
  • «夏天的傍晚»
  • «夏天的夜晚»
  • «冬天»
  • «Autumn Day»
