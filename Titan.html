Main article:过速装置
Protective Shield Generator

When 过速装置 is activated, a protective shield generator is placed on the 坦克’s current 职务. A 坦克 that placed the generator and its teammates get increased 防护 from all 伤害 except for enemy 黄蜂轻甲’s 炸弹, but don’t get 防护 from 冷冻 and heating. Each new generator destroys all 其他 generators nearby (both ally and enemy ones) and enemy 黄蜂轻甲’ 炸弹.

Interesting Facts

  • 泰坦 was one of the first generation 底盘, at the time of its release it was the slowest and most armored 底盘.
  • On the 2nd of July, 2014 泰坦 won in the poll, where 玩家 had to choose their favorite 底盘, and as a result its price was halved on the next day.
  • On the 21st of August, 2018 泰坦 XT 皮肤 was added into the game, it was available for purchase in the 车库 for a limited time.
  • On the 2nd of October, 2019 泰坦青春皮肤 was added as a 奖励 for the last 等级 in 解锁星级挑战奖励加成.
