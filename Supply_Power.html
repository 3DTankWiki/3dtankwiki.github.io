道具启动加速度

see also:无人机and道具

On April 30, 2021, significant changes were made to the 道具 and 无人机 mechanics in the game. Since then, there have been further adjustments. This article provides a concise and clear explanation of how your 无人机 and 道具 now function. Let's dive in!

Quick Summary of Key Change

  1. 护甲提升 and伤害提升道具 are now weaker by default compared to their previous strength.
  2. These 道具 can be strengthened by equipping a无人机. The degree of strengthening depends on the 升级等级 of the 装备无人机. A fully upgraded 无人机 restores the 道具 to their original, powerful Double 护甲 and Double 伤害 effects.
  3. After acquiring a decent 底盘 and 炮塔, your top priority should be obtaining and fully upgrading the布鲁特斯无人机. It's a cost-effective investment that significantly enhances the strength of your 护甲 and 伤害道具, providing a crucial advantage in 战斗.



速度提升

The 速度提升道具 was only minimally affected by the update and continues to function largely as it always has. You do not need a 无人机 to utilize this 道具 at its full potential.

When 速度提升 is active:

  • Your 坦克's top 速度, the 最快速度 it can reach, is increased by 40%. This 允许 you to move 1.4 times faster than without the 道具 activated.
  • Your 坦克's acceleration, the rate at which it reaches top 速度, also increases slightly. You will gain an additional meter per second each second while accelerating, until you reach the 最高 1.4x 速度.
  • The 最高 rotation 速度 of your 炮塔 is also increased by 40%. However, the rotation acceleration, or the 速度 at which it reaches its full rotation 速度, remains unchanged.


Only two 无人机 enhance the effectiveness of the 速度提升道具:魔术师and危机.

Both 无人机 provide the following benefits:

  • They increase the Top 速度提升 (which is 40% without a 无人机) based on their 升级等级.
    • 魔术师 and 危机 adds an additional 20-50%, resulting in top 速度 of 1.6 to 1.9 times the normal 速度.
  • They reduce your 坦克's acceleration by 10%. This means that even with 速度提升 active, it will take slightly longer to reach your normal 速度. However, this effect is generally perceived as a minor 星级挑战 to maneuverability, as the increased top 速度 more than compensates for the slight delay in reaching it.



伤害提升

Formerly known as "Double 伤害," this is one of the 道具 that got a new mechanic in the update. 伤害提升 provides a significant increase to your 炮塔's offensive capabilities.

When active, this 道具 boosts your 炮塔's 伤害 by 50% to 100% of its base 伤害 (伤害 without any 道具 active). The specific amount of the boost depends on your无人机's升级等级. Without a 无人机, you receive a +50% 伤害 increase (1.5x 伤害). A fully upgraded 等级 20 无人机 grants the 最高 +100% 伤害 boost (2x 伤害).

**A Note on 炮塔装备改造:** In most cases, 炮塔装备改造 that alter your 伤害, such as the 大口径弹药 for 激光炮, simply modify the base 伤害. The 伤害提升道具 then applies its percentage increase to this new base 伤害 value, functioning as expected.
 However, there's an exception: the肾上腺素装备改造. Similar to 伤害提升, the extra 伤害 provided by 肾上腺素 is calculated as a percentage of your original base 伤害. Therefore, the extra 伤害 from 肾上腺素 is added directly to the extra 伤害 provided by the 伤害提升道具; it does not multiply the 伤害 since it does not change the base 伤害.

无人机 that Enhance 伤害提升:Certain 无人机 further amplify the effects of 伤害提升. These include助推器and危机, and露营者(This 无人机 was removed from the game and got added as an 装备改造). All three 无人机 add to the 伤害 boost provided by the 道具, increasing your overall 伤害 output.

  • 助推器 and 危机:Adds an extra 10-25% 伤害 on top of the standard 50-100% boost whenever 伤害提升 is active. This results in a total 伤害 boost of +60% to +125% (1.6x to 2.25x 伤害), depending on the 无人机's 升级.



护甲提升

Formerly known as "Double 护甲," this is the 其他道具 that got a new mechanic in the update, and it's a little confusing at first. Hopefully, this will 帮助 you understand it! Let's take a look at three situations:

  • Situation 1: You have normal 护甲 and you took some 伤害. You'll get all of that 伤害, simple as that. Ouch!
  • Situation 2: What if you had 50% more 护甲 than normal? Well, since you have 1.5 times as much 护甲 as usual, the incoming 伤害 gets divided by 1.5. When you do that division, you'll see that you take two-thirds of the normal 伤害. Pretty handy!
  • Situation 3: Now let's say you put on twice as much 护甲 as normal. What will happen? You'll take half the 伤害! The incoming 伤害 got divided by 2 since you have twice as much 护甲. Sweet!

Those three situations are exactly what's going on with no 护甲提升 active (Situation 1), 护甲提升 with no 无人机 or a 等级 0 无人机 (Situation 2), and 护甲提升 with a 等级 20 无人机 (Situation 3). So, the 道具 gives 50-100% extra 护甲 depending on your 装备无人机's 升级等级, resulting in between 67% and 50% 伤害 taken.

Now for some 无人机. There are three which increase the effect of 护甲提升:守卫者,露营者, and危机. All three of them make this change:

  • Add to the extra 护甲 put on by the 道具 (as in, more than the standard 50% to 100% extra 护甲 that all 无人机 give).
    • 守卫者 adds 25-50% of your base 护甲 on top of the passive 50-100% boost, whenever 护甲提升 is active. This means the total 护甲 boost is +75% to +150% (1.75x 护甲 to 2.5x 护甲), resulting in only taking between 57.1% and 40% 伤害.
    • 露营者 adds 100-200% of your base 护甲 on top of the passive 50-100% boost, whenever 护甲提升 is active and the 坦克 is at full health. This means the total 护甲 boost is +80% to +190% (1.8x 护甲 to 2.9x 护甲), resulting in only taking between 55.5% and 34.5% 伤害.
    • 危机 adds 50-90% of your base 护甲 on top of the passive 50-100% boost, whenever 护甲提升 is active. This means the total 护甲 boost is +100% to +190% (1.5x 护甲 to 2.9x 护甲), resulting in only taking between 50% and 34.5% 伤害.
