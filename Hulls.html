Hulls are a part of your tank in "Tanki Online", alongside Turrets, Drones, Paints, and Modules. They are a major contender as to what playstyle players may choose. They differ in speed, strength, and weight.

In battle, your tank can be controlled by these default controls: ←→↑↓ or WASD. Use Shift to activate your Hull's Overdrive. These keybinds can be edited in the Settings menu.

General Information

Hulls can be obtained from Containers and Early Access offers in the Shop. The exception is the special hull «Juggernaut»: it is automatically granted by the game in certain battle modes, so it's impossible to get it into your garage.

* - some special accounts may possess Juggernaut.

Each hull has its own unique ability - Overdrive. For example, a Wasp can plant a devastating bomb that stuns players, and a Crusader can release a tactical battle icicle that shreds armor. These abilities significantly affect gameplay, so they should be used wisely in battle.

Appearances can be modified by equipping Skins. Augments are components equipped in the Garage that affect hull parameters.

Hull upgrade

Each hull has 7 levels of modification. The upgrades start from level 1 (Mk1), the weakest modification. All hulls can be upgraded to Mk7 for crystals. But you won't be able to buy everything at once: to unlock new modifications, you need to reach the required rank for the next modification to become available.

Each modification can be gradually upgraded until the next one becomes available. The maximum modification has the inscription «MAX», at Mk7-20.

You can see how the hulls looked in the old graphics (Sep. 6, 2012) and which characteristics they had before rebalancing (Dec. 17, 2012) in the article Hulls Before the Rebalance.

Light Hulls

see also: Light Hulls: How to Choose, How to Use

Wasp

Light tracked hull. It has amazing maneuverability and grip on the surface.

Overdrive plants a compact thermonuclear bomb with a timer. In addition to good damage, such a bomb leaves behind a great number of unfortunate consequences. The enemy will be forced to respond and run for cover.

The small light «Wasp» hull can look dismissible only to beginners. Small size and high speed grant true freedom on the battlefield.

  • Power
Pros
High speed and very maneuverable. Overdrive can destroy all enemies in the radius of explosion at the same time.
Cons
Poor armor and mass. It is easy to flip when using a turret with high recoil.


Hopper

Light hovering hull. It is very fast but not very stable when moving above a complex surface.

Overdrive launches the detonation jump engine placed below the hull. It stuns enemy tanks and throws «Hopper» into the air, allowing to jump over obstacles.

The «Hopper» hover tank uses the maximum available anti-gravity engine resources to let the tanker pursue their dream - flying up to the sky far away from danger. Are you daring enough to break through the enemy's defenses? «Hopper» can help you.

  • Protection
  • Top speed
  • Turning speed
  • Weight
  • Power
Pros
High speed, can strafe sideways and stabilize in horizontal position. Thanks to overdrive, can fly high and catch a gold box in a decisive moment.
Cons
Poor armor and mass. Due to the sideways strafe mechanic can be easily pushed. Easy to destroy on distance at the moment of jump.

Hornet

Light tracked hull. Highly compact. Can enter a brief drift while turning.

Overdrive allows the tanker to detect all enemy tanks behind any obstacles using a thermal visor and sends orders to allies using a multichannel system of communication. Overdrive activation also applies the «Supercharge» status effect to your tank, letting it deal critical damage to opponents.

«Hornet» is a light hull with aerodynamic curves. It moves beautifully and smoothly, carefully sliding between obstacles. Top-notch dynamic attributes are required for recon operations leading to lightning-quick attacks.

  • Power
Pros
High speed and very maneuverable. Overdrive lets the entire team to see enemies behind obstacles and weakens all enemies’ protection.
Cons
Poor armor and mass. It is easy to flip when using a turret with high recoil.

Medium Hulls

see also: Medium Hulls: How to Choose, How to Use

Viking

Medium tracked hull. It has a quite small mass for a middle hull and a low profile.

Its «Berserk Reactor» overdrive significantly increases the damage of its turret, thanks to the increased rate of fire. You can destroy multiple targets by attacking the enemy team from the flanks before they realize how dangerous their opponent is. You might end up wasting the charge of your overdrive if you attack the enemy head on.

This hull encompasses all of the latest military technologies combined in a single piece of engineering art. Enhanced armor and second-generation tracks-system make this hull an outstanding universal tank. The hull can be used for any type of battle missions: from strategic defense to a full-frontal assault.

  • Protection
  • Top speed
  • Turning speed
  • Weight
  • Power
Pros
Versatile hull with medium speed and armor. Overdrive can quickly destroy a lot of enemies in a short time.
Cons
Small hull height. Mass is equal to light hulls. Overdrive doesn’t fit the turrets that deal damage in close range.


Crusader

Medium hovering hull. It is characterized by good passability and a compact silhouette.

Its «Cryo Blaster» overdrive makes a shell from almost nothing by cooling moisture and then shoots a big armor-piercing icicle. After being hit by this chunk of ice, the opponent's tank freezes, making it easy to finish off the tank, if not destroy it.

The «Crusader» hull belongs to the second generation of hovertanks. To create a compact but powerful hull with attacking possibilities, a lot of ingenious solutions were used. Compactness of the second generation anti-grav allowed engineers to equip enough armor to the tank for it to be a medium hull without decreasing its speed.

  • Protection
  • Top speed
  • Turning speed
  • Weight
  • Power
Pros
Can strafe sideways and stabilize in horizontal position. Overdrive lets quickly attack multiple enemies on distance.
Cons
It is possible to not notice approaching enemies and self-destruct with the overdrive if they get too close. Due to the sideways strafe mechanic can be easily pushed.

Hunter

Medium tracked hull. It has the most maneuverability among all medium hulls.

Its overdrive generates an electromagnetic pulse, damaging and, more importantly, immobilizing enemy tanks for a short time. Without special protection, the enemy is completely defenseless to finish off.

This hull, covered in strengthened armored steel, is the most universal. It will perfectly suit the fans of active, dynamic battles and also tankers who like to play positionally. You will never be out of work, be it protecting a point or an intense battle for a city block in ruins.

  • Protection
  • Top speed
  • Turning speed
  • Weight
  • Power
Pros
Versatile hull with medium speed and armor. Useful overdrive that deals damage and helps the team.
Cons
Can go into skid when moving fast.

Paladin

Medium hovering hull. It has the most maneuverability among all hovering hulls.

Its overdrive effect is applied in a range around the hull, polarizing ally tanks’ armor. Nearby allies get immunity from some status effects and get continuously healed, while negative status effects get applied to enemy tanks.

The «Paladin» hovertank was created as a support tank to assist assault groups, for which maintaining the speed of the group is essential. Unlike heavy tanks, Paladin has medium armor and uses second-generation anti-grav engines, allowing it to keep up even with light tanks.

  • Protection
  • Top speed
  • Turning speed
  • Weight
  • Power
Pros
Can strafe sideways and stabilize in horizontal position. Overdrive protects from negative status-effects and heals the team.
Cons
Overdrive charges very slowly. Due to the sideways strafe mechanic can be easily pushed.

Dictator

Medium tracked hull. It is very heavy and strong for a medium tank and is also known for its increased dimensions.

Its overdrive activates the effect of all supplies for itself and all nearby allies, applies the «Supercharge» status effect, and freezes enemies around.

Thanks to an improved system of active protection and the latest composite material used in its construction, this hull successfully absorbs the energy of explosions from rounds of any caliber, while still remaining a medium hull. The owner of such a hull can easily lead the team into a full assault, taking the brunt of the attack.

  • Protection
  • Top speed
  • Turning speed
  • Weight
  • Power
Pros
Big mass and great stability combined with high speed. Big hull height allows getting on firing positions in certain situations. Overdrive gives extra supplies to the whole team in the effect radius.
Cons
Big height makes it an easy target and at close range it’s sometimes easy to miss a shot on an enemy. In most cases it isn't quite usable when attacking an enemy base.

Heavy Hulls

see also: Heavy Hulls: How to Choose, How to Use

Titan

Heavy tracked hull. Its compact dimensions and improved acceleration dynamics distinguish it from other heavy tanks.

The «Titan» hull places a protective dome generator on overdrive activation. The dome significantly reduces incoming damage for all allied tanks and gradually repairs them. An opponent can deactivate the dome by attacking the generator using special attacks like other overdrives.

Heavy hull for the hardest defense duties. Armor from the strongest titanium alloy combined with a perfect structure and geometry make «Titan» a perfect shield from even big-caliber turrets. Tankers with this hull can drive straight into hell.

  • Protection
  • Top speed
  • Turning speed
  • Weight
  • Power
Pros
Great armor and high mass allow easily pushing enemies and protecting ally tanks. Overdrive grants protection for the entire team in the effect radius.
Cons
Very low speed, which makes it impossible to dodge shots. Protection dome can be turned off with other overdrives.

Ares

Heavy hovering hull. It remains stable even while driving under fire.

The most powerful «BFG Cannon» is built directly into the «Ares» hull. This overdrive shoots a special ball of plasma that attacks enemy tanks with lightning and heals allies.

The hovertank «Ares» became the first hull with an anti-gravity feature. Its impressive weight and proportions of the first-generation anti-gravs led to the entry of «Ares» into the heavy hulls category. To flip this hovertank in a battle, you need to try really hard.

  • Protection
  • Top speed
  • Turning speed
  • Weight
  • Power
Pros
Great armor and high mass. Can strafe sideways and stabilize in horizontal position. Overdrive can destroy multiple enemies and heal allies in a straight line.
Cons
Tank with big gabarites that takes up a lot of space, is slow and noticeable. Overdrive causes self-destruction if it explodes too close to the tank.

Mammoth

Heavy tracked hull. It has the most armor, mass, and stability.

Overdrive of the «Mammoth» hull boosts its speed, instantly repairs it, and gives it a deadly touch that crushes enemy tanks and destroys mines along the way.

A moving fortress on tracks. Flagship among the heavy hulls. Solid piece of heaviness, reinforced by concrete armor, with slow speed, but capable of surviving under the fire of several enemy turrets at once. It is unstoppable — even if you block the road with tanks, they will just burn under this monster's tracks.

  • Protection
  • Top speed
  • Turning speed
  • Weight
  • Power
Pros
Great health and high mass allow easily blocking enemies while staying stable. Overdrive turns the tank into an absolute machine of destruction for a short time.
Cons
Very low speed and maneuverability combined with the big size of the hull, which make it hard to hide from shots.

Special Hulls

Juggernaut

He'll live!

Super-heavy hull. It has special insulation to work in dangerous areas.

Juggernaut's Overdrive deploys a field of energy that launches all nearby enemies away and restores the hull’s health. Due to the massive amounts of material used, other ways of repairing the tank are extremely slow.

Heavy-duty experimental machine. Admission is strictly limited.

  • Protection
  • Top speed
  • Turning speed
  • Weight
  • Power
Pros
Very good health, mass and speed. Can handle continuous enemy fire for a long time.
Cons
Very big tank, because of that it may not go through some passages. It’s easy to lose control over the tank on high speed.
