控制点 (控制点)
团队 fight for possession of several 控制点 on the 地图. Holding points periodically gives 分数 to your 团队 and removes 分数 from the enemy. The more points your 团队 holds, the faster the 分数 changes. Destroying 红队帮助, but the main objective is capturing and holding points. 战斗 are limited either by the 分数 limit or by time.

The 模式 is available in匹配from 二等兵.

Siege (围城大战)
There are several inactive capture 动感区域 on the 地图. One 动感区域 activates at a time, and both 团队 fight for it. A 动感区域 is captured only if no enemy is standing on it. Each successfully captured point gives 1 分数 to the 团队. 战斗 are limited either by the 分数 limit or by time.

The 模式 is available in匹配from 下士.

团队剑圣 (团队剑圣)
Each 团队 has one 坦克手 controlling a powerful剑圣坦克. When a 剑圣 is destroyed, the role 通行证 to the next teammate who respawns. 团队 receive 1 point for each destruction of the enemy 剑圣. The objective is to destroy the enemy 剑圣 more times than the opponent. 战斗 are limited either by 分数 or by time.

The 模式 is available in匹配from 下士.

团队对战 (团队对战)
A classic 团队-versus-团队 destruction 模式. The objective is to destroy more enemy 坦克 than the opposing 团队. 战斗 are limited either by the number of frags or by time. Each 坦克手’s performance directly affects the final 分数 and the outcome of the 战斗.

The 模式 is available in匹配from 新兵.

橄榄球 (橄榄球)
Two 团队 bases, several drop 动感区域, and a 球 that drops by parachute. 团队 must pick up the 球 and deliver it to the enemy base. The 球 can be passed usingRin the direction of your 炮塔, allowing creative 通行证 over obstacles and 红队. Each successful delivery gives 1 point. 战斗 are limited either by number of goals or by time.

The 模式 is available in匹配from 中士.

互夺军旗 (互夺军旗)
Classic 团队模式 where each 团队 must steal the enemy 旗子 and bring it back to their own base. A capture only counts if your 旗子 is safely on its flagpole when you deliver the enemy 旗子. 战斗 are limited either by the number of captured 旗子 or by time. 玩家 gain 分数 for destroying 红队, 治疗蓝队, and interacting with 旗子.

The 模式 is available in匹配from 上士.

突袭 (突袭)
An asymmetrical 模式 where each 团队 has a different objective. The red 团队 attacks and must deliver 旗子 to the enemy point; only red 玩家 can pick up 旗子. The blue 团队 defends and must prevent deliveries. The red 团队 receives 20 points for each delivered 旗子, while the blue 团队 receives 1 point for each destroyed attacker. 战斗 are limited either by 分数 or by time.

The 模式 is available in匹配from 三级军士长.

军备竞赛 (军备竞赛)
All 玩家 start with维京and轰天炮, regardless of their 装备 items in the 车库. Every 3 kills, their 炮塔 automatically changes to the next one in the historical release order, ending with the uniqueB0-NK炮塔. When the first 玩家 reaches a new 炮塔, a message appears for everyone. The 战斗 ends when a 玩家 makes 7 kills with B0-NK. There is also a 团队-based variant called"团队军备竞赛".

Note:Botscan also appear as 玩家 in 军备竞赛战斗.
