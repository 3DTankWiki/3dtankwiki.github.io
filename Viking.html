  • 防护 (hp)— 坦克's health. 伤害 dealt by a 炮塔 is subtracted from a 坦克's 防护. Once the 防护 gets to zero, the 坦克 explodes.

  • Lateral acceleration (m/sec²)— responsible for damping of lateral 速度. The less this parameter is, the easier the 坦克 can drift. The higher it is, the more difficult it is for a 坦克 to move sideways.
  • 转向灵活度 (deg/sec)— the 最快速度 at which the 坦克 can turn.

  • 启动加速度 (m/sec²)— affects the time it takes a 底盘 to get to its 最快速度 and ability to push 其他坦克.
  • Acceleration (m/sec²)— the time it takes for the 底盘 to build to top 速度.
  • Reverse acceleration (m/sec²)— anti-inertial acceleration that comes into force when a 坦克 is moving in one direction and a 玩家 presses the button of moving to another direction. It works until 速度 gets to zero. After that, the usual acceleration starts working.

  • 过速装置 charge per 战斗 point (%)— the percentage by which the 过速装置 charges per 战斗 point received.
  • 过速装置 charge per second (%)— the percentage by which the 过速装置 charges per second played.
