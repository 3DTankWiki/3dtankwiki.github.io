一般信息

  • 装备改造 are unlocked at 二级军士长and can be obtained by completing精英通行证, opening普通幸运礼盒and史诗幸运礼盒; some can also be bought for水晶and红宝石in the车库.
  • Only one 装备改造 can be 装备 on a 炮塔 and 底盘 each at any given time.
  • You can 装备 an 装备改造 or 取消装备 an 装备改造 at any time.
  • There isn't any visual indicator of what 装备改造 a 玩家 is using on their 坦克; you can see 装备改造 of 其他玩家装备 in 战斗 by pressingTab.
  • Standard 设置 (no 装备装备改造) in a 炮塔 or 底盘 is shown within the 车库.


Methods of obtainment

Most standard 装备改造 are available for purchase for245 000 水晶or4 990 红宝石. Also, such 装备改造 can be received as a prize from 普通幸运礼盒. 装备改造 are also eligible for discounts during活动.

传奇装备改造 for a 炮塔/底盘 can now drop from 幸运礼盒only'after gettingat least oneof the珍奇装备改造 for the corresponding 炮塔/底盘.

装备改造 for the temperature immunity for any 底盘 can now drop from 幸运礼盒onlyafter getting the temperature 抗性装备改造 for the same 底盘. If those 装备改造 are also in the异国情调category of 史诗幸运礼盒, then the restriction does not apply.

Especially 珍奇装备改造 can only be received from史诗幸运礼盒or as a prize in one of the 活动.

There are separate rules for trophy 装备改造: it is required to earn5,000,000reputation pointsusing 装备, and the “凤凰” type 装备改造 is made.
