沙皇


see also:手榴弹

沙皇 is a specialized 手榴弹 that has a big area of explosion. It deals chaos 伤害to all enemy 坦克 in a certain 半径 and destroys enemy地雷.

When 沙皇 is thrown with theFkey, a timer starts that makes the 手榴弹 explode on impact with any surface. Destroying 红队 with a 手榴弹 counts as 普通 kills with a 炮塔, and therefore awardsreputationand经验值points.

Description in the 车库

Explodes with a large blast 半径.

The 手榴弹 throw triggers a timer. After the timer runs out, the 手榴弹 explodes. The 射程 of explosion is much higher compared to 其他手榴弹. Before the explosion, a 特殊 melody is played.

Before throwing, it is recommended to count to three. Counting to five is not recommended.

Obtaining 沙皇

沙皇手榴弹 can be usually obtained in limited amounts as a 奖励 in 特殊活动, mini-games, 精英通行证, etc. Similar to the核能道具, it cannot be directly purchased.
