Purpose

The purpose of the test 服务器is for checking the new releases and searching for bugs in conditions that are comfortable for testers.水晶, purchased items, and any 其他 in-game items received on the test 服务器 willneverbe added to your账号在主服务器上。

邀请函

If 质量 testing isn’t happening on the main 服务器, entrance to it is limited to invitations. Only one person can use it - the one who received it.

Invitations are only given totesters and tester candidates.

Invitations are仅由开发者提供.

Administration never gives invitations to the 玩家 who are not a part of the testers and tester candidates 小组. If more 玩家 are needed on the test 服务器, an open test starts (质量 test).

If you “registered” somewhere and received an “invitation” - you will not get access to the official test 服务器. Most probably your 账号 is stolen and you will see that it is banned or blocked for cheating. In this case you have probably registered on a scam (fishing) site, and giving your 密码 away has resulted in your 账号 being hacked. Remember that to get an invitation neither your email nor your 密码 are required. In case your 账号 was stolen, contact thetech support.

质量 Testing

有时候要检查像新版本这样的东西，需要大量玩家同时参与。在这种情况下，测试服务器对所有人开放。测试服务器的开放从不提前通知。

测试服务器的命令

Sometimes 特殊 commands are仅仅available on the test 服务器 that can change parameters of the test 账号. Usually they only work for developers.

  • “/addcry n”- adds n水晶to the current 账号 on the test 服务器;
  • “/addexp n”- adds n经验值指向测试服务器上的当前账号;
  • “/addpremium n”- 增加 n 分钟的贵宾to the current 账号 on the test 服务器;



注意！命令在主服务器上无效。当尝试在测试服务器之外使用它们时，您可能会得到警告或禁令。

Don’t forget that test 服务器 resources are limited. If you can’t get on the test 服务器, just try again later. During the testing period 服务器 can be reloaded or stopped in case some issues need to be fixed or an update is required.

The real test 服务器 has three states:

  • Off. No one has access;
  • On fortesters and tester candidates需要邀请才能进入；
  • On for everyone. To enter, it is required to create a new 账号 and "activate" the login and 密码 of the 账号 on the main 服务器.

如何成为测试员并获得邀请

To apply to be a tester, fill out the application form on theTester Candidateswebsite. When you fill in the form and send it, you will be added to a 特殊Discord服务器将在这里处理您的请求。您将能够在这个 Discord 服务器上直接与测试管理员交谈。

Frequently Asked Questions

问： 什么是邀请代码？
A:测试服务器邀请码是一个由字母和数字组成的字符串，测试人员和开发人员在进行封闭测试时用它来访问测试服务器。这个代码就像是进入测试服务器的密码。


问： 测试服务器何时会向公众开放？（不需要邀请码）
A: 测试服务器仅在需要大规模玩家基础来测试新功能或修复漏洞时开放。这些需要大量玩家的时间没有固定的时间表，因此唯一知道测试服务器是否开放的方法就是自己检查。

问： Why is the 商店 on the test 服务器 locked?
A: The developers lock the 商店 on purpose since there is nothing needed to be tested there, and do not need their test 统计数据 to be affected by purchasing actions done in the 商店. It also prevents 玩家 from purchasing items with real money since these 账号 are deleted after testing.


Attention! Do not purchase anything in the test 服务器商店 with real money. All items obtained in the test 服务器 are removed after testing ends, and are not added to your real 账号.


Q: Where can I obtain an invite code?
A： Invite codes are only given to候选测试员、测试员和开发者获得一个的唯一方式是become a Tester.


注意！只有候选测试员、测试员和开发者可以获得邀请代码。其他人无法获得邀请代码。提供出售邀请代码的玩家正在试图骗你！
