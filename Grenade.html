一般信息

The 手榴弹 has a 特殊 mechanic with its own physical properties and interactions with the environment. It bounces off all immovable objects on the 地图: walls, house roofs, relief stones.

At the same time, 手榴弹 ignore all moving objects, like 坦克 and 其他手榴弹. When throwing a 手榴弹, you can be sure that it will reach the target even if there is an ally 坦克 on its trajectory.

手榴弹 can be thrown in 2 ways:

  • Short press of theF钥匙. Throw happens after the release of the button.
  • Holding theF钥匙. Throw happens after the release of the button. This 允许 you to see the trajectory of the 手榴弹's flight and aim more precisely.

手榴弹 are thrown at a specific angle. The 速度 and distance of the 手榴弹 depend on the 坦克’s 速度 and 职务 at the moment of throwing. When your 坦克 is not moving, the 手榴弹 will just land right in front of your 坦克. In order to reach the enemy from far away, it is required to accelerate your 坦克 and then throw a 手榴弹 at 最快速度. And to throw it higher, you should try to find a proper angle of the 坦克.

手榴弹 can be thrown only once per respawn (unless you have the 榴弹兵装备改造), and have a 冷却 of 25-50 seconds. After the 冷却 time 通行证, the 手榴弹 reloads on the next respawn. Also, it can’t be modified by using 道具, for example«伤害提升». The amount of 伤害 dealt can’t be changed.

特殊手榴弹 Mechanics

The 手榴弹 is a physical object that can be shot.

激光炮and 手榴弹 can be a powerful combination. If you 射击 your own 手榴弹 with a 激光炮, it will detonate immediately. This can create situations in which the usage of this mechanic will catch even an experienced 坦克手 off guard.

One more important 手榴弹 use is as a distraction of the enemy 团队's automatic 瞄准 systems. For example, when you throw a 手榴弹, an enemy雷暴炮can mistakenly aim at the flying 手榴弹 and miss its original target 坦克. For teammates' 瞄准 systems, 手榴弹 are invisible, and therefore are not a liability.

Interesting facts

  • 手榴弹 was added into the game on the 8th of September, 2023.
  • Firstly, 手榴弹 was added as a test of its mechanic in the 特殊 «榴弹兵» 战斗模式 during the celebration of the«坦克手’s Day 2023». Over the next year, 手榴弹 with variously adjusted 设置 were further tested in many 活动 gamemodes.
  • On 6th September 2024, at the start of the 坦克手's Day celebrations, the first 手榴弹 type "炸弹" was publicly released in 玩家' 车库, and could be used in any 匹配 or 专家战场. At the start, only 玩家 who obtained the 道具 from Memo 2024 活动 could use them, but from 9th September anyone can purchase them in the 车库.
