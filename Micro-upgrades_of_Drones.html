Micro-升级 of 无人机

see also:Micro-升级and无人机


All无人机except for神谕者can bemicro-upgradeda total of 20 times, from 等级 0 to 等级 20. As you 升级 them, their effects increase, and they make your道具stronger. 无人机 do not have modifications like炮塔and底盘do, so each 升级 simply requires 水晶 to complete.

The total cost of all 20 等级 for a standard 无人机 is 1 million 水晶.
